V4 — Medical Referral

There are four people in a medical referral chain.

Doctor 1 moves first and chooses between DoNotRefer (Out1) and ReferCase (In1).

If Doctor 1 chooses DoNotRefer (Out1), the interaction ends with reward 0 to all four.

If Doctor 1 chooses ReferCase (In1), Coordinator 2 moves and chooses between
StopReferral (Out2) and AdvanceReferral (In2).

If Coordinator 2 chooses StopReferral (Out2), the interaction ends with reward -1 to Doctor 1
and reward 0 to the others.

If Coordinator 2 chooses AdvanceReferral (In2), Specialist 3 moves and chooses between
Prioritize (L3) and Delay (R3).

Approver 4 then moves without knowing whether Specialist 3 chose Prioritize (L3)
or Delay (R3).

Approver 4 chooses between Authorize (L4) and Refuse (R4).

If the path is ReferCase (In1), AdvanceReferral (In2), Prioritize (L3), Authorize (L4),
each player receives reward 1.

If the path is ReferCase (In1), AdvanceReferral (In2), Prioritize (L3), Refuse (R4),
Doctor 1 gets 1, Coordinator 2 gets -5, and Players 3 and 4 get 0.

If the path is ReferCase (In1), AdvanceReferral (In2), Delay (R3), Authorize (L4),
Doctor 1 gets 1, Coordinator 2 gets -5, and Players 3 and 4 get 0.

If the path is ReferCase (In1), AdvanceReferral (In2), Delay (R3), Refuse (R4),
Doctor 1 gets 1, Coordinator 2 gets -5, while Players 3 and 4 each get 1.
