Strategic Equivalence Notion: Better-response equivalence

Constraints:
Check that the worker is high-ability with probability 1/3 and low-ability with probability 2/3.
Check that the firm’s two decision nodes after no education are in the same information set.
Check that the firm’s two decision nodes after education are in the same information set.
Check that if the worker is high-ability and gets no education, then assigning the managerial job gives a final reward of 10 for the worker and 10 for the firm.
Check that if the worker is low-ability and gets an education, then assigning the clerical job gives a final reward of -3 for the worker and 4 for the firm.
