V5 (professional cycling team planning)

A team manager first chooses between season plan A and season plan B.

If the manager chooses season plan A, the race director then chooses between schedule C and schedule D.
If the race director chooses schedule C, the manager then chooses between training block G and training block H.
Training block G gives a final reward of 3 to the manager and 4 to the race director.
Training block H gives a final reward of 4 to the manager and 3 to the race director.

If the race director chooses schedule D, the manager then chooses between training block I and training block J.
Training block I gives a final reward of 1 to the manager and 2 to the race director.
Training block J gives a final reward of 5 to the manager and 7 to the race director.

If the manager chooses season plan B, the race director then chooses between schedule E and schedule F.
If the race director chooses schedule E, the manager then chooses between training block K and training block L.
Training block K gives a final reward of 6 to the manager and 5 to the race director.
Training block L gives a final reward of 2 to the manager and 8 to the race director.

If the race director chooses schedule F, the manager then chooses between training block M and training block N.
Training block M gives a final reward of 9 to the manager and 1 to the race director.
Training block N gives a final reward of 3 to the manager and 6 to the race director.
