V2 [Desert convoy]

A convoy chief and a scout are planning how to cross a desert route.

The convoy chief moves first and chooses between TurnBackNow (T) and PushForward (B).

If the convoy chief chooses TurnBackNow (T), the trip ends there.
This gives the convoy chief 2, and the scout 6.

If the convoy chief chooses PushForward (B), the scout chooses between NorthernTrack (U) and SouthernTrack (D).

Later, the scout chooses between LeftRidge (L) and RightRidge (R),
but at that point the scout cannot tell whether the earlier track choice was NorthernTrack (U) or SouthernTrack (D).

After NorthernTrack (U), LeftRidge (L) gives the convoy chief 4, and the scout 4,
while RightRidge (R) gives the convoy chief 0, and the scout 0.

After SouthernTrack (D), LeftRidge (L) gives the convoy chief 0, and the scout 0,
while RightRidge (R) gives the convoy chief 1, and the scout 1.
