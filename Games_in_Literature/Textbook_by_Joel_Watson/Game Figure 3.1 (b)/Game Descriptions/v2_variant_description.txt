V2 (restaurant expansion)

A restaurant group first decides whether to open in a new neighborhood or stay out.
If it stays out, the game ends with 2 for the first group and 2 for the local rival.
If it opens, the rival then decides whether to stay out of that contest or expand too.
If the rival stays out, the outcome is 1 for the first group and 3 for the rival.
If the rival also expands, the first group then chooses between two business approaches.
The more aggressive follow-up gives 4 to the first group and 2 to the rival.
The softer follow-up gives 3 to the first group and 4 to the rival.
