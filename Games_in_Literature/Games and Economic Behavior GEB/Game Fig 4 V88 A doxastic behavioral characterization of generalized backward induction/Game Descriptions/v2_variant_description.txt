V2 — Medical Review

A lead doctor begins by choosing between Close Case (a), Standard Referral (b), and Special Referral (c).
If the doctor chooses Close Case (a), the case ends immediately with reward 2 to the doctor,
reward 0 to the department head, and reward 0 to the final reviewer.

If the doctor chooses Standard Referral (b) or Special Referral (c), the department head moves next.
The department head cannot tell which referral route was chosen.
The department head then chooses between Track D (d) and Track E (e).

After that, the final reviewer moves.
The final reviewer cannot tell which earlier route led there.
The final reviewer then chooses between Clear Quickly (s), Revise Plan (t), and Delay (u).

If the path is Standard Referral (b), Track D (d), Clear Quickly (s),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the doctor, reward 1 to the department head, and reward 1 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Standard Referral (b), Track D (d), Revise Plan (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor, reward 1 to the department head, and reward 2 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Standard Referral (b), Track D (d), Delay (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor, reward 2 to the department head, and reward 0 to the final reviewer.

If the path is Standard Referral (b), Track E (e), Clear Quickly (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor, reward 0 to the department head, and reward 2 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Standard Referral (b), Track E (e), Revise Plan (t),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor, reward 0 to the department head, and reward 1 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Standard Referral (b), Track E (e), Delay (u),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the doctor, reward 2 to the department head, and reward 0 to the final reviewer.

If the path is Special Referral (c), Track D (d), Clear Quickly (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor, reward 1 to the department head, and reward 1 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Special Referral (c), Track D (d), Revise Plan (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor, reward 1 to the department head, and reward 2 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Special Referral (c), Track D (d), Delay (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor, reward 2 to the department head, and reward 0 to the final reviewer.

If the path is Special Referral (c), Track E (e), Clear Quickly (s),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor, reward 0 to the department head, and reward 2 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Special Referral (c), Track E (e), Revise Plan (t),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the doctor, reward 0 to the department head, and reward 1 to the final reviewer.
If the path is Special Referral (c), Track E (e), Delay (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor, reward 2 to the department head, and reward 0 to the final reviewer.
