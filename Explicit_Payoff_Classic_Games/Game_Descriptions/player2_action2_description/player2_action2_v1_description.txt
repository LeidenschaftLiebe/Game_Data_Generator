Game Description 1: Courtroom Trial

In the high-pressure world of courtroom trial, split-second choices can tip the balance.

**Player1** considers their approach:
- Deploy a tactical **present legal documents**, or
- Engage with a heartfelt **make emotional appeal**.

Across the table, **Player2** strategizes their defense:
- Spring a **call surprise witness**, or
- Hold steady with a **challenge prosecution**.

The resulting dynamics unfold as follows:

- If Player1 commits to **present legal documents**:
  - And Player2 responds with **call surprise witness**, the payoff is (6, -6).
  - And Player2 responds with **challenge prosecution**, the payoff is (-6, 6).

- If Player1 commits to **make emotional appeal**:
  - And Player2 responds with **call surprise witness**, the payoff is (1, -1).
  - And Player2 responds with **challenge prosecution**, the payoff is (4, -4).

Victory belongs to the one who reads the situation — and their opponent — best.
