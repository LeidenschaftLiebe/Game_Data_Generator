V3 — Stadium Booking

An event promoter is deciding how to handle a concert booking.

The promoter moves first and chooses between Drop Event (O),
Small Venue (L), and Arena Booking (R).

If the promoter chooses Drop Event (O), the game ends immediately.
The promoter gets reward 2 and the venue partner gets reward 6.

If the promoter chooses Small Venue (L) or Arena Booking (R),
the venue partner moves next.

The venue partner cannot tell whether the promoter chose
Small Venue (L) or Arena Booking (R).

The venue partner then chooses between Minimal Promotion (A)
and Major Promotion (B).

If the path is Small Venue (L), Minimal Promotion (A),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the promoter and reward 1 to the venue partner.

If the path is Small Venue (L), Major Promotion (B),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the promoter and reward 2 to the venue partner.

If the path is Arena Booking (R), Minimal Promotion (A),
the outcome gives reward -1 to the promoter and reward 3 to the venue partner.

If the path is Arena Booking (R), Major Promotion (B),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the promoter and reward 2 to the venue partner.
