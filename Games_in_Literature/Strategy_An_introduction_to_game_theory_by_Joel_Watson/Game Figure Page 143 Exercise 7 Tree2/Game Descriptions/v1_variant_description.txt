V1 (market entry)

A firm first chooses whether to go in or stay out of a market.
If it stays out, the interaction ends immediately and each side gets a final reward of 1.

If the firm goes in, the rival firm then chooses whether to go in as well or stay out.
If the rival stays out, the final reward is -1 for the first firm and 0 for the rival.

If the rival also goes in, the first firm then chooses between a tougher move and a more moderate move.
The tougher move gives a final reward of 4 to the first firm and -1 to the rival.
The moderate move gives a final reward of 3 to the first firm and 2 to the rival.
