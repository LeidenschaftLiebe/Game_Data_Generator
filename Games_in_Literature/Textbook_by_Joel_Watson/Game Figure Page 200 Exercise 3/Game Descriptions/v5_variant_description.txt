V5 (wildlife documentary production)

A producer first chooses between production plan A and production plan B.
If the producer chooses production plan B, the project ends immediately with a final reward of 5 for the producer and 4 for the distribution chief.

If the producer chooses production plan A, the distribution chief then chooses between route C and route D.
If the distribution chief chooses route D, the producer then chooses between edit I and edit J.
Edit I gives a final reward of 9 to the producer and 2 to the distribution chief.
Edit J gives a final reward of 4 to the producer and 5 to the distribution chief.

If the distribution chief chooses route C, the producer then chooses between cut G and cut H.
Cut H gives a final reward of 7 to the producer and 3 to the distribution chief.
Cut G leads to one more choice by the distribution chief between release option E and release option F.

If the distribution chief chooses release option E, the producer then chooses between format K and format L.
Format K gives a final reward of 2 to the producer and 4 to the distribution chief.
Format L gives a final reward of 3 to the producer and 6 to the distribution chief.

If the distribution chief chooses release option F, the producer then chooses between format M and format N.
Format M gives a final reward of 6 to the producer and 7 to the distribution chief.
Format N gives a final reward of 8 to the producer and 1 to the distribution chief.
