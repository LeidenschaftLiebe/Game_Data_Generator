V4 [Artifact convoy pursuit]

A truck driver first chooses between MakeRun [T] and PullOver [S].
If the truck driver pulls over, the result is 0, 0.

If the truck driver makes the run, the patrol chief chooses between StayBack [W] and BlockNow [I].
BlockNow [I] gives -1, -1.
StayBack [W] leads to a repeated road choice.

The truck driver then repeatedly chooses between KeepRolling [C] and TakeTurnoff [E], 
without knowing whether it is the earlier chance or the later one.

If the truck driver takes the earlier turnoff, the patrol chief later chooses between RoadPatrol [H] and TurnoffPatrol [E].
RoadPatrol [H] gives 1, -1.
TurnoffPatrol [E] gives 0, 0.

If the truck driver keeps rolling once and then takes the later turnoff, the result is 0, 0.

If the truck driver keeps rolling twice, the patrol chief again chooses between RoadPatrol [H] and TurnoffPatrol [E].
RoadPatrol [H] gives 0, 0.
TurnoffPatrol [E] gives 1, -1.
