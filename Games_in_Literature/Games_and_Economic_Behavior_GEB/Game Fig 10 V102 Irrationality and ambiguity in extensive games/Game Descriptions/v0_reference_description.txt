V0 — Example 6

There are two players.

Player 1 moves first and chooses between O, L, and R.

If Player 1 chooses O, the game ends immediately with reward 2 to Player 1
and reward 6 to Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses L or R, Player 2 moves next.

At Player 2’s decision point, Player 2 cannot tell whether Player 1 chose L or R.

Player 2 then chooses between A and B.

If the path is L, A, the outcome gives reward 0 to Player 1
and reward 1 to Player 2.

If the path is L, B, the outcome gives reward 3 to Player 1
and reward 2 to Player 2.

If the path is R, A, the outcome gives reward -1 to Player 1
and reward 3 to Player 2.

If the path is R, B, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1
and reward 2 to Player 2.
