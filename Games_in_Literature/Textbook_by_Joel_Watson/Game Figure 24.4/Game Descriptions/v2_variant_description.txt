V2 (treasure hunt expedition)

It is unknown whether a hidden island contains a major treasure cache or nothing valuable.
With probability 2/3, the cache is worth 12.
With probability 1/3, the cache is worth 0.
The expedition captain learns this information before choosing a search route.
The backer does not observe the hidden value and does not observe which route the captain chose before deciding how strongly to support the expedition.

If the hidden value is 12 and the captain chooses route A while the backer chooses support plan C, the final reward is 12 for the captain and 9 for the backer.
If the hidden value is 12 and the captain chooses route A while the backer chooses support plan D, the final reward is 3 for the captain and 6 for the backer.
If the hidden value is 12 and the captain chooses route B while the backer chooses support plan C, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 0 for the backer.
If the hidden value is 12 and the captain chooses route B while the backer chooses support plan D, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 9 for the backer.

If the hidden value is 0 and the captain chooses route A while the backer chooses support plan C, the final reward is 0 for the captain and 9 for the backer.
If the hidden value is 0 and the captain chooses route A while the backer chooses support plan D, the final reward is 3 for the captain and 6 for the backer.
If the hidden value is 0 and the captain chooses route B while the backer chooses support plan C, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 0 for the backer.
If the hidden value is 0 and the captain chooses route B while the backer chooses support plan D, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 9 for the backer.
