V3 [Political coalition talks]

The political environment first turns out to be LeftShift (L), RightShift (R), or OutsideCrisis (O).

The lead negotiator can tell whether the situation is OutsideCrisis (O) or not.
If it is not OutsideCrisis (O), the negotiator chooses between MajorConcession (M) and NoConcession (N).

If the environment is LeftShift (L) and the negotiator chooses MajorConcession (M), the result is 0, 0.
If the environment is LeftShift (L) and the negotiator chooses NoConcession (N), the result is 101, 0.

If the environment is RightShift (R) and the negotiator chooses MajorConcession (M), the result is 101, 0.
If the environment is RightShift (R) and the negotiator chooses NoConcession (N), then the coalition partner moves.

If the environment is OutsideCrisis (O), the coalition partner also moves.
The coalition partner chooses between Support (S) and Stall (T) without knowing whether the hidden case 
is RightShift (R) after NoConcession (N) or directly OutsideCrisis (O).

If the hidden case is RightShift (R), both Support (S) and Stall (T) give 100, 0.
If the hidden case is OutsideCrisis (O), both Support (S) and Stall (T) give -1, 0.
