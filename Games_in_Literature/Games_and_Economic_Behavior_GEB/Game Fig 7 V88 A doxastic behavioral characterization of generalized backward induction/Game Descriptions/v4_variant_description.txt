V4 — Cybersecurity Policy

A firm is deciding how to respond to a security concern.
The firm moves first and chooses between Immediate Patch (a), Internal Review (b),
and Temporary Delay (c).

After that, the outside auditor moves.
The auditor cannot tell which of those three response paths was chosen.
The auditor then chooses between Approve (d) and Flag Issue (e).

If the path is Immediate Patch (a), Approve (d),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is Immediate Patch (a), Flag Issue (e),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the firm and reward 0 to the auditor.

If the path is Internal Review (b), Approve (d),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the firm and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Review (b), Flag Issue (e),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm and reward 0 to the auditor.

If the path is Temporary Delay (c), Approve (d),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm and reward 0 to the auditor.
If the path is Temporary Delay (c), Flag Issue (e),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm and reward 1 to the auditor.
