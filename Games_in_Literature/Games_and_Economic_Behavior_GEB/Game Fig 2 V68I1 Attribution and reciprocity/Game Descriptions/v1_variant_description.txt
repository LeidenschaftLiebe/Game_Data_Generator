V1 — Casting Call

A producer is deciding how to assign a starring role.
The producer first chooses between Give Role to Actor 2 (L), Flip a Coin (M), and Give Role to Actor 3 (R).

If the producer chooses Give Role to Actor 2 (L), Actor 2 then chooses between Stay Friendly (l) and Complain Publicly (r).
If the path is Give Role to Actor 2 (L), Stay Friendly (l), the outcome gives reward 3 to the producer, reward 0 to Actor 2, and reward 1 to Actor 3.
If the path is Give Role to Actor 2 (L), Complain Publicly (r), the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer, reward -1 to Actor 2, and reward 1 to Actor 3.

If the producer chooses Give Role to Actor 3 (R), Actor 3 then chooses between Stay Friendly (l) and Complain Publicly (r).
If the path is Give Role to Actor 3 (R), Stay Friendly (l), the outcome gives reward 3 to the producer, reward 1 to Actor 2, and reward 0 to Actor 3.
If the path is Give Role to Actor 3 (R), Complain Publicly (r), the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer, reward 1 to Actor 2, and reward -1 to Actor 3.

If the producer chooses Flip a Coin (M), chance selects Actor 2’s side or Actor 3’s side with probability 1/2 each.
On Actor 2’s side, Actor 2 chooses between Stay Friendly (l) and Complain Publicly (r).
On Actor 3’s side, Actor 3 chooses between Stay Friendly (l) and Complain Publicly (r).
The resulting rewards match the corresponding direct-assignment branch awards above.
