V2 [Wildfire equipment contract]

A county buyer first chooses between BuyNothing and PlaceOrder.
If the county buyer chooses BuyNothing, this gives 1 to the county buyer, and 1 to the supplier.

If the county buyer chooses PlaceOrder, a random disruption may happen next.
With probability 1/4, there is Disruption.
With probability 3/4, there is NoDisruption.

If there is NoDisruption, the supplier chooses between DeliverAsPromised and KeepTheMoney.
If the supplier chooses DeliverAsPromised, this gives 2 to the county buyer, and 2 to the supplier.

If the supplier chooses KeepTheMoney, a court review begins.
The court OrdersCompliance with probability 1/2, or DoesNotOrderCompliance with probability 1/2.
OrdersCompliance gives 2 to the county buyer, and 1.8 to the supplier.
DoesNotOrderCompliance gives 0 to the county buyer, and 4 to the supplier.

If there is Disruption, the court review begins immediately and leads to those same two possible results.
