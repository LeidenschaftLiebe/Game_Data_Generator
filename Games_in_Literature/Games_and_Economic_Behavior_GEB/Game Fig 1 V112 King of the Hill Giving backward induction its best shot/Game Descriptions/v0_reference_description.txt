V0 — King of the Hill line game

Four subjects stand in a line in front of a king on a hill.

Subject 1 moves first and chooses whether to Stay or Charge.

If Subject 1 chooses Stay, the interaction stops immediately.
All four people remain ordinary subjects, and it ends with reward 4 to each of the four players.

If Subject 1 chooses Charge, Subject 1 becomes the new king and Subject 2 gets the next choice.

Subject 2 then chooses whether to Stay or Charge.

If Subject 2 chooses Stay, the interaction ends there.
Subject 1 remains king and receives reward 8, while Subjects 2, 3, and 4 each receive reward 4.

If Subject 2 chooses Charge, Subject 2 becomes the new king, Subject 1 is dethroned,
and Subject 3 gets the next choice.

Subject 3 then chooses whether to Stay or Charge.

If Subject 3 chooses Stay, the interaction ends there.
Subject 1 gets reward 0, Subject 2 gets reward 8, and Subjects 3 and 4 each get reward 4.

If Subject 3 chooses Charge, Subject 3 becomes the new king, Subject 2 is dethroned,
and Subject 4 gets the final choice.

Subject 4 then chooses whether to Stay or Charge.

If Subject 4 chooses Stay, the interaction ends there.
Subjects 1 and 2 each get reward 0, Subject 3 gets reward 8, and Subject 4 gets reward 4.

If Subject 4 chooses Charge, Subject 4 becomes the final king and Subject 3 is dethroned.
The interaction ends with reward 0 to Subjects 1, 2, and 3, and reward 8 to Subject 4.
