V2 [Smuggling pursuit]

A courier first chooses between RunCargo [T] and StopNow [S].
If the courier stops now, the result is 0, 0.

If the courier runs the cargo, the border officer chooses between WaitAndTrack [W] and StrikeNow [I].
StrikeNow [I] gives -1, -1.
WaitAndTrack [W] sends the courier onto the route.

On that route, the courier repeatedly chooses between StayOnRoad [C] and SlipOff [E], 
without knowing whether it is the first chance or the second.

If the courier slips off at the earlier chance, the border officer later chooses between RoadWatch [H] and ExitTrap [E].
RoadWatch [H] gives 1, -1.
ExitTrap [E] gives 0, 0.

If the courier stays on the road once and then slips off, the result is 0, 0.

If the courier stays on the road twice, the border officer again chooses between RoadWatch [H] and ExitTrap [E].
RoadWatch [H] gives 0, 0.
ExitTrap [E] gives 1, -1.
