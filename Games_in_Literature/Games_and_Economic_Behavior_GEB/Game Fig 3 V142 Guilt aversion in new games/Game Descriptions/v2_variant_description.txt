V2 [Research grant support]

A lead researcher, a lab manager, and a junior researcher are involved in a funding decision.
The lead researcher first chooses between KeepBudget (Out) and TransferBudget (In).

If the lead researcher chooses KeepBudget (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the lead researcher, 100 for the lab manager, and 30 for the junior researcher.

If the lead researcher chooses TransferBudget (In), 25 units from the lead researcher’s own budget are placed under the lab manager’s control.
The lab manager then chooses between SmallSupport (Left) and FullSupport (Right).

If the lab manager chooses SmallSupport (Left), the lab manager keeps 20 and sends 5 to the junior researcher.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the junior researcher receives 10.
The result is 145 for the lead researcher, 120 for the lab manager, and 40 for the junior researcher.

If the lab manager chooses FullSupport (Right), the lab manager sends the full 25 to the junior researcher.
That amount is doubled, so the junior researcher receives 50.
The result is 145 for the lead researcher, 100 for the lab manager, and 80 for the junior researcher.
