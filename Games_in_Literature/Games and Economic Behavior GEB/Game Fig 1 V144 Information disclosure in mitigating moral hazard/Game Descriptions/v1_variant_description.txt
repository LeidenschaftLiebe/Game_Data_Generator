V1 [Job assignment]

A worker is considering whether to accept a job for a manager.
Chance first determines whether the job is EasyTask or DifficultTask, each with probability 0.50.

The worker does not know which kind of job it is when making the first choice.
So the worker first chooses between AcceptJob (IN) and DeclineJob (OUT).

If the worker chooses DeclineJob (OUT), the interaction ends immediately.
The worker gets 30 and the manager gets 25.

If the worker chooses AcceptJob (IN), the worker then chooses between WorkHard (HIGH) and WorkLight (LOW).
The worker still does not know whether the job is easy or difficult when making that second choice.

If the job is easy and the worker chooses WorkHard (HIGH), the result is 20 for the worker and 50 for the manager.
If the job is easy and the worker chooses WorkLight (LOW), the result is 50 for the worker and 35 for the manager.

If the job is difficult and the worker chooses WorkHard (HIGH), the result is 20 for the worker and 50 for the manager.
If the job is difficult and the worker chooses WorkLight (LOW), the result is 0 for the worker and 35 for the manager.
