V5 (space center visitor day)

At the outset, a special spacecraft tour may either be available that day or not, with probability 0.5 for each case.
Sam learns whether the tour is available.
Quinn does not observe this directly.

If the tour is available, Sam chooses whether to bring the official visitor notice to Quinn or not.
If Sam brings the notice in the tour-available case, Quinn then chooses whether to go to the space center or not.
If Quinn goes after seeing the notice in the tour-available case, the final reward is 1 for Sam and 1 for Quinn.
If Quinn refuses after seeing the notice in the tour-available case, the final reward is -2 for Sam and 0 for Quinn.

If Sam does not bring the notice in the tour-available case, Quinn still chooses whether to go to the space center or not.
If Quinn goes after Sam does not bring the notice in the tour-available case, the final reward is 3 for Sam and 1 for Quinn.
If Quinn refuses after Sam does not bring the notice in the tour-available case, the final reward is 0 for Sam and 0 for Quinn.

If the tour is not available, Sam again chooses whether to bring the notice or not.
If Sam brings the notice in the no-tour case, Quinn then chooses whether to go to the space center or not.
If Quinn goes after seeing the notice in the no-tour case, the final reward is -1 for Sam and -1 for Quinn.
If Quinn refuses after seeing the notice in the no-tour case, the final reward is -2 for Sam and 0 for Quinn.

If Sam does not bring the notice in the no-tour case, Quinn still chooses whether to go to the space center or not.
Quinn cannot tell from the missing notice whether the tour is available or not.
If Quinn goes after Sam does not bring the notice in the no-tour case, the final reward is 1 for Sam and -1 for Quinn.
If Quinn refuses after Sam does not bring the notice in the no-tour case, the final reward is 0 for Sam and 0 for Quinn.
