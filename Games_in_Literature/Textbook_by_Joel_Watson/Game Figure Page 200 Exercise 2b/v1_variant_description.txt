V1 (deep-sea expedition)

An expedition leader first chooses between the upper-ocean route and the deep-trench route.

If the leader chooses the upper-ocean route, the navigation chief then chooses between survey plan A and survey plan B.
If the navigation chief chooses survey plan A, the final reward is 2 for the expedition leader and 3 for the navigation chief.
If the navigation chief chooses survey plan B, the final reward is 5 for the expedition leader and 4 for the navigation chief.

If the leader chooses the deep-trench route, the navigation chief then chooses between support plan C and support plan D.
If the navigation chief chooses support plan D, the final reward is 0 for the expedition leader and 2 for the navigation chief.
If the navigation chief chooses support plan C, the expedition leader then chooses between equipment package E and equipment package F.
Equipment package E gives a final reward of 6 to the expedition leader and 2 to the navigation chief.
Equipment package F gives a final reward of 2 to the expedition leader and 6 to the navigation chief.
