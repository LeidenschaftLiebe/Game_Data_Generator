V4 [Supplier negotiation]

A retailer and a supplier are deciding whether to deepen a business relationship.

The supplier chooses between TakeAdvantage (Exploit), DropOut (Exit), and HonorDeal (Collaborate).

At the same time, the retailer chooses between CancelOrder (Exit), UseStrictTerms (Defensive), and OfferFairTerms (Generous).
The retailer chooses without seeing the supplier’s action.

If the supplier chooses TakeAdvantage (Exploit) and the retailer chooses CancelOrder (Exit), both get 0, 0.
If the supplier chooses TakeAdvantage (Exploit) and the retailer chooses UseStrictTerms (Defensive), this gives the supplier 0, and the retailer 10.
If the supplier chooses TakeAdvantage (Exploit) and the retailer chooses OfferFairTerms (Generous), this gives the supplier 9, and the retailer 1.

If the supplier chooses DropOut (Exit), the result is always 0, 0.

If the supplier chooses HonorDeal (Collaborate) and the retailer chooses CancelOrder (Exit), both get 0, 0.
If the supplier chooses HonorDeal (Collaborate) and the retailer chooses UseStrictTerms (Defensive), this gives the supplier 1, and the retailer 9.
If the supplier chooses HonorDeal (Collaborate) and the retailer chooses OfferFairTerms (Generous), this gives the supplier 5, and the retailer 5.
