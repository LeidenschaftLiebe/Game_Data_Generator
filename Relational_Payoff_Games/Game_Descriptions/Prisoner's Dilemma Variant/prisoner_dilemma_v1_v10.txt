1. The Prisoner's Dilemma

Two suspects are held in separate cells, unable to communicate. Each must decide whether to stay silent or betray the other.

If both stay silent, they each get a light sentence.
If one betrays while the other stays silent, the betrayer goes free while the other receives a heavy sentence.
If both betray, they both receive medium sentences.






2. The Hacker's Pact

Two hackers are caught during a cyberattack and interrogated separately. Each must decide whether to deny involvement or reveal the other's role.

If both deny, they face a light penalty.
If one reveals while the other denies, the revealer walks free while the denier faces a heavy penalty.
If both reveal, each receives a medium penalty.






3. The Athlete's Betrayal

Two athletes on the same team are suspected of doping and questioned separately. Each must decide whether to admit nothing or accuse the other.

If both admit nothing, they each face a light suspension.
If one accuses while the other stays quiet, the accuser avoids punishment while the quiet one gets a heavy ban.
If both accuse, each receives a medium suspension.






4. The Businessman's Secret

Two business partners are investigated for fraud and questioned separately. Each must decide whether to stay loyal or confess against the other.

If both stay loyal, they face only a small fine.
If one confesses while the other stays loyal, the confessor avoids punishment while the loyal one bears a heavy fine.
If both confess, they share a medium penalty.






5. The Student's Test

Two students are caught cheating on an exam and questioned separately. Each must decide whether to deny or blame the other.

If both deny, they each get a light penalty.
If one blames while the other denies, the blamer goes unpunished while the denier receives a heavy penalty.
If both blame, they each receive a medium penalty.






6. The Soldier's Silence

Two captured soldiers are interrogated separately. Each must decide whether to keep silent or give information about the other.

If both keep silent, they face only a light punishment.
If one gives information while the other stays silent, the informer avoids punishment while the silent one suffers harsh treatment.
If both give information, each receives a medium punishment.






7. The Scientist's Trial

Two scientists are accused of falsifying data and questioned separately. Each must decide whether to defend each other or reveal the truth.

If both defend each other, they receive only a small sanction.
If one reveals while the other defends, the revealer avoids blame while the defender receives a severe sanction.
If both reveal, they both face a medium sanction.






8. The Pirate's Oath

Two pirates are captured with stolen treasure and interrogated separately. Each must decide whether to stay silent or betray the other.

If both stay silent, they face only a small punishment.
If one betrays while the other stays silent, the betrayer avoids punishment while the silent one is severely punished.
If both betray, they each face a medium punishment.






9. The Spy's Interrogation

Two spies are captured behind enemy lines and interrogated separately. Each must decide whether to keep quiet or expose the other.

If both keep quiet, they endure a light punishment.
If one exposes while the other keeps quiet, the exposer is released while the quiet one is harshly punished.
If both expose, they receive medium punishment.






10. The Politician's Scandal

Two politicians are accused of corruption and questioned separately. Each must decide whether to deny everything or implicate the other.

If both deny, they face minor consequences.
If one implicates while the other denies, the implicator avoids punishment while the denier receives major consequences.
If both implicate, they each face medium consequences.
