V3 (software launch)

A software company first chooses whether to launch a product or stay out.
If it stays out, the process ends immediately and each side gets a final reward of 1.

If it launches, the rival company then chooses whether to launch too or stay out.
If the rival stays out, the final reward is -1 for the first company and 0 for the rival.

If the rival also launches, the first company then chooses between a stronger rollout and a safer rollout.
The stronger rollout gives a final reward of 4 to the first company and -1 to the rival.
The safer rollout gives a final reward of 3 to the first company and 2 to the rival.
