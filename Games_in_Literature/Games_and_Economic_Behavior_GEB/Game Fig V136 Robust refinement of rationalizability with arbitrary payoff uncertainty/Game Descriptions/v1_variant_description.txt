V1 [Startup pivot]

A founder moves first and chooses between ScaleUp (U) and Delay (D).

Then an investor responds, but the investor cannot tell whether the founder scaled up or delayed.
The investor chooses between LeadRound (L), RemainPassive (R), and ProposePivot (X).

If the founder chose ScaleUp (U) and the investor chooses LeadRound (L), the result is 1, 3.
If the founder chose ScaleUp (U) and the investor chooses RemainPassive (R), the result is 0, 2.
If the founder chose ScaleUp (U) and the investor chooses ProposePivot (X), the result is 1, 1.

If the founder chose Delay (D) and the investor chooses LeadRound (L), the result is 0, 2.
If the founder chose Delay (D) and the investor chooses RemainPassive (R), the result is 1, 3.

If the founder chose Delay (D) and the investor chooses ProposePivot (X), the founder moves again.
The founder then chooses between AcceptPivot (u) and RejectPivot (d).

Delay (D), ProposePivot (X), AcceptPivot (u) leads to 2, 1.
Delay (D), ProposePivot (X), RejectPivot (d) leads to 0, 1.
