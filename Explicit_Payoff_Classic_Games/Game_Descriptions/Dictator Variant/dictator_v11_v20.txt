11) Disaster Relief Fund — $2 million

A national disaster agency receives a one-time relief fund of $2 million.
The director alone decides whether to keep the full $2 million for headquarters, transfer $0.6 million to a local partner, or split the money evenly at $1 million each.
The local partner has no vote; whatever the director selects is final.






12) Philanthropic Pledge — $500 thousand

A foundation officer controls $500 thousand earmarked for a pilot program.
They may keep the full $500 thousand for central operations, pass $150 thousand to a grassroots partner, or divide it evenly at $250 thousand each.
The partner has no ability to challenge the decision.






13) Corporate CSR Budget — $1.2 million

The neighborhood fund has no say.

The squad fund cannot object.
