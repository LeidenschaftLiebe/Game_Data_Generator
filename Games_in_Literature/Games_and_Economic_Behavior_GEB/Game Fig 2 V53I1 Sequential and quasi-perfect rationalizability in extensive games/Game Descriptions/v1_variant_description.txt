V1 — Museum Launch

A museum director is deciding how to handle a new exhibit.
The director first chooses among Keep Current Plan (L), Soft Opening (M), and Full Premiere (R).

If the director chooses Keep Current Plan (L), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the director and reward 2 to the curator.

If the director chooses Soft Opening (M) or Full Premiere (R), the curator then chooses between
Left Review (l) and Right Review (r).
When the curator moves, the curator does not know whether the director chose
Soft Opening (M) or Full Premiere (R).

If the director chose Soft Opening (M) and the curator chooses Left Review (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the director and reward 1 to the curator.
If the director chose Soft Opening (M) and the curator chooses Right Review (r),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the director and reward 0 to the curator.

If the director chose Full Premiere (R) and the curator chooses Left Review (l),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the director and reward 0 to the curator.
If the director chose Full Premiere (R) and the curator chooses Right Review (r),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the director and reward 3 to the curator.
