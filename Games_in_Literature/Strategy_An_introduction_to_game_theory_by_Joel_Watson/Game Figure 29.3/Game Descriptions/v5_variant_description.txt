V5 (cybersecurity platform rollout)

At the outset, the platform owner may be either an ordinary owner or a cooperative owner.
The ordinary owner occurs with probability 3/4.
The cooperative owner occurs with probability 1/4.
The owner learns which type applies.
The outside partner does not observe the owner’s type.

If the owner is ordinary, the owner first chooses whether to invest in the platform rollout or not.
If the ordinary owner does not invest, the game ends with a final reward of 0 for the owner and 0 for the outside partner.
If the ordinary owner invests, the outside partner then chooses whether to invest as well or not.
The outside partner does not know whether the investor is the ordinary owner or the cooperative owner.
If the outside partner does not invest after the ordinary owner invests, the final reward is -2 for the owner and 0 for the outside partner.
If the outside partner invests after the ordinary owner invests, the owner then chooses whether to keep the platform selfishly or share it.
If the ordinary owner keeps it selfishly, the final reward is 6 for the owner and -2 for the outside partner.
If the ordinary owner shares it, the final reward is 2 for the owner and 2 for the outside partner.

If the owner is cooperative, the owner first chooses whether to invest in the platform rollout or not.
If the cooperative owner does not invest, the game ends with a final reward of 0 for the owner and 0 for the outside partner.
If the cooperative owner invests, the outside partner then chooses whether to invest as well or not.
The outside partner again does not know whether the investor is ordinary or cooperative.
If the outside partner does not invest after the cooperative owner invests, the final reward is 1 for the owner and 0 for the outside partner.
If the outside partner invests after the cooperative owner invests, the owner then chooses whether to keep the platform selfishly or share it.
If the cooperative owner keeps it selfishly, the final reward is 1 for the owner and -2 for the outside partner.
If the cooperative owner shares it, the final reward is 2 for the owner and 2 for the outside partner.
