V5 (coffee shop expansion)

A coffee chain first decides whether to open in a neighborhood or stay out.
If it stays out, the game ends with 2 for the first chain and 2 for the existing chain.
If it opens, the existing chain then decides whether to stay out of the local fight or compete directly.
If the existing chain stays out, the outcome is 1 for the entrant and 3 for the existing chain.
If the existing chain also competes, the entrant then chooses between two ways of operating.
One choice leads to 4 for the entrant and 2 for the existing chain.
The other leads to 3 for the entrant and 4 for the existing chain.
