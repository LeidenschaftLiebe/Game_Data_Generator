Strategic Equivalence Notion: Better-response equivalence

Constraints:
Check that the shoes are on sale with probability 0.5 and not on sale with probability 0.5.
Check that Brenda’s two decision nodes after Amy does not take the newspaper are in the same information set.
Check that Brenda’s decision nodes after Amy takes the newspaper are in different information sets, because the newspaper reveals whether the shoes are on sale.
Check that if the shoes are on sale, Amy does not take the newspaper, and Brenda goes to the mall, the final reward is 3 for Amy and 1 for Brenda.
Check that if the shoes are not on sale, Amy takes the newspaper, and Brenda goes to the mall, the final reward is -1 for Amy and -1 for Brenda.
