V1 — Product Routing

A firm begins by choosing between Internal Route (a) and External Route (b).
If it chooses Internal Route (a), the operations lead chooses between Local Release (c)
and Escalate Review (d).

If Local Release (c) is chosen, the process ends with reward 2 to the firm,
reward 2 to operations, reward 1 to the outside partner, and reward 0 to the reviewer.
If Escalate Review (d) is chosen, the reviewer moves later.

If the firm instead chooses External Route (b), the outside partner chooses between
Escalate Review (e) and Direct Delivery (f).
If Direct Delivery (f) is chosen, the process ends with reward 1 to the firm,
reward 0 to operations, reward 2 to the outside partner, and reward 0 to the reviewer.

If the reviewer gets to move, the reviewer cannot tell whether the earlier path was
Internal Route (a), Escalate Review (d) or External Route (b), Escalate Review (e).
The reviewer then chooses between Approve (g), Revise (h), and Reject (k).

If the path is Internal Route (a), Escalate Review (d), Approve (g),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm, reward 1 to operations, reward 0 to the outside partner,
and reward 0 to the reviewer.
If the path is Internal Route (a), Escalate Review (d), Revise (h),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 3 to operations, reward 1 to the outside partner,
and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is Internal Route (a), Escalate Review (d), Reject (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the firm, reward 4 to operations, reward 0 to the outside partner,
and reward 1 to the reviewer.

If the path is External Route (b), Escalate Review (e), Approve (g),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm, reward 2 to operations, reward 3 to the outside partner,
and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is External Route (b), Escalate Review (e), Revise (h),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm, reward 3 to operations, reward 1 to the outside partner,
and reward 1 to the reviewer.
If the path is External Route (b), Escalate Review (e), Reject (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the firm, reward 3 to operations, reward 4 to the outside partner,
and reward 0 to the reviewer.
