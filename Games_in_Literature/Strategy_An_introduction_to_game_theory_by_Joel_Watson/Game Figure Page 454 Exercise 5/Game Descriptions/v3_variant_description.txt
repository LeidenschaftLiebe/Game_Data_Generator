V3 (museum token exchange)

Two curators each start with 1 exhibit token.
On each turn, the curator whose turn it is may stop or continue.
Continuing means giving up 1 of their own tokens while adding 2 tokens to the other curator’s side.
If either curator stops, the process ends and both keep the tokens they currently hold.
If the fifth move passes without anyone stopping, both curators get 0.

Curator 1 moves first.
Stopping immediately gives final rewards of 1 and 1.
Continuing changes the token counts to 0 for Curator 1 and 3 for Curator 2.

Curator 2 then chooses.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 0 and 3.
Continuing changes the counts to 2 and 2.

Curator 1 then chooses again.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 2 and 2.
Continuing changes the counts to 1 and 4.

Curator 2 then chooses again.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 1 and 4.
Continuing changes the counts to 3 and 3.

Curator 1 then makes the final move.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 3 and 3.
Continuing means the five-move limit is reached with no stop, so both get 0.
