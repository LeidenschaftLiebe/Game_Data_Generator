V1 — Product Launch

A company is deciding how to release a new product.

The company moves first and chooses between Cancel Launch (O),
Soft Launch (L), and Wide Launch (R).

If the company chooses Cancel Launch (O), the process ends immediately.
The company gets reward 2 and the distributor gets reward 6.

If the company chooses Soft Launch (L) or Wide Launch (R),
the distributor moves next.

The distributor cannot tell whether the company chose
Soft Launch (L) or Wide Launch (R).

The distributor then chooses between Conservative Support (A)
and Full Support (B).

If the path is Soft Launch (L), Conservative Support (A),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the company and reward 1 to the distributor.

If the path is Soft Launch (L), Full Support (B),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the company and reward 2 to the distributor.

If the path is Wide Launch (R), Conservative Support (A),
the outcome gives reward -1 to the company and reward 3 to the distributor.

If the path is Wide Launch (R), Full Support (B),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the company and reward 2 to the distributor.
