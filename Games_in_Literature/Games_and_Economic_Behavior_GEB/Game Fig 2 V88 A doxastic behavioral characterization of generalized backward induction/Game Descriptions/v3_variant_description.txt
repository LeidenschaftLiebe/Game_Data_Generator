V3 — Film Distribution

A producer begins by choosing between Festival Route (a) and Commercial Route (b).
If Festival Route (a) is chosen, the distributor chooses between
Release Quietly (c) and Send to Review (d).

If Release Quietly (c) is chosen, the campaign ends with reward 2 to the producer,
reward 2 to the distributor, reward 1 to the marketing partner, and reward 0 to the critic panel.
If Send to Review (d) is chosen, the critic panel moves later.

If Commercial Route (b) is chosen first, the marketing partner chooses between
Send to Review (e) and Push Directly (f).
If Push Directly (f) is chosen, the campaign ends with reward 1 to the producer,
reward 0 to the distributor, reward 2 to the marketing partner, and reward 0 to the critic panel.

When the critic panel moves, it cannot tell whether the earlier path was
Festival Route (a), Send to Review (d) or Commercial Route (b), Send to Review (e).
The panel then chooses between Praise (g), Request Changes (h), and Dismiss (k).

If the path is Festival Route (a), Send to Review (d), Praise (g),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the producer, reward 1 to the distributor,
reward 0 to the marketing partner, and reward 0 to the panel.
If the path is Festival Route (a), Send to Review (d), Request Changes (h),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the producer, reward 3 to the distributor,
reward 1 to the marketing partner, and reward 2 to the panel.
If the path is Festival Route (a), Send to Review (d), Dismiss (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the producer, reward 4 to the distributor,
reward 0 to the marketing partner, and reward 1 to the panel.

If the path is Commercial Route (b), Send to Review (e), Praise (g),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the producer, reward 2 to the distributor,
reward 3 to the marketing partner, and reward 2 to the panel.
If the path is Commercial Route (b), Send to Review (e), Request Changes (h),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the producer, reward 3 to the distributor,
reward 1 to the marketing partner, and reward 1 to the panel.
If the path is Commercial Route (b), Send to Review (e), Dismiss (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the producer, reward 3 to the distributor,
reward 4 to the marketing partner, and reward 0 to the panel.
