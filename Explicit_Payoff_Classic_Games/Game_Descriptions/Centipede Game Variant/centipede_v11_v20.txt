11) Renewable Energy Investment

Two environmental investors take turns deciding whether to cash out or pass to let the solar fund grow.
Each pass increases the project’s total value, but whoever cashes out first secures the larger personal payout.
The game ends when it comes back to the first investor’s final decision.

If the first takes immediately, they earn $100 K, and the second earns $50 K.
If the first passes and the second takes, they earn $150 K and $100 K.
If the second passes again and the first takes later, they earn $250 K and $150 K.
If both pass until the end, each receives $300 K.
