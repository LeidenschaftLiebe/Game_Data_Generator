V4 — Desert Relay

A relay team starts from either Dune Camp or Rock Camp.
Each starting point occurs with probability 1/2.
The team captain then chooses between Left Route (L) and Right Route (R).

If the team starts from Dune Camp and the captain chooses Left Route (L),
the navigator chooses between Lead March (l) and Retreat Marker (r).
If the path is Dune Camp, Left Route (L), Lead March (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the captain and reward 2 to the navigator.
If the path is Dune Camp, Left Route (L), Retreat Marker (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the team starts from Dune Camp and the captain chooses Right Route (R),
the navigator again chooses between Lead March (l) and Retreat Marker (r).
If the path is Dune Camp, Right Route (R), Lead March (l),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the captain and reward 2 to the navigator.
If the path is Dune Camp, Right Route (R), Retreat Marker (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the team starts from Rock Camp and the captain chooses Left Route (L),
the navigator chooses between Lead March (l) and Retreat Marker (r).
If the path is Rock Camp, Left Route (L), Lead March (l),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the captain and reward 2 to the navigator.
If the path is Rock Camp, Left Route (L), Retreat Marker (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the team starts from Rock Camp and the captain chooses Right Route (R),
the navigator chooses between Use Ramp (u) and Dig Through (d).
If the path is Rock Camp, Right Route (R), Use Ramp (u), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.
If the path is Rock Camp, Right Route (R), Dig Through (d),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the captain and reward 2 to the navigator.
