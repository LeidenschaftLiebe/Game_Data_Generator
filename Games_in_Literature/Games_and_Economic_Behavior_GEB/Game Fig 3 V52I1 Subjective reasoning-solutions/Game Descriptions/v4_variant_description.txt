V4 — Prototype Development

Alice is a product lead deciding what to do with an early prototype.
She first chooses SendPrototype [l], ReworkDesign [m], or DropProject [r].

If Alice chooses ReworkDesign [m] or DropProject [r], the project goes to Bob in operations.
Bob does not know which of those two decisions Alice made.
Bob then chooses Continue [L] or Stop [R].

If Alice chooses SendPrototype [l], the product goes to Emma in user testing.
If Alice chooses ReworkDesign [m] and Bob chooses Continue [L], the product also goes to Emma.

When Emma receives it, she cannot tell whether she is testing the original prototype or a reworked version that Bob allowed to continue.
Emma then chooses TestModeA [p] or TestModeB [q].

If Alice chose SendPrototype [l], then whether Emma chooses TestModeA [p] or TestModeB [q], Alice gets 0, Bob gets 2, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose ReworkDesign [m] and Bob chose Continue [L], then whether Emma chooses TestModeA [p] or TestModeB [q], Alice gets 2, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose ReworkDesign [m] and Bob chose Stop [R], then Alice gets 1, Bob gets 1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose DropProject [r] and Bob chose Continue [L], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets -1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose DropProject [r] and Bob chose Stop [R], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.
