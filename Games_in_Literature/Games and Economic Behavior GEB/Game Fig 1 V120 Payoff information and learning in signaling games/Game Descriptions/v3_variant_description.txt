V3 [Submarine mission]

A secret systems check first determines whether the submarine is SilentMode or ExposedMode.
SilentMode happens with probability 9/10, and ExposedMode happens with probability 1/10.

The captain then chooses between AbortMission and Proceed.

If the captain chooses AbortMission, the mission ends there.
This gives 0 to the captain, and 0 to the sonar officer.

If the captain chooses Proceed, the sonar officer chooses between Climb, Descend, and SweepSideways.
The sonar officer cannot tell whether the submarine is in SilentMode or ExposedMode.

If the submarine is in SilentMode and the sonar officer chooses Climb, this gives 2 to the captain, and 1 to the sonar officer.
If the submarine is in SilentMode and the sonar officer chooses Descend, this gives -1 to the captain, and 0 to the sonar officer.
If the submarine is in SilentMode and the sonar officer chooses SweepSideways, this gives 1 to the captain, and -1 to the sonar officer.

If the submarine is in ExposedMode and the sonar officer chooses Climb, this gives 1 to the captain, and 0 to the sonar officer.
If the submarine is in ExposedMode and the sonar officer chooses Descend, this gives -1 to the captain, and 1 to the sonar officer.
If the submarine is in ExposedMode and the sonar officer chooses SweepSideways, this gives 3 to the captain, and -1 to the sonar officer.
