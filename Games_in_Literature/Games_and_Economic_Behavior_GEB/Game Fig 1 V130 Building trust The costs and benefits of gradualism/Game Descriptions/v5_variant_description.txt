V5 [Film production]

A producer is deciding how much of a film budget to trust to a new studio partner.

The producer moves first and chooses one of four levels:
NoDeal (N), SmallProject (L), MidProject (M), or BlockbusterProject (H).

If the producer chooses NoDeal (N), this gives the producer 24, and the studio partner 15.

If the producer chooses SmallProject (L), the studio partner chooses between SupportRelease (R) and GrabValue (D).
If the studio partner chooses SupportRelease (R), this gives the producer 32, and the studio partner 22.
If the studio partner chooses GrabValue (D), this gives the producer 20, and the studio partner 42.

If the producer chooses MidProject (M), the studio partner again chooses between SupportRelease (R) and GrabValue (D).
If the studio partner chooses SupportRelease (R), this gives the producer 44, and the studio partner 36.
If the studio partner chooses GrabValue (D), this gives the producer 12, and the studio partner 72.

If the producer chooses BlockbusterProject (H), the studio partner again chooses between SupportRelease (R) and GrabValue (D).
If the studio partner chooses SupportRelease (R), this gives the producer 56, and the studio partner 42.
If the studio partner chooses GrabValue (D), this gives the producer 0, and the studio partner 80.
