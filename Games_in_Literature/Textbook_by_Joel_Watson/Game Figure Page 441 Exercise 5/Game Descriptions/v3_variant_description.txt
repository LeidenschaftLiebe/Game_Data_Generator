V3 (festival prop game)

One performer first decides whether to place a prop on her head or on the stage floor.
A second performer sees that choice.
Then the second performer decides whether to wear his own prop or set it on the floor.

If the second performer wears his prop, the scene ends and all three people get 0.
If he sets it on the floor, a third performer must guess whether the first prop is on the first performer’s head.
The third performer says yes or no.
The third performer sees what the second performer did, but not what the first performer did.

So the third performer faces the same guessing problem across two different hidden situations.

If the first performer put the prop on her head and the third performer says yes, the guess is correct.
The final reward is -1 for the first performer, 0 for the second, and 1 for the third.
If the first performer put the prop on her head and the third performer says no, the guess is wrong.
The final reward is 1 for the first performer, 0 for the second, and -1 for the third.

If the first performer put the prop on the floor and the third performer says yes, the guess is wrong.
The final reward is 1 for the first performer, 0 for the second, and -1 for the third.
If the first performer put the prop on the floor and the third performer says no, the guess is correct.
The final reward is -1 for the first performer, 0 for the second, and 1 for the third.
