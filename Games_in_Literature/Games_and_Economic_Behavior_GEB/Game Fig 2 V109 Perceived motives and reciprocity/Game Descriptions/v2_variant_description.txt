V2 — Book Release

A publisher and an author are deciding how to handle a new release.

The publisher moves first and chooses between Small Release and Heavy Promotion.

If the publisher chooses Small Release, a later stage falls into one of three cases,
with probabilities 0.01, 0.98, and 0.01.

If case 1 happens after Small Release, the author chooses between
Stay Quiet and Push Back.

If the path is Small Release, 1, Stay Quiet, the outcome is
Publisher: $4.50 and Author: $2.50.

If the path is Small Release, 1, Push Back, the outcome is
Publisher: $3.00 and Author: $2.00.

If case 2 happens after Small Release, the outcome is
Publisher: $4.50 and Author: $2.50.

If case 3 happens after Small Release, the outcome is again
Publisher: $4.50 and Author: $2.50.

If the publisher chooses Heavy Promotion, the same three cases follow
with probabilities 0.01, 0.98, and 0.01.

If case 1 happens after Heavy Promotion, the outcome is
Publisher: $4.00 and Author: $4.00.

If case 2 happens after Heavy Promotion, the outcome is
Publisher: $4.00 and Author: $4.00.

If case 3 happens after Heavy Promotion, the author chooses between
Keep Pace and Do Extra Publicity.

If the path is Heavy Promotion, 3, Keep Pace, the outcome is
Publisher: $4.00 and Author: $4.00.

If the path is Heavy Promotion, 3, Do Extra Publicity, the outcome is
Publisher: $5.50 and Author: $3.50.
