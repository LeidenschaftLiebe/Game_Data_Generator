V0 (closest to the book)

An entrant first decides whether to stay out of the market or enter it.
If the entrant stays out, the interaction ends immediately and the final reward is 0 for the entrant and 4 for the incumbent.

If the entrant enters, the incumbent then decides whether to accommodate the entry or start a price war.
If the incumbent accommodates, the final reward is 2 for the entrant and 2 for the incumbent.
If the incumbent starts a price war, the final reward is -1 for the entrant and -1 for the incumbent.
