V0 (closest to the figure)

Player 1 moves first and chooses between action A, action B, and action C.
If Player 1 chooses action A, then Player 2 chooses between action X and action Y.
If Player 2 chooses action X after A, the outcome is 2 for Player 1 and 5 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses action Y after A, the outcome is 5 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses action B, then Player 2 again chooses between action X and action Y.
At that point, Player 2 cannot tell whether the game reached this decision after action A or after action B.
If Player 2 chooses action X after B, the outcome is 5 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses action Y after B, the outcome is 2 for Player 1 and 5 for Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses action C, then Player 1 makes one more choice between action W and action Z.
If Player 1 chooses action W, the outcome is 2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses action Z, the outcome is 3 for Player 1 and 3 for Player 2.
