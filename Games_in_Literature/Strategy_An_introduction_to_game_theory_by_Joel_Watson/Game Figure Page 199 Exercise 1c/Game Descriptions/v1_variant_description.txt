V1 (international research expedition)

A lead scientist first decides whether to keep a project on its original path or shift it into an intensive field phase.
If the scientist keeps the original path, the project ends immediately and the final reward is 2 for the scientist, 2 for the logistics director, and 2 for the field specialist.

If the scientist shifts into the field phase, the logistics director then chooses among route A, route B, and route C.
If the logistics director chooses route C, the project ends immediately with a final reward of 4 for the scientist, 3 for the logistics director, and 1 for the field specialist.

If the logistics director chooses route A, the field specialist then chooses between protocol X and protocol Y.
Protocol X after route A leads to a final reward of 3 for the scientist, 2 for the logistics director, and 1 for the field specialist.
Protocol Y after route A leads to a final reward of 5 for the scientist, 0 for the logistics director, and 0 for the field specialist.

If the logistics director chooses route B, the field specialist again chooses between protocol X and protocol Y.
At that point, the field specialist cannot tell whether the project reached this stage after route A or route B.
Protocol X after route B leads to a final reward of 1 for the scientist, 2 for the logistics director, and 6 for the field specialist.
Protocol Y after route B leads to a final reward of 7 for the scientist, 5 for the logistics director, and 5 for the field specialist.
