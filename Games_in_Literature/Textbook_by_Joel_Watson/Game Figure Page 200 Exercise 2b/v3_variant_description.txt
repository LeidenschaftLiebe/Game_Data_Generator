V3 (space habitat design)

A mission architect first chooses between the upper-orbit habitat plan and the deep-space habitat plan.

If the architect chooses the upper-orbit habitat plan, the systems chief then chooses between module layout A and module layout B.
If the systems chief chooses module layout A, the final reward is 2 for the architect and 3 for the systems chief.
If the systems chief chooses module layout B, the final reward is 5 for the architect and 4 for the systems chief.

If the architect chooses the deep-space habitat plan, the systems chief then chooses between engineering path C and engineering path D.
If the systems chief chooses engineering path D, the final reward is 0 for the architect and 2 for the systems chief.
If the systems chief chooses engineering path C, the architect then chooses between life-support design E and life-support design F.
Life-support design E gives a final reward of 6 to the architect and 2 to the systems chief.
Life-support design F gives a final reward of 2 to the architect and 6 to the systems chief.
