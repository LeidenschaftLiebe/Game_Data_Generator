Player 1 moves first and chooses between action A, action B, and action C.
If Player 1 chooses action C, the game ends immediately with a final reward of 5 for Player 1 and 5 for Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses action A, then Player 2 chooses between action X and action Y.
If Player 2 chooses action X after A, the final reward is 8 for Player 1 and 8 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses action Y after A, the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses action B, then Player 2 again chooses between action X and action Y.
When Player 2 makes that choice, Player 2 cannot tell whether the game reached this point after action A or after action B.
If Player 2 chooses action X after B, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses action Y after B, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and 6 for Player 2.
