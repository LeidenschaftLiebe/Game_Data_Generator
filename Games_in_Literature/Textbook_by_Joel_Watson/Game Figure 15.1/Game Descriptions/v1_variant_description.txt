V1 (new coffee shop vs local café)

A new coffee shop first decides whether to stay out of a neighborhood or open there.
If the new shop stays out, the story ends immediately and the final reward is 0 for the newcomer and 4 for the local café.

If the new shop opens, the local café then decides whether to share the market peacefully or slash prices aggressively.
If the local café shares the market, the final reward is 2 for the newcomer and 2 for the local café.
If the local café slashes prices and starts a price war, the final reward is -1 for the newcomer and -1 for the local café.
