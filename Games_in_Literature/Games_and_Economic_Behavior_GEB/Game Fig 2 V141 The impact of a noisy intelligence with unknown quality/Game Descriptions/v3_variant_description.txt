V3 [Illegal lab inspection]

A suspected organization decides whether to run a hidden lab.
It first chooses between NoLab (NB) and BuildLab (B).

A monitoring system then sends a noisy signal.
It sends LabSignal (b) with probability 0.8 if the lab exists, and with probability 0.2 if it does not.
It sends NoLabSignal (nb) with probability 0.8 if the lab does not exist, and with probability 0.2 if it does.

After that, the authority chooses between Raid (A) and NoRaid (NA).

If the organization chose NoLab (NB) and the authority chooses Raid (A), the result is 0.2 for the organization and 0.3 for the authority.
If the organization chose NoLab (NB) and the authority chooses NoRaid (NA), the result is 0.7 for the organization and 1 for the authority.

If the organization chose BuildLab (B) and the authority chooses Raid (A), the result is 0 for the organization and 0.8 for the authority.
If the organization chose BuildLab (B) and the authority chooses NoRaid (NA), the result is 1 for the organization and 0 for the authority.
