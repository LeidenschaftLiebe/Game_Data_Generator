V4 [Orbital debris cleanup]

A mission director first chooses between WideSweep (A), NarrowSweep (B), and AbortLaunch (E).

If the director chooses AbortLaunch (E), the mission ends there.
This gives 3 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight operator.

If the director chooses NarrowSweep (B), the flight operator chooses between LowBurn (a) and HighBurn (b).
LowBurn (a) gives 0 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight operator.
HighBurn (b) gives 1 to the mission director, and 1 to the flight operator.

If the director chooses WideSweep (A), the director then chooses between InnerArc (C) and OuterArc (D).

After InnerArc (C), the flight operator chooses between LowBurn (a) and HighBurn (b).
LowBurn (a) gives 4 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight operator.
HighBurn (b) gives 2 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight operator.

After OuterArc (D), the flight operator chooses between LowBurn (a) and HighBurn (b).
LowBurn (a) gives 1 to the mission director, and 1 to the flight operator.
HighBurn (b) gives 0 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight operator.

When choosing between LowBurn (a) and HighBurn (b), the flight operator cannot tell which earlier situation led there.
