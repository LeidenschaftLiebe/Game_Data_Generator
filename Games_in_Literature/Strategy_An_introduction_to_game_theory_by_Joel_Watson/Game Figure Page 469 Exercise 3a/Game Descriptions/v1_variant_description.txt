V1 (music festival booking)

A festival organizer first learns whether the event is running under schedule A or schedule B.
That schedule is determined randomly, with each case happening half the time.
The organizer sees the schedule, but the headliner does not.

If the event is in schedule A, the organizer chooses between plan U and plan D.
The headliner then chooses between option L and option R without knowing which schedule was drawn.
Under schedule A with plan U, option L gives a final reward of 2 for the organizer and 2 for the headliner, while option R gives 0 for both.
Under schedule A with plan D, option L gives 0 for both, while option R gives 4 for the organizer and 4 for the headliner.

If the event is in schedule B, the organizer chooses between plan u and plan d.
The headliner again chooses between L and R without knowing whether the event is in schedule A or schedule B.
Under schedule B with plan u, option L gives 0 for the organizer and 2 for the headliner, while option R gives 2 for the organizer and 0 for the headliner.
Under schedule B with plan d, option L gives 4 for the organizer and 0 for the headliner, while option R gives 0 for the organizer and 4 for the headliner.
