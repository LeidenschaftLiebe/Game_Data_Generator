V5 [Canyon rescue cable]

A rescue leader and a rope specialist are deciding how to cross a canyon during a rescue.

The rescue leader moves first and chooses between StaticCamp (T) and CableCrossing (B).

If the rescue leader chooses StaticCamp (T), then the rope specialist chooses between UpperLine (U) and DeepLine (D).
If the rope specialist chooses UpperLine (U), this gives 2 to the rescue leader, and 2 to the rope specialist.
If the rope specialist chooses DeepLine (D), this gives 0 to the rescue leader, and 0 to the rope specialist.

If the rescue leader chooses CableCrossing (B), then the rope specialist chooses between LeftAnchor (L) and RightAnchor (R).

If the rope specialist chooses RightAnchor (R), the operation ends there.
This gives 1 to the rescue leader, and 1 to the rope specialist.

If the rope specialist chooses LeftAnchor (L), the rope specialist later chooses between UpperLine (U) and DeepLine (D).
If the rope specialist chooses UpperLine (U), this gives 0 to the rescue leader, and 0 to the rope specialist.
If the rope specialist chooses DeepLine (D), this gives 3 to the rescue leader, and 3 to the rope specialist.

The two UpperLine (U) or DeepLine (D) nodes are linked, so the rope specialist cannot tell whether 
that later choice follows StaticCamp (T) or follows CableCrossing (B) and then LeftAnchor (L).
