V4 [Software release]

A product lead and a compliance reviewer are deciding how to release an update.
The product lead first chooses between Pipeline A, Pipeline B, and Cancel Release.
If the release is cancelled, the process ends immediately.
The product lead gets 1 and the reviewer gets 0.

If Pipeline A or Pipeline B is chosen,
the reviewer then chooses between Escalate (d), Auto Approve (e), and Standard Approval (f),
without knowing which pipeline was used.

If the reviewer chooses Auto Approve (e), the process ends immediately.
After Pipeline A then Auto Approve, the product lead gets 0 and the reviewer gets 5.
After Pipeline B then Auto Approve, the product lead gets 0 and the reviewer gets 4.

If the reviewer chooses Standard Approval (f), the process also ends immediately.
After Pipeline A then Standard Approval, the product lead gets 1 and the reviewer gets 4.
After Pipeline B then Standard Approval, the product lead gets 1 and the reviewer gets 5.

If the reviewer chooses Escalate (d),
the reviewer later chooses between Sign Off (g) and Block (h),
still without knowing whether the case came from Pipeline A or Pipeline B.

After Pipeline A then Escalate then Sign Off, the product lead gets 2 and the reviewer gets 1.
After Pipeline A then Escalate then Block, both get 0.
After Pipeline B then Escalate then Sign Off, the product lead gets 2 and the reviewer gets 0.
After Pipeline B then Escalate then Block, the product lead gets 0 and the reviewer gets 3.
