V4 (record label collaboration)

At the outset, a major record label may be either a loyal partner or a predatory partner, with probability 0.5 for each.
The label knows its own type.
The independent studio knows only that the two types are equally likely.

After learning its type, the label chooses whether to Back the collaboration or Hijack it.
The independent studio then chooses whether to Reveal its master tracks or Keep them private.
When the studio makes that choice, it does not know the label’s type and does not observe whether the label chose Back or Hijack.

If the label is predatory and chooses Back, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 5 for the label and 1 for the studio.
If the label is predatory and chooses Back, then Keep private leads to a final reward of 0 for the label and 3 for the studio.

If the label is predatory and chooses Hijack, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 8 for the label and -1 for the studio.
If the label is predatory and chooses Hijack, then Keep private leads to a final reward of 7 for the label and 0 for the studio.

If the label is loyal and chooses Back, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 9 for the label and 9 for the studio.
If the label is loyal and chooses Back, then Keep private leads to a final reward of 0 for the label and 0 for the studio.

If the label is loyal and chooses Hijack, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 2 for the label and 0 for the studio.
If the label is loyal and chooses Hijack, then Keep private leads to a final reward of 0 for the label and 4 for the studio.
