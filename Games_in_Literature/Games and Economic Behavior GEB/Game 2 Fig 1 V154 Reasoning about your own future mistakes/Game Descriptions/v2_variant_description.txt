V2 [Airport trip]

A traveler and a driver both want the traveler to arrive on time for an important meeting.
The traveler first chooses between going personally and letting the driver handle the final leg.
If the traveler chooses to go personally, the traveler later chooses between following the route correctly and making a wrong turn.
If the traveler follows the route correctly, both sides end with 1.
If the traveler makes a wrong turn, both sides end with 0.
If the traveler lets the driver handle it instead, the driver chooses between taking the correct route and missing the turn.
If the driver takes the correct route, both sides end with 1.
If the driver misses the turn, both sides end with 0.
