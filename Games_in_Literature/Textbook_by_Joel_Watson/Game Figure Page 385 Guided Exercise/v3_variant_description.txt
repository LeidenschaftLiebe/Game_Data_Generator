V3 (museum exhibit preview)

At the outset, a special exhibit may either be open for preview that day or not, with probability 0.5 for each case.
Jordan learns whether the preview is open.
Casey does not observe this directly.

If the preview is open, Jordan chooses whether to bring the museum bulletin to Casey or not.
If Jordan brings the bulletin in the preview-open case, Casey then chooses whether to go to the museum or not.
If Casey goes after seeing the bulletin in the preview-open case, the final reward is 1 for Jordan and 1 for Casey.
If Casey refuses after seeing the bulletin in the preview-open case, the final reward is -2 for Jordan and 0 for Casey.

If Jordan does not bring the bulletin in the preview-open case, Casey still chooses whether to go to the museum or not.
If Casey goes after Jordan does not bring the bulletin in the preview-open case, the final reward is 3 for Jordan and 1 for Casey.
If Casey refuses after Jordan does not bring the bulletin in the preview-open case, the final reward is 0 for Jordan and 0 for Casey.

If the preview is not open, Jordan again chooses whether to bring the bulletin or not.
If Jordan brings the bulletin in the preview-closed case, Casey then chooses whether to go to the museum or not.
If Casey goes after seeing the bulletin in the preview-closed case, the final reward is -1 for Jordan and -1 for Casey.
If Casey refuses after seeing the bulletin in the preview-closed case, the final reward is -2 for Jordan and 0 for Casey.

If Jordan does not bring the bulletin in the preview-closed case, Casey still chooses whether to go to the museum or not.
Casey cannot tell from the missing bulletin whether the preview is open or not.
If Casey goes after Jordan does not bring the bulletin in the preview-closed case, the final reward is 1 for Jordan and -1 for Casey.
If Casey refuses after Jordan does not bring the bulletin in the preview-closed case, the final reward is 0 for Jordan and 0 for Casey.
