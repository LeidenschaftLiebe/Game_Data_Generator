22. Soldier vs. Bridge

A soldier advances, while bridges determine the crossing.

The soldier can cross the broken bridge or the reinforced bridge.
The hazard can be weak planks or collapsing ropes.

If the broken bridge is chosen, the soldier always falls, regardless of the hazard.

If the reinforced bridge is chosen, the soldier always crosses safely, whichever hazard is present.
