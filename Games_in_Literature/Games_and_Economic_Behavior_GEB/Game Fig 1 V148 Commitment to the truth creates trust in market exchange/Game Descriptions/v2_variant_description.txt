V2 [Freelance project]

A client and a freelancer are deciding whether to start a small project.
The freelancer moves first and sends one of three messages: Undecided (m(E)), WillDeliverFairly (m(S)), or WillTakeAdvanceOnly (m(K)).

After reading the message, the client chooses between Hire (I) and DoNotHire (N).
If the client chooses DoNotHire (N), the interaction ends immediately and the result is 2 for the client and 0 for the freelancer.

If the client chooses Hire (I), the freelancer then chooses between DeliverProperly (S) and TakeMoneyAndRun (K).
After Undecided (m(E)), DeliverProperly (S) gives 4 to the client and 4 to the freelancer, 
while TakeMoneyAndRun (K) gives 0 to the client and 8 to the freelancer.

After WillDeliverFairly (m(S)), DeliverProperly (S) gives 4 to the client and 4 to the freelancer.
If the freelancer instead chooses TakeMoneyAndRun (K), the freelancer is fined for dishonest communication.
With f = 2, that result is 0 for the client and 6 for the freelancer.

After WillTakeAdvanceOnly (m(K)), TakeMoneyAndRun (K) gives 0 to the client and 8 to the freelancer.
If the freelancer instead chooses DeliverProperly (S), the freelancer is fined because the later choice does not match the earlier message.
With f = 2, that result is 4 for the client and 2 for the freelancer.
