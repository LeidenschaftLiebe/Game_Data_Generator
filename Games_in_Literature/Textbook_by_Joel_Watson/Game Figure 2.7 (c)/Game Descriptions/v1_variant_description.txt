V1 (airline fare competition)

Two airlines are competing on the same route.
Airline 1 posts its fare first, choosing either a high fare or a low fare.
Airline 2 observes that fare and then chooses its own ticket price.
If both airlines keep fares high, Airline 1 earns 10 million while Airline 2 earns 1 million.
If Airline 1 keeps fares high and Airline 2 cuts fares, Airline 1 earns 8 million while Airline 2 earns 2 million.
If Airline 1 cuts fares and Airline 2 keeps fares high, Airline 1 earns 11 million while Airline 2 earns nothing.
If both airlines cut fares, Airline 1 earns 9 million while Airline 2 earns 0.5 million.
