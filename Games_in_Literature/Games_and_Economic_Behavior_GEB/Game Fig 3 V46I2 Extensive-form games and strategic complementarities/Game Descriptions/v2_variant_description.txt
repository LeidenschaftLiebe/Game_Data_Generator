V2 — Optional Product Focus

The founder moves first and chooses DelayDecision [No] or LockDirection [Yes].

After that, the partner chooses DesignFocus [O] or BusinessFocus [B].

Then the founder chooses DesignFocus [O] or BusinessFocus [B].
At this later move, the founder knows whether the first move was DelayDecision [No] or LockDirection [Yes], but does not know which focus the partner selected.

If the first move was DelayDecision [No] and both later choices are DesignFocus [O], the founder gets 2 and the partner gets 1.
If the first move was DelayDecision [No] and both later choices are BusinessFocus [B], the founder gets 1 and the partner gets 2.
If the first move was DelayDecision [No] and the later choices do not match, both get 0.

If the first move was LockDirection [Yes] and both later choices are DesignFocus [O], the founder gets 2 and the partner gets 1.
If the first move was LockDirection [Yes] and both later choices are BusinessFocus [B], the founder gets 1 and the partner gets 2.
If the first move was LockDirection [Yes] and the later choices do not match, both get 0.
