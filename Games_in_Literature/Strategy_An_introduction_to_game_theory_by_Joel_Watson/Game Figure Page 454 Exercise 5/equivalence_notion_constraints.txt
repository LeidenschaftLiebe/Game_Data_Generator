Strategic Equivalence Notion: Better-response equivalence

Constraints:
Check that Player 1 moves first, Player 2 moves second, Player 1 moves third, Player 2 moves fourth, and Player 1 moves fifth.

Check that if nobody stops by the end of the fifth move, the final reward is 0 for both players.
