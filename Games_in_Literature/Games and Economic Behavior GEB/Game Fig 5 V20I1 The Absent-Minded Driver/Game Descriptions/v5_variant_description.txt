V5 — Attentive Visitor or Absent-Minded Visitor

At START, the visitor chooses between Attentive and AbsentMinded.

Under Attentive, the visitor first reaches HallX [X].
At HallX [X], the visitor exits with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or continues with probability 4/7.

If the visitor continues, the visitor reaches HallY [Y].
At HallY [Y], the visitor exits with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or continues with probability 4/7 and gets 1.

Under AbsentMinded, the visitor first reaches HallX [X].
At HallX [X], the visitor exits with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or continues with probability 1/2.

If the visitor continues, the visitor reaches HallY [Y].
At HallY [Y], the visitor exits with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or continues with probability 1/2 and gets 1.

Under AbsentMinded, the visitor cannot tell whether the current hallway is HallX [X] or HallY [Y].
