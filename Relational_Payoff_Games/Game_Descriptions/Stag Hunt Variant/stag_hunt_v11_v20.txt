11. The Explorers’ Expedition

Two explorers set out into uncharted lands. They can either cooperate to reach the hidden city or search for small villages alone.

If both seek the hidden city, each gains a great treasure.

If one goes for the city while the other searches villages, the village explorer gains a small bounty, while the city explorer returns with nothing.

If both explore villages, each secures a moderate treasure.






12. The Musicians’ Performance

Two musicians are invited to play. They can either perform together in a grand concert or play solo at small venues.

If both perform the concert, each enjoys a major success.

If one performs solo while the other attempts the concert, the soloist gets a small success, while the concert attempt brings nothing.

If both play solo, each earns a moderate success.
