V5 [Treasure salvage]

A salvage chief and a dive specialist are deciding how to recover a treasure cache.

The salvage chief moves first and chooses between AbortSearch (T) and ContinueSearch (B).

If the salvage chief chooses AbortSearch (T), the operation ends there.
This gives the salvage chief 2, and the dive specialist 6.

If the salvage chief chooses ContinueSearch (B), the dive specialist chooses between UpperWreck (U) and DeepWreck (D).

Later, the dive specialist chooses between LeftHold (L) and RightHold (R),
but at that later point the dive specialist cannot tell whether the earlier wreck choice was UpperWreck (U) or DeepWreck (D).

After UpperWreck (U), LeftHold (L) gives the salvage chief 4, and the dive specialist 4,
while RightHold (R) gives the salvage chief 0, and the dive specialist 0.

After DeepWreck (D), LeftHold (L) gives the salvage chief 0, and the dive specialist 0,
while RightHold (R) gives the salvage chief 1, and the dive specialist 1.
