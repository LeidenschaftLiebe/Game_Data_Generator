V4 [Catering order]

A head cook and a junior cook both want a catering order to go out properly.
The head cook first chooses between finishing the order personally and leaving the final step to the junior cook.
If the head cook finishes it personally, the head cook later chooses between plating it correctly and making a serving mistake.
If the head cook plates it correctly, both people end with 1.
If the head cook makes a serving mistake, both people end with 0.
If the head cook leaves the final step to the junior cook instead, the junior cook chooses between finishing it properly and messing it up.
If the junior cook finishes it properly, both people end with 1.
If the junior cook messes it up, both people end with 0.
