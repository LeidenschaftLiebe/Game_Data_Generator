V2 — Medical Triage

A doctor is deciding how to handle a case.
The doctor moves first and chooses between Direct Treatment (a), Standard Review (b),
and Minimal Intervention (c).

After that, the hospital administrator moves.
The administrator cannot tell which of those three medical routes was chosen.
The administrator then chooses between Approve Resources (d) and Limit Resources (e).

If the path is Direct Treatment (a), Approve Resources (d),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor and reward 1 to the administrator.
If the path is Direct Treatment (a), Limit Resources (e),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the doctor and reward 0 to the administrator.

If the path is Standard Review (b), Approve Resources (d),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the doctor and reward 1 to the administrator.
If the path is Standard Review (b), Limit Resources (e),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the doctor and reward 0 to the administrator.

If the path is Minimal Intervention (c), Approve Resources (d),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor and reward 0 to the administrator.
If the path is Minimal Intervention (c), Limit Resources (e),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor and reward 1 to the administrator.
