Player 1 moves first and chooses between action U and action D.
After that, Player 2 chooses between action A and action B.
When Player 2 makes that choice, Player 2 cannot tell whether Player 1 chose U or D.

If Player 2 chooses action A after U, the game ends immediately with 3 for Player 1 and 3 for Player 2.

If Player 2 chooses action B after U, then Player 1 chooses between action X and action Y.
If Player 1 chooses action X there, the final reward is 5 for Player 1 and 1 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses action Y there, the final reward is 3 for Player 1 and 6 for Player 2.

If Player 2 chooses action A after D, then Player 1 chooses between action W and action Z.
If Player 1 chooses action W there, the final reward is 4 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses action Z there, the final reward is 9 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.

If Player 2 chooses action B after D, the game ends immediately with 2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
