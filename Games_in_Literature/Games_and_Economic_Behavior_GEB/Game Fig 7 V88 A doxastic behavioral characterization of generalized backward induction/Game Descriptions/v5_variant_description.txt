V5 — Transfer Strategy

A club is deciding how to approach a player search.
The club moves first and chooses between Go All-In (a), Balanced Search (b),
and Wait Option (c).

After that, the sporting director moves.
The sporting director cannot tell which of those three search plans was chosen.
The sporting director then chooses between Back the Move (d) and Stay Cautious (e).

If the path is Go All-In (a), Back the Move (d),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club and reward 1 to the sporting director.
If the path is Go All-In (a), Stay Cautious (e),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the club and reward 0 to the sporting director.

If the path is Balanced Search (b), Back the Move (d),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the club and reward 1 to the sporting director.
If the path is Balanced Search (b), Stay Cautious (e),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club and reward 0 to the sporting director.

If the path is Wait Option (c), Back the Move (d),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club and reward 0 to the sporting director.
If the path is Wait Option (c), Stay Cautious (e),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club and reward 1 to the sporting director.
