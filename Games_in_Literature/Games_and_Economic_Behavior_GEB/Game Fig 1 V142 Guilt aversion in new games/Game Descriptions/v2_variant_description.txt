V2 [Research budget]

A lead researcher, a lab manager, and a passive colleague are involved in a budget decision.
The lead researcher first chooses between KeepBudget (Out) and TransferBudget (In).

If the lead researcher chooses KeepBudget (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the lead researcher, 100 for the lab manager, and 30 for the colleague.

If the lead researcher chooses TransferBudget (In), 25 units from the lead researcher’s own budget are placed under the lab manager’s control.
The lab manager then chooses between SmallReimbursement (Left) and FullReimbursement (Right).

If the lab manager chooses SmallReimbursement (Left), the lab manager keeps 20 and sends back 5 to the lead researcher.
That returned amount is doubled, so the lead researcher receives 10.
The result is 155 for the lead researcher, 120 for the lab manager, and 30 for the colleague.

If the lab manager chooses FullReimbursement (Right), the lab manager sends the full 25 back to the lead researcher.
That amount is doubled, so the lead researcher receives 50.
The result is 195 for the lead researcher, 100 for the lab manager, and 30 for the colleague.
