Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:

The root is a chance node that chooses between u and v with probability 1/2 each.
Player I moves after u and after v, choosing among R, S, and P.
After u, player II moves without observing player I’s action, and the three player II nodes there are in the same information set.
After v, player II moves without observing player I’s action, and the three player II nodes there are in the same information set.
The player II information set after u is different from the player II information set after v.
t terminals u_I_P_II_S and v_I_P_II_S, the payoffs are (-2, 4).
