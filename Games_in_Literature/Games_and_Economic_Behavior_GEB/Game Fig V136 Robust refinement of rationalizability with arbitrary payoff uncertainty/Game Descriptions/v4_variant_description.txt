V4 [Product launch]

A product lead moves first and chooses between GoBig (U) and GoSlow (D).

Then the marketing lead responds, but cannot tell whether the product lead went big or went slow.
The marketing lead chooses between MajorCampaign (L), MinimalCampaign (R), and Reposition (X).

If the product lead chose GoBig (U) and the marketing lead chooses MajorCampaign (L), the result is 1, 3.
If the product lead chose GoBig (U) and the marketing lead chooses MinimalCampaign (R), the result is 0, 2.
If the product lead chose GoBig (U) and the marketing lead chooses Reposition (X), the result is 1, 1.

If the product lead chose GoSlow (D) and the marketing lead chooses MajorCampaign (L), the result is 0, 2.
If the product lead chose GoSlow (D) and the marketing lead chooses MinimalCampaign (R), the result is 1, 3.

If the product lead chose GoSlow (D) and the marketing lead chooses Reposition (X), the product lead moves again.
The product lead then chooses between AcceptReposition (u) and RejectReposition (d).

GoSlow (D), Reposition (X), AcceptReposition (u) leads to 2, 1.
GoSlow (D), Reposition (X), RejectReposition (d) leads to 0, 1.
