V2 — Rescue Allocation

There are two people, a Rescue Lead and a Field Coordinator.
The Rescue Lead moves first and chooses between Route Alpha (A), Route Bravo (B), and Route Charlie (C).

If the Lead chooses Route Alpha (A), the Coordinator chooses between Left Action Alpha (LA) and Right Action Alpha (RA).
If the path is Route Alpha (A), Left Action Alpha (LA), the outcome gives reward 20 to the Lead and reward 35 to the Coordinator.
If the path is Route Alpha (A), Right Action Alpha (RA), the outcome gives reward 40 to the Lead and reward 15 to the Coordinator.

If the Lead chooses Route Bravo (B), the Coordinator chooses between Left Action Bravo (LB) and Right Action Bravo (RB).
If the path is Route Bravo (B), Left Action Bravo (LB), the outcome gives reward 10 to the Lead and reward 20 to the Coordinator.
If the path is Route Bravo (B), Right Action Bravo (RB), the outcome gives reward 90 to the Lead and reward 75 to the Coordinator.

If the Lead chooses Route Charlie (C), the Coordinator chooses between Left Action Charlie (LC) and Right Action Charlie (RC).
If the path is Route Charlie (C), Left Action Charlie (LC), the outcome gives reward 70 to the Lead and reward 50 to the Coordinator.
If the path is Route Charlie (C), Right Action Charlie (RC), the outcome gives reward 30 to the Lead and reward 50 to the Coordinator.
