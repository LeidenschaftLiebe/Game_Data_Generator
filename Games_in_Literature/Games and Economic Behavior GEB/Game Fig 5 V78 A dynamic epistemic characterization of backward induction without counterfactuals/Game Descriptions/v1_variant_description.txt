V1 — Harbor Deployment

A harbor chief is deciding how to organize a coastal operation.
The harbor chief moves first and chooses between Inspection Plan (a1) and Stand Down (b1).

If the harbor chief chooses Stand Down (b1), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the harbor chief,
reward 2 to the dock supervisor, and reward 0 to the marine analyst.

If the harbor chief chooses Inspection Plan (a1), the dock supervisor moves next and chooses between
Send Patrol Boat (a2) and Send Survey Team (b2).

If the dock supervisor chooses Send Patrol Boat (a2), the marine analyst then chooses between
Board Immediately (a3) and Shadow First (b3).
If the path is a1, a2, a3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the harbor chief, reward 1 to the dock supervisor,
and reward 0 to the marine analyst.
If the path is a1, a2, b3, the outcome gives reward 3 to the harbor chief, reward 3 to the dock supervisor,
and reward 1 to the marine analyst.

If the dock supervisor chooses Send Survey Team (b2), the marine analyst then chooses between
Surface Scan (c3) and Deep Scan (d3).
If the path is a1, b2, c3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the harbor chief, reward 2 to the dock supervisor,
and reward 1 to the marine analyst.
If the path is a1, b2, d3, the outcome gives reward 4 to the harbor chief, reward 0 to the dock supervisor,
and reward 0 to the marine analyst.
