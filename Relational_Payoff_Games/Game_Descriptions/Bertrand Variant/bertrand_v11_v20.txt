11) Solar Energy Providers

Two companies, BrightSun and SolarNova, supply identical rooftop solar panels.
Each firm sets a High or Low selling price.
If both choose High prices, they divide the market evenly and each earns solid profit.
If one lowers its price, that firm wins most customers and gains higher earnings, while the other earns little.
If both lower prices, their profits shrink as margins collapse.
