V4 — Security Response

A tech firm starts by choosing between Internal Fix (a) and Public Response (b).
If Internal Fix (a) is chosen, the compliance lead chooses between
Close Incident (c) and Escalate Review (d).

If Close Incident (c) is chosen, the matter ends with reward 2 to the firm,
reward 2 to compliance, reward 1 to the outside consultant, and reward 0 to the auditor.
If Escalate Review (d) is chosen, the auditor moves later.

If Public Response (b) is chosen first, the outside consultant chooses between
Escalate Review (e) and Publish Guidance (f).
If Publish Guidance (f) is chosen, the matter ends with reward 1 to the firm,
reward 0 to compliance, reward 2 to the outside consultant, and reward 0 to the auditor.

When the auditor moves, the auditor cannot tell whether the earlier path was
Internal Fix (a), Escalate Review (d) or Public Response (b), Escalate Review (e).
The auditor then chooses between Approve (g), Demand Changes (h), and Issue Warning (k).

If the path is Internal Fix (a), Escalate Review (d), Approve (g),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm, reward 1 to compliance,
reward 0 to the outside consultant, and reward 0 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Fix (a), Escalate Review (d), Demand Changes (h),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 3 to compliance,
reward 1 to the outside consultant, and reward 2 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Fix (a), Escalate Review (d), Issue Warning (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the firm, reward 4 to compliance,
reward 0 to the outside consultant, and reward 1 to the auditor.

If the path is Public Response (b), Escalate Review (e), Approve (g),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm, reward 2 to compliance,
reward 3 to the outside consultant, and reward 2 to the auditor.
If the path is Public Response (b), Escalate Review (e), Demand Changes (h),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm, reward 3 to compliance,
reward 1 to the outside consultant, and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is Public Response (b), Escalate Review (e), Issue Warning (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the firm, reward 3 to compliance,
reward 4 to the outside consultant, and reward 0 to the auditor.
