V4 [Platform chargeback case]

A merchant, a platform officer, and a customer are involved in a payment dispute.
The merchant first chooses between DropClaim (Out) and UseCustomerFunds (In).

If the merchant chooses DropClaim (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the merchant, 100 for the platform officer, and 30 for the customer.

If the merchant chooses UseCustomerFunds (In), 25 units are taken from the customer’s balance and handed to the platform officer.
The platform officer then chooses between PartialRelease (Left) and FullRelease (Right).

If the platform officer chooses PartialRelease (Left), the platform officer keeps 20 and sends 5 to the merchant.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the merchant receives 10.
The result is 180 for the merchant, 120 for the platform officer, and 5 for the customer.

If the platform officer chooses FullRelease (Right), the platform officer sends the full 25 to the merchant.
That amount is doubled, so the merchant receives 50.
The result is 220 for the merchant, 100 for the platform officer, and 5 for the customer.
