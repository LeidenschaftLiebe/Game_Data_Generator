V1 (space agency recruitment)

At the outset, a job candidate may be either high-aptitude or low-aptitude.
The high-aptitude type occurs with probability 1/3.
The low-aptitude type occurs with probability 2/3.
The candidate learns her own type.
The agency does not observe the candidate’s type.

The candidate then chooses whether to complete an advanced astronautics certification or not.
The agency observes whether the candidate completed the certification, but it does not observe her true aptitude.
After that, the agency chooses between assigning her to a mission-command role or a clerical support role.

If the candidate is high-aptitude and does not complete the certification, the agency chooses between the mission-command role and the clerical support role.
If the agency assigns the mission-command role there, the final reward is 10 for the candidate and 10 for the agency.
If the agency assigns the clerical support role there, the final reward is 4 for the candidate and 4 for the agency.

If the candidate is low-aptitude and does not complete the certification, the agency again chooses between the mission-command role and the clerical support role.
The agency cannot tell whether the no-certification signal came from the high-aptitude or low-aptitude candidate.
If the agency assigns the mission-command role there, the final reward is 10 for the candidate and 0 for the agency.
If the agency assigns the clerical support role there, the final reward is 4 for the candidate and 4 for the agency.

If the candidate is high-aptitude and completes the certification, the agency chooses between the mission-command role and the clerical support role.
If the agency assigns the mission-command role there, the final reward is 6 for the candidate and 10 for the agency.
If the agency assigns the clerical support role there, the final reward is 0 for the candidate and 4 for the agency.

If the candidate is low-aptitude and completes the certification, the agency again chooses between the mission-command role and the clerical support role.
The agency cannot tell whether the certification signal came from the high-aptitude or low-aptitude candidate.
If the agency assigns the mission-command role there, the final reward is 3 for the candidate and 0 for the agency.
If the agency assigns the clerical support role there, the final reward is -3 for the candidate and 4 for the agency.
