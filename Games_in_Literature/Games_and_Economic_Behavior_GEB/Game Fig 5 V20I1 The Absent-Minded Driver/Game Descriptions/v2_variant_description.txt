V2 — Awake Pilot or Drowsy Pilot

At START, the pilot chooses between AwakeMode and DrowsyMode.

Under AwakeMode, the pilot reaches BeaconX [X] first.
At BeaconX [X], the pilot lands with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or keeps flying with probability 4/7.

If the pilot keeps flying, the pilot reaches BeaconY [Y].
At BeaconY [Y], the pilot lands with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or keeps flying with probability 4/7 and gets 1.

Under DrowsyMode, the pilot reaches BeaconX [X] first.
At BeaconX [X], the pilot lands with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or keeps flying with probability 1/2.

If the pilot keeps flying, the pilot reaches BeaconY [Y].
At BeaconY [Y], the pilot lands with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or keeps flying with probability 1/2 and gets 1.

Under DrowsyMode, the pilot cannot tell whether the current beacon is BeaconX [X] or BeaconY [Y].
