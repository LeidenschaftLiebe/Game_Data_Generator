V3 [Smuggling pursuit]

Nature first chooses, with probability 1/2 each, which courier-order is used.

In the left setup, Courier A moves first and chooses between StayOnRoad and SlipOff.
If Courier A chooses SlipOff, then the Border Officer later chooses between RoadWatch and ExitTrap.
RoadWatch gives (1, 1, -1).
ExitTrap gives (0, 0, 0).

If Courier A chooses StayOnRoad, then Courier B chooses between StayOnRoadAgain and SlipOffLate.
SlipOffLate gives (0, 0, 0).
If Courier B chooses StayOnRoadAgain, the Border Officer chooses between RoadWatch and ExitTrap.
RoadWatch gives (0, 0, 0).
ExitTrap gives (1, 1, -1).

In the right setup, Courier B moves first and Courier A moves second in the mirrored version.
