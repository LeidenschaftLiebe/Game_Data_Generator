V0 (Microsoft and Celera, closest to the prompt)

At the outset, Microsoft may be one of two types, Friend or Enemy, with probability 0.5 for each.
Microsoft knows its own type.
Celera knows only that the two types are equally likely.

If Microsoft is Friend, it chooses whether to Buy more Celera stock or Sell what it is holding.
Celera observes whether Microsoft chose Buy or Sell, but Celera does not observe whether Microsoft is Friend or Enemy.
If Microsoft is Friend and chooses Buy, then Celera chooses whether to Reveal or Hide its trade secrets.
If Celera reveals after seeing Buy from a Friend, the final reward is 15 for Microsoft and 12 for Celera.
If Celera hides after seeing Buy from a Friend, the final reward is -1 for Microsoft and 1 for Celera.

If Microsoft is Friend and chooses Sell, then Celera again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If Celera reveals after seeing Sell from a Friend, the final reward is 5 for Microsoft and 6 for Celera.
If Celera hides after seeing Sell from a Friend, the final reward is 5 for Microsoft and -1 for Celera.

If Microsoft is Enemy, it also chooses whether to Buy more stock or Sell what it is holding.
If Microsoft is Enemy and chooses Buy, then Celera chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If Celera reveals after seeing Buy from an Enemy, the final reward is 5 for Microsoft and -6 for Celera.
If Celera hides after seeing Buy from an Enemy, the final reward is 5 for Microsoft and 1 for Celera.

If Microsoft is Enemy and chooses Sell, then Celera again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If Celera reveals after seeing Sell from an Enemy, the final reward is 15 for Microsoft and -14 for Celera.
If Celera hides after seeing Sell from an Enemy, the final reward is 0 for Microsoft and -1 for Celera.
