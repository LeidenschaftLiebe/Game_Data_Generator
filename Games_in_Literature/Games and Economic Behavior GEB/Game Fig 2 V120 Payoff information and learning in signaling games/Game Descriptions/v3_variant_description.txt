V3 [Volcano evacuation]

A hidden condition first determines whether the field commander is CalmCommander or UneasyCommander.
CalmCommander happens with probability 9/10, and UneasyCommander happens with probability 1/10.

The commander then chooses between NorthRoad and SouthRoad.

After seeing that route call, the checkpoint officer chooses between Stop and LetPass.

If the commander is CalmCommander and chooses NorthRoad, then Stop gives 0 to the commander, and 0 to the officer.
If the commander is CalmCommander and chooses NorthRoad, then LetPass gives 2 to the commander, and 1 to the officer.

If the commander is CalmCommander and chooses SouthRoad, then Stop gives 1 to the commander, and 0 to the officer.
If the commander is CalmCommander and chooses SouthRoad, then LetPass gives 3 to the commander, and 1 to the officer.

If the commander is UneasyCommander and chooses NorthRoad, then Stop gives 1 to the commander, and 1 to the officer.
If the commander is UneasyCommander and chooses NorthRoad, then LetPass gives 3 to the commander, and 0 to the officer.

If the commander is UneasyCommander and chooses SouthRoad, then Stop gives 0 to the commander, and 1 to the officer.
If the commander is UneasyCommander and chooses SouthRoad, then LetPass gives 2 to the commander, and 0 to the officer.
