V2 — Film Release Strategy

Alice is a producer deciding what to do with a film.
She first chooses IndieRelease [l], StudioRevision [m], or ShelveFilm [r].

If Alice chooses StudioRevision [m] or ShelveFilm [r], the project goes to Bob, the distributor.
Bob does not know which of those two choices Alice made.
Bob then chooses PushRelease [L] or HoldBack [R].

If Alice chooses IndieRelease [l], the film goes to Emma, the festival planner.
If Alice chooses StudioRevision [m] and Bob chooses PushRelease [L], the film also goes to Emma.

When Emma becomes involved, she cannot tell whether she is handling the original indie version or a revised version that Bob pushed forward.
Emma then chooses MorningScreening [p] or EveningScreening [q].

If Alice chose IndieRelease [l], then whether Emma chooses MorningScreening [p] or EveningScreening [q], Alice gets 0, Bob gets 2, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose StudioRevision [m] and Bob chose PushRelease [L], then whether Emma chooses MorningScreening [p] or EveningScreening [q], 
Alice gets 2, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose StudioRevision [m] and Bob chose HoldBack [R], then Alice gets 1, Bob gets 1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose ShelveFilm [r] and Bob chose PushRelease [L], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets -1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose ShelveFilm [r] and Bob chose HoldBack [R], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.
