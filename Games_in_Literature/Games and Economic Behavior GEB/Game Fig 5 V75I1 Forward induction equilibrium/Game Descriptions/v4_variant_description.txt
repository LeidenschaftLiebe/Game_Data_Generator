V4 — Emergency Broadcast

There are three sides in a public warning system: a central director, a station editor, and a field monitor.
The central director moves first and chooses between Issue Standard Notice (Out), Top Alert (T), and Bottom Alert (B).

If the central director chooses Issue Standard Notice (Out), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to each side.

If the central director chooses Top Alert (T) or Bottom Alert (B), the station editor moves next and chooses
between Left Channel (L) and Right Channel (R).
The station editor cannot tell whether the earlier alert was Top Alert (T) or Bottom Alert (B).

After that, the field monitor moves and chooses between Urgent Tag (U), Moderate Tag (M), and Delayed Tag (D).
The field monitor does not know the earlier choices.

If the path is Top Alert (T), Left Channel (L), Urgent Tag (U),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the central director, reward 1 to the station editor, and reward 1 to the field monitor.
If the path is Top Alert (T), Left Channel (L), Moderate Tag (M),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 0 to the station editor, and reward -1 to the field monitor.
If the path is Top Alert (T), Left Channel (L), Delayed Tag (D),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 2 to the station editor, and reward 0 to the field monitor.

If the path is Top Alert (T), Right Channel (R), Urgent Tag (U),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 0 to the station editor, and reward 1 to the field monitor.
If the path is Top Alert (T), Right Channel (R), Moderate Tag (M),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 1 to the station editor, and reward -1 to the field monitor.
If the path is Top Alert (T), Right Channel (R), Delayed Tag (D),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 3 to the station editor, and reward 0 to the field monitor.

If the path is Bottom Alert (B), Left Channel (L), Urgent Tag (U),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 1 to the station editor, and reward 0 to the field monitor.
If the path is Bottom Alert (B), Left Channel (L), Moderate Tag (M),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 0 to the station editor, and reward -1 to the field monitor.
If the path is Bottom Alert (B), Left Channel (L), Delayed Tag (D),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 2 to the station editor, and reward 1 to the field monitor.

If the path is Bottom Alert (B), Right Channel (R), Urgent Tag (U),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 0 to the station editor, and reward 0 to the field monitor.
If the path is Bottom Alert (B), Right Channel (R), Moderate Tag (M),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the central director, reward 1 to the station editor, and reward -1 to the field monitor.
If the path is Bottom Alert (B), Right Channel (R), Delayed Tag (D),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the central director, reward 1 to the station editor, and reward 1 to the field monitor.
