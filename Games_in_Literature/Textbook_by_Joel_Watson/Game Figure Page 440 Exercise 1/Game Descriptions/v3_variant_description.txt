V3 (sports locker challenge)

Before a locker-room game begins, one player hides a golden ticket in Locker A or Locker B.
The referee sees where it is placed.
Another player then enters, and the referee says either “Red” or “Green.”
After hearing that, the player picks one locker.
If the player opens the correct locker, the player gets 100 and the hider gets 0.
If the player opens the wrong locker, the hider gets 100 and the player gets 0.
The referee gets 10 whenever Locker A is chosen and 0 whenever Locker B is chosen.

If the ticket is hidden in Locker A and the referee says Red, the player’s choice settles the outcome.
Choosing Locker A gives final rewards of 0 for the hider, 10 for the referee, and 100 for the chooser.
Choosing Locker B gives final rewards of 100 for the hider, 0 for the referee, and 0 for the chooser.

If the ticket is hidden in Locker A and the referee says Green, the same two locker choices remain.
Choosing Locker A gives final rewards of 0 for the hider, 10 for the referee, and 100 for the chooser.
Choosing Locker B gives final rewards of 100 for the hider, 0 for the referee, and 0 for the chooser.

If the ticket is hidden in Locker B and the referee says Red, the player chooses between Locker A and Locker B.
Choosing Locker A gives final rewards of 100 for the hider, 10 for the referee, and 0 for the chooser.
Choosing Locker B gives final rewards of 0 for the hider, 0 for the referee, and 100 for the chooser.

If the ticket is hidden in Locker B and the referee says Green, the player again chooses between the two lockers.
Choosing Locker A gives final rewards of 100 for the hider, 10 for the referee, and 0 for the chooser.
Choosing Locker B gives final rewards of 0 for the hider, 0 for the referee, and 100 for the chooser.
