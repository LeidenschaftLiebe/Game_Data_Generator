V0 (closest to the book)

The value of x is private to Player 1.
With probability 2/3, x equals 12.
With probability 1/3, x equals 0.
Player 1 observes this value before making a choice.
Player 2 does not observe x and does not observe Player 1’s action before making a choice.

If x equals 12, then Player 1 chooses between A and B.
If Player 1 chooses A and Player 2 chooses C, the final reward is 12 for Player 1 and 9 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses A and Player 2 chooses D, the final reward is 3 for Player 1 and 6 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses B and Player 2 chooses C, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses B and Player 2 chooses D, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and 9 for Player 2.

If x equals 0, then Player 1 chooses between A and B.
If Player 1 chooses A and Player 2 chooses C, the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 9 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses A and Player 2 chooses D, the final reward is 3 for Player 1 and 6 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses B and Player 2 chooses C, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses B and Player 2 chooses D, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and 9 for Player 2.
