V5 (fashion brand expansion)

A fashion brand first chooses between strategy U and strategy D.
The competing brand then chooses between response A and response B without knowing which strategy the first brand selected.

If the competing brand chooses response A after strategy U, the game ends with 3 for the first brand and 3 for the competitor.

If the competing brand chooses response B after strategy U, the first brand then chooses between follow-up option X and follow-up option Y.
Follow-up option X gives a final reward of 5 to the first brand and 1 to the competitor.
Follow-up option Y gives a final reward of 3 to the first brand and 6 to the competitor.

If the competing brand chooses response A after strategy D, the first brand then chooses between follow-up option W and follow-up option Z.
Follow-up option W gives a final reward of 4 to the first brand and 2 to the competitor.
Follow-up option Z gives a final reward of 9 to the first brand and 0 to the competitor.

If the competing brand chooses response B after strategy D, the game ends with 2 for the first brand and 2 for the competitor.
