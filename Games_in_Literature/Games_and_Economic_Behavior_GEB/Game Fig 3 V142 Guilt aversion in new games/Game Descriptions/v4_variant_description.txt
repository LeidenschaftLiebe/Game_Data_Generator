V4 [Platform customer credit]

A merchant, a platform officer, and a customer are involved in a transfer decision.
The merchant first chooses between DropCase (Out) and ReleaseCredit (In).

If the merchant chooses DropCase (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the merchant, 100 for the platform officer, and 30 for the customer.

If the merchant chooses ReleaseCredit (In), 25 units from the merchant’s own side are handed to the platform officer.
The platform officer then chooses between SmallCredit (Left) and FullCredit (Right).

If the platform officer chooses SmallCredit (Left), the platform officer keeps 20 and sends 5 to the customer.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the customer receives 10.
The result is 145 for the merchant, 120 for the platform officer, and 40 for the customer.

If the platform officer chooses FullCredit (Right), the platform officer sends the full 25 to the customer.
That amount is doubled, so the customer receives 50.
The result is 145 for the merchant, 100 for the platform officer, and 80 for the customer.
