V2 [Mountain rescue]

Weather conditions first determine whether the rescue team is WellEquipped or UnderEquipped.
WellEquipped happens with probability 9/10, and UnderEquipped happens with probability 1/10.

The rescue leader then chooses between HoldPosition and PushForward.

If the rescue leader chooses HoldPosition, the effort ends there.
This gives 0 to the rescue leader, and 0 to the spotter.

If the rescue leader chooses PushForward, the spotter chooses between GuideUp, GuideDown, and SideTraverse.
The spotter cannot tell whether the team is WellEquipped or UnderEquipped.

If the team is WellEquipped and the spotter chooses GuideUp, this gives 2 to the rescue leader, and 1 to the spotter.
If the team is WellEquipped and the spotter chooses GuideDown, this gives -1 to the rescue leader, and 0 to the spotter.
If the team is WellEquipped and the spotter chooses SideTraverse, this gives 1 to the rescue leader, and -1 to the spotter.

If the team is UnderEquipped and the spotter chooses GuideUp, this gives 1 to the rescue leader, and 0 to the spotter.
If the team is UnderEquipped and the spotter chooses GuideDown, this gives -1 to the rescue leader, and 1 to the spotter.
If the team is UnderEquipped and the spotter chooses SideTraverse, this gives 3 to the rescue leader, and -1 to the spotter.
