V1 [Volcano evacuation]

A mayor and an evacuation chief are deciding how to respond to a fast-moving volcano threat.

The mayor moves first and chooses between TownShelter (T) and MountainRoute (B).

If the mayor chooses TownShelter (T), then the evacuation chief chooses between UpperPass (U) and DeepPass (D).
If the evacuation chief chooses UpperPass (U), this gives 2 to the mayor, and 2 to the evacuation chief.
If the evacuation chief chooses DeepPass (D), this gives 0 to the mayor, and 0 to the evacuation chief.

If the mayor chooses MountainRoute (B), then the evacuation chief chooses between LeftRoad (L) and RightRoad (R).

If the evacuation chief chooses RightRoad (R), the operation ends there.
This gives 1 to the mayor, and 1 to the evacuation chief.

If the evacuation chief chooses LeftRoad (L), the evacuation chief later chooses between UpperPass (U) and DeepPass (D).
If the evacuation chief chooses UpperPass (U), this gives 0 to the mayor, and 0 to the evacuation chief.
If the evacuation chief chooses DeepPass (D), this gives 3 to the mayor, and 3 to the evacuation chief.

The two later UpperPass (U) or DeepPass (D) decision nodes are linked.
So at that later choice, the evacuation chief cannot tell whether the path came directly after TownShelter (T), 
or after MountainRoute (B) and then LeftRoad (L).
