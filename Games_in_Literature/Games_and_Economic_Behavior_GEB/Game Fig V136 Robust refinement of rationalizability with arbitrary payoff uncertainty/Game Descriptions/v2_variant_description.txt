V2 [Research project]

A lead researcher moves first and chooses between UpgradePlan (U) and DoLess (D).

Then a coauthor responds, but the coauthor cannot tell whether the lead researcher upgraded the plan or did less.
The coauthor chooses between FullSupport (L), LimitedSupport (R), and SuggestRedesign (X).

If the lead researcher chose UpgradePlan (U) and the coauthor chooses FullSupport (L), the result is 1, 3.
If the lead researcher chose UpgradePlan (U) and the coauthor chooses LimitedSupport (R), the result is 0, 2.
If the lead researcher chose UpgradePlan (U) and the coauthor chooses SuggestRedesign (X), the result is 1, 1.

If the lead researcher chose DoLess (D) and the coauthor chooses FullSupport (L), the result is 0, 2.
If the lead researcher chose DoLess (D) and the coauthor chooses LimitedSupport (R), the result is 1, 3.

If the lead researcher chose DoLess (D) and the coauthor chooses SuggestRedesign (X), the lead researcher moves again.
The lead researcher then chooses between AdoptRedesign (u) and KeepOldPlan (d).

DoLess (D), SuggestRedesign (X), AdoptRedesign (u) leads to 2, 1.
DoLess (D), SuggestRedesign (X), KeepOldPlan (d) leads to 0, 1.
