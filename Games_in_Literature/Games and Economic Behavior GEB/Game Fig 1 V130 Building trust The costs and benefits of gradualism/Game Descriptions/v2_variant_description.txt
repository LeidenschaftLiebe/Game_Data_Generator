V2 [Supplier relationship]

A retailer is deciding how much business to entrust to a new supplier.

The retailer moves first and chooses one of four levels:
NoOrder (N), TrialOrder (L), RegularOrder (M), or MajorOrder (H).

If the retailer chooses NoOrder (N), this gives the retailer 24, and the supplier 15.

If the retailer chooses TrialOrder (L), the supplier chooses between Fulfill (R) and CutCorners (D).
If the supplier chooses Fulfill (R), this gives the retailer 32, and the supplier 22.
If the supplier chooses CutCorners (D), this gives the retailer 20, and the supplier 42.

If the retailer chooses RegularOrder (M), the supplier again chooses between Fulfill (R) and CutCorners (D).
If the supplier chooses Fulfill (R), this gives the retailer 44, and the supplier 36.
If the supplier chooses CutCorners (D), this gives the retailer 12, and the supplier 72.

If the retailer chooses MajorOrder (H), the supplier again chooses between Fulfill (R) and CutCorners (D).
If the supplier chooses Fulfill (R), this gives the retailer 56, and the supplier 42.
If the supplier chooses CutCorners (D), this gives the retailer 0, and the supplier 80.
