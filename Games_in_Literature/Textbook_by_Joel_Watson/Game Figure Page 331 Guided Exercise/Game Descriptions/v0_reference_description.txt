V0 (closest to the tree and context)

At the outset, the incumbent may be a high-cost firm or a low-cost firm, with probability 0.5 for each case.
The incumbent learns its own cost type before choosing a price.
The entrant does not know the incumbent’s type.
The entrant only sees which of the two price options was posted.

If the incumbent is the high-cost type, it chooses between price option 1 and price option 2.
If the high-cost incumbent chooses price option 1, the entrant then decides whether to enter or stay out.
If the entrant enters after seeing price option 1 from the high-cost incumbent, the final reward is 0 for the incumbent and 1 for the entrant.
If the entrant stays out after seeing price option 1 from the high-cost incumbent, the final reward is 0 for the incumbent and 0 for the entrant.

If the high-cost incumbent chooses price option 2, the entrant again decides whether to enter or stay out.
If the entrant enters after seeing price option 2 from the high-cost incumbent, the final reward is 0 for the incumbent and 1 for the entrant.
If the entrant stays out after seeing price option 2 from the high-cost incumbent, the final reward is 2 for the incumbent and 0 for the entrant.

If the incumbent is the low-cost type, it also chooses between price option 1 and price option 2.
If the low-cost incumbent chooses price option 1, the entrant then decides whether to enter or stay out.
If the entrant enters after seeing price option 1 from the low-cost incumbent, the final reward is 0 for the incumbent and -1 for the entrant.
If the entrant stays out after seeing price option 1 from the low-cost incumbent, the final reward is 2 for the incumbent and 0 for the entrant.

If the low-cost incumbent chooses price option 2, the entrant again decides whether to enter or stay out.
If the entrant enters after seeing price option 2 from the low-cost incumbent, the final reward is 0 for the incumbent and -1 for the entrant.
If the entrant stays out after seeing price option 2 from the low-cost incumbent, the final reward is 4 for the incumbent and 0 for the entrant.
