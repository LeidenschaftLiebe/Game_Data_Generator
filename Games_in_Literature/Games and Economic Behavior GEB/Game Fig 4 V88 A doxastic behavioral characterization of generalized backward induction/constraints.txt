Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:
1) Check the move order: Player 1 moves first; after b or c, Player 2 moves; then Player 3 moves last.
2) Check that Player 2's two decision nodes are in the same information set, 
and that Player 3's four decision nodes are in the same information set.
3) Check the anchor payoff: the outcome after c -> e -> t is (3, 0, 1).
