V5 [Academic presentation]

A researcher’s preparation level is either Strong (s) with probability 0.9 or Weak (w) with probability 0.1.

After learning that privately, the researcher chooses between BoldPresentation (B) and QuietPresentation (Q).
An evaluator observes whether the presentation was bold or quiet, but does not know the true preparation level.

If the researcher chooses BoldPresentation (B) after Strong (s), the evaluator chooses between NoProbe (NF) and Probe (F).
NoProbe (NF) gives 3 to the researcher and 1 to the evaluator.
Probe (F) gives 1 to the researcher and 0 to the evaluator.

If the researcher chooses BoldPresentation (B) after Weak (w), NoProbe (NF) gives 2 and 0,
while Probe (F) gives 0 and 1.

If the researcher chooses QuietPresentation (Q) after Strong (s), NoProbe (NF) gives 2 and 1,
while Probe (F) gives 0 and 0.

If the researcher chooses QuietPresentation (Q) after Weak (w), NoProbe (NF) gives 3 and 0,
while Probe (F) gives 1 and 1.
