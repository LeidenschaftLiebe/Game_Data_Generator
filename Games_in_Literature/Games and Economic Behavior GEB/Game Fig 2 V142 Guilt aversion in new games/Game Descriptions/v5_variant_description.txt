V5 [Club budget raid]

A club president, a treasurer, and a member are involved in a resource decision.
The president first chooses between DoNothing (Out) and UseMemberFunds (In).

If the president chooses DoNothing (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the president, 100 for the treasurer, and 30 for the member.

If the president chooses UseMemberFunds (In), 25 units are taken from the member’s funds and handed to the treasurer.
The treasurer then chooses between SmallReturn (Left) and LargeReturn (Right).

If the treasurer chooses SmallReturn (Left), the treasurer keeps 20 and sends 5 to the president.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the president receives 10.
The result is 180 for the president, 120 for the treasurer, and 5 for the member.

If the treasurer chooses LargeReturn (Right), the treasurer sends the full 25 to the president.
That amount is doubled, so the president receives 50.
The result is 220 for the president, 100 for the treasurer, and 5 for the member.
