V5 — Optional Museum Exhibit Theme

The museum manager moves first and chooses Wait [No] or Announce [Yes].

After that, the curator chooses OldMasters [O] or ModernArt [B].

Then the museum manager chooses OldMasters [O] or ModernArt [B].
At this later move, the museum manager knows whether the first move was Wait [No] or Announce [Yes], but cannot observe which theme the curator chose.

If the first move was Wait [No] and both later choices are OldMasters [O], the museum manager gets 2 and the curator gets 1.
If the first move was Wait [No] and both later choices are ModernArt [B], the museum manager gets 1 and the curator gets 2.
If the first move was Wait [No] and the later choices do not match, both get 0.

If the first move was Announce [Yes] and both later choices are OldMasters [O], the museum manager gets 2 and the curator gets 1.
If the first move was Announce [Yes] and both later choices are ModernArt [B], the museum manager gets 1 and the curator gets 2.
If the first move was Announce [Yes] and the later choices do not match, both get 0.
