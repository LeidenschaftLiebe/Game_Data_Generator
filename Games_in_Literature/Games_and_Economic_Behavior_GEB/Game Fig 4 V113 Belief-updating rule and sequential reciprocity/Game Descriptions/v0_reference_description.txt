V0 — Delegation setting from the article

Three people are involved in a delegation-style decision. Player 1 moves first.

Player 1 can keep the matter simple by choosing A, and the interaction ends with reward 5 to each player.

Or Player 1 can choose B and pass the situation forward to Player 2.

If Player 2 then chooses C, the process stops there.
It ends with reward 7 to Player 1, reward 9 to Player 2, and a loss of 3 to Player 3.

If Player 2 instead chooses D, control returns to Player 1.

At that point, Player 1 can choose E or F.

If Player 1 chooses E, the interaction ends with reward 8 to Player 1, reward 5 to Player 2,
and reward 0 to Player 3.

If Player 1 chooses F, Player 3 gets the final say.

Player 3 can then choose G or H.

If Player 3 chooses G, the interaction ends with reward 11 to Player 1, reward 11 to Player 2,
and reward 9 to Player 3.

If Player 3 chooses H, the interaction ends with reward 2 to Player 1, reward 7 to Player 2,
and reward 10 to Player 3.
