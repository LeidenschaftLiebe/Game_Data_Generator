V4 — Robot Navigation with Repeating Checkpoints

A robot first reaches a checkpoint and chooses LeftRoute [L] or RightRoute [R].

If the robot chooses RightRoute [R], the run ends immediately with reward 0.

If the robot chooses LeftRoute [L], it reaches another checkpoint that appears identical to the first one.
There, it again chooses LeftRoute [L] or RightRoute [R].

After that, the robot reaches a later checkpoint, but cannot tell which of two possible later checkpoints it is at.

At one later checkpoint, choosing LeftMode [L] gives reward 1, while RightMode [R] gives reward 0.

At the other later checkpoint, choosing LeftMode [L] gives reward 0, while RightMode [R] gives reward 3.
