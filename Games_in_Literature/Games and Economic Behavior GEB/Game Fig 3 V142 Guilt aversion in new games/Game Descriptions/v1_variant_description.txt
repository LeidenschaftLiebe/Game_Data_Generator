V1 [Startup donation redirect]

A founder, an investor, and a beneficiary are involved in a transfer decision.
The founder moves first and chooses between StayOut (Out) and ReleaseFunds (In).

If the founder chooses StayOut (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the founder, 100 for the investor, and 30 for the beneficiary.

If the founder chooses ReleaseFunds (In), 25 units come out of the founder’s own resources and are handed to the investor.
The investor then chooses between SmallDonation (Left) and FullDonation (Right).

If the investor chooses SmallDonation (Left), the investor keeps 20 and sends 5 to the beneficiary.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the beneficiary receives 10.
The result is 145 for the founder, 120 for the investor, and 40 for the beneficiary.

If the investor chooses FullDonation (Right), the investor sends the full 25 to the beneficiary.
That amount is doubled, so the beneficiary receives 50.
The result is 145 for the founder, 100 for the investor, and 80 for the beneficiary.
