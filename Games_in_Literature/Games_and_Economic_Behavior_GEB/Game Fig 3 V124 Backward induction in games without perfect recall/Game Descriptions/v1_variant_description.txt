V1 [Cave expedition]

A team leader and a navigator are deciding how to handle a cave expedition.

The team leader moves first and chooses between TakeShortcut (T) and GoDeeper (B).

If the team leader chooses TakeShortcut (T), the expedition ends there.
This gives the team leader 2, and the navigator 6.

If the team leader chooses GoDeeper (B), the navigator chooses between UpperTunnel (U) and LowerTunnel (D).

After that, the navigator later chooses between LeftPassage (L) and RightPassage (R),
but at that later choice the navigator cannot tell whether the earlier tunnel choice was UpperTunnel (U) or LowerTunnel (D).

If the earlier choice was UpperTunnel (U), then LeftPassage (L) gives the team leader 4, and the navigator 4,
while RightPassage (R) gives the team leader 0, and the navigator 0.

If the earlier choice was LowerTunnel (D), then LeftPassage (L) gives the team leader 0, and the navigator 0,
while RightPassage (R) gives the team leader 1, and the navigator 1.
