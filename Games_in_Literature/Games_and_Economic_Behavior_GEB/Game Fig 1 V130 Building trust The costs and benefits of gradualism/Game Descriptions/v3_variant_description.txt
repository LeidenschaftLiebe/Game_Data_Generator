V3 [Research collaboration]

A lead researcher is deciding how much of a project to share with a new collaborator.

The lead researcher moves first and chooses one of four levels:
WorkAlone (N), ShareSmallTask (L), ShareLargeTask (M), or ShareCoreProject (H).

If the lead researcher chooses WorkAlone (N), this gives the lead researcher 24, and the collaborator 15.

If the lead researcher chooses ShareSmallTask (L), the collaborator chooses between ContributeHonestly (R) and FreeRide (D).
If the collaborator chooses ContributeHonestly (R), this gives the lead researcher 32, and the collaborator 22.
If the collaborator chooses FreeRide (D), this gives the lead researcher 20, and the collaborator 42.

If the lead researcher chooses ShareLargeTask (M), the collaborator again chooses between ContributeHonestly (R) and FreeRide (D).
If the collaborator chooses ContributeHonestly (R), this gives the lead researcher 44, and the collaborator 36.
If the collaborator chooses FreeRide (D), this gives the lead researcher 12, and the collaborator 72.

If the lead researcher chooses ShareCoreProject (H), the collaborator again chooses between ContributeHonestly (R) and FreeRide (D).
If the collaborator chooses ContributeHonestly (R), this gives the lead researcher 56, and the collaborator 42.
If the collaborator chooses FreeRide (D), this gives the lead researcher 0, and the collaborator 80.
