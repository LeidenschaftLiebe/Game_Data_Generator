Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:
1) Check the move order: Player 1 moves first; if Player 1 chooses the L or R action, Player 2 moves next; then Player 1 moves again.
2) Check that Player 2's two decision nodes are in the same information set.
3) Check the anchor payoff: the outcome after R -> A -> G is (3, 5).
