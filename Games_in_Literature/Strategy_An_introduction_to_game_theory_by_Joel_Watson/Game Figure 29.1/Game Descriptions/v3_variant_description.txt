V3 (museum curator hiring)

At the outset, a candidate may be either a high-skill curator or a low-skill curator.
The high-skill type occurs with probability 1/3.
The low-skill type occurs with probability 2/3.
The candidate learns her own type.
The museum does not observe the candidate’s type.

The candidate then chooses whether to complete a costly advanced heritage fellowship or not.
The museum observes whether the candidate completed the fellowship, but it does not observe her true skill.
After that, the museum chooses between assigning her to a flagship curatorial role or a clerical archive role.

If the candidate is high-skill and does not complete the fellowship, the museum chooses between the flagship curatorial role and the clerical archive role.
If the museum assigns the flagship curatorial role there, the final reward is 10 for the candidate and 10 for the museum.
If the museum assigns the clerical archive role there, the final reward is 4 for the candidate and 4 for the museum.

If the candidate is low-skill and does not complete the fellowship, the museum again chooses between the flagship curatorial role and the clerical archive role.
The museum cannot tell whether the no-fellowship signal came from the high-skill or low-skill candidate.
If the museum assigns the flagship curatorial role there, the final reward is 10 for the candidate and 0 for the museum.
If the museum assigns the clerical archive role there, the final reward is 4 for the candidate and 4 for the museum.

If the candidate is high-skill and completes the fellowship, the museum chooses between the flagship curatorial role and the clerical archive role.
If the museum assigns the flagship curatorial role there, the final reward is 6 for the candidate and 10 for the museum.
If the museum assigns the clerical archive role there, the final reward is 0 for the candidate and 4 for the museum.

If the candidate is low-skill and completes the fellowship, the museum again chooses between the flagship curatorial role and the clerical archive role.
The museum cannot tell whether the fellowship signal came from the high-skill or low-skill candidate.
If the museum assigns the flagship curatorial role there, the final reward is 3 for the candidate and 0 for the museum.
If the museum assigns the clerical archive role there, the final reward is -3 for the candidate and 4 for the museum.
