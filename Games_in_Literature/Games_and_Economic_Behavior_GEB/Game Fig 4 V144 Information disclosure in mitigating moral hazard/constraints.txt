Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints

Chance first chooses between the easy task and the difficult task with equal probability.
The principal moves after learning the task difficulty and chooses between no disclosure and immediate disclosure.
Under no disclosure, the agent’s entry decision and later effort decision do not reveal that the agent has learned the task difficulty.
Seed payoff constraint: after the easy task, then immediate disclosure, then IN, then LOW, the payoffs are 50 and 35.
