V5 [Ancient tunnel escape]

Nature first chooses, with probability 1/2 each, which explorer-order is used.

In the left setup, Explorer A moves first and chooses between GoDeeper and TakePassage.
If Explorer A chooses TakePassage, then the Guard Captain later chooses between WatchTunnel and GuardPassage.
WatchTunnel gives (1, 1, -1).
GuardPassage gives (0, 0, 0).

If Explorer A chooses GoDeeper, then Explorer B chooses between GoDeeperAgain and TakeLatePassage.
TakeLatePassage gives (0, 0, 0).
If Explorer B chooses GoDeeperAgain, the Guard Captain chooses between WatchTunnel and GuardPassage.
WatchTunnel gives (0, 0, 0).
GuardPassage gives (1, 1, -1).

In the right setup, Explorer B moves first and Explorer A moves second in the mirrored version.
