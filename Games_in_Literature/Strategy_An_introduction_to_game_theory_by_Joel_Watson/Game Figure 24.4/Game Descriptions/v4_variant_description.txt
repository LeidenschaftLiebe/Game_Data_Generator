V4 (space salvage mission)

It is unknown that whether a drifting wreck contains valuable salvage or almost nothing worth recovering.
With probability 2/3, the salvage value is 12.
With probability 1/3, the salvage value is 0.
The salvage captain learns this hidden value before choosing a recovery plan.
The funding agency does not observe the salvage value and does not observe the captain’s plan before choosing how to support the mission.

If the salvage value is 12 and the captain chooses plan A while the agency chooses support level C, the final reward is 12 for the captain and 9 for the agency.
If the salvage value is 12 and the captain chooses plan A while the agency chooses support level D, the final reward is 3 for the captain and 6 for the agency.
If the salvage value is 12 and the captain chooses plan B while the agency chooses support level C, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 0 for the agency.
If the salvage value is 12 and the captain chooses plan B while the agency chooses support level D, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 9 for the agency.

If the salvage value is 0 and the captain chooses plan A while the agency chooses support level C, the final reward is 0 for the captain and 9 for the agency.
If the salvage value is 0 and the captain chooses plan A while the agency chooses support level D, the final reward is 3 for the captain and 6 for the agency.
If the salvage value is 0 and the captain chooses plan B while the agency chooses support level C, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 0 for the agency.
If the salvage value is 0 and the captain chooses plan B while the agency chooses support level D, the final reward is 6 for the captain and 9 for the agency.
