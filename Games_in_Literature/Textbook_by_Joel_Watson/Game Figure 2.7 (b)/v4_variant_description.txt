V4 (airlines on the same route)

Two airlines are pricing tickets on the same route.
Each airline must choose its fare without observing the other airline’s fare in advance.
If both airlines keep fares high, each earns 1 million.
If Airline 1 keeps a high fare while Airline 2 cuts fares, Airline 1 earns nothing and Airline 2 earns 2 million.
If Airline 1 cuts fares while Airline 2 stays high, Airline 1 earns 2 million and Airline 2 earns nothing.
If both cut fares, profits are squeezed and each airline earns 0.5 million.
