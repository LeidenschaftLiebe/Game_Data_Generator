V1 [Startup partnership]

A startup founder moves first and chooses between ShareTech (C) and KeepPrivate (D).
Then an outside partner observes that choice and chooses between ShareDistribution (c) and HoldBack (d).

If the founder chooses ShareTech (C) and the partner chooses ShareDistribution (c), the result is 2, 2.
If the founder chooses ShareTech (C) and the partner chooses HoldBack (d), the result is 0, 3.

If the founder chooses KeepPrivate (D) and the partner chooses ShareDistribution (c), the result is 3, 0.
If the founder chooses KeepPrivate (D) and the partner chooses HoldBack (d), the result is 1, 1.
