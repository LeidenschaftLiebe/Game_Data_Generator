V5 [Grant application]

A researcher and a committee member are handling a grant application.
The researcher first chooses between Route A, Route B, and Drop Out.
If the researcher drops out, the process ends immediately.
The researcher gets 1 and the committee member gets 0.

If the researcher chooses Route A or Route B,
the committee member then chooses between Full Panel (d), Early Decision (e), and Standard Decision (f),
without knowing which route was taken.

If the committee member chooses Early Decision (e), the process ends immediately.
After Route A then Early Decision, the researcher gets 0 and the committee member gets 5.
After Route B then Early Decision, the researcher gets 0 and the committee member gets 4.

If the committee member chooses Standard Decision (f), the process also ends immediately.
After Route A then Standard Decision, the researcher gets 1 and the committee member gets 4.
After Route B then Standard Decision, the researcher gets 1 and the committee member gets 5.

If the committee member chooses Full Panel (d),
the committee member later chooses between Fund (g) and Decline (h),
still without knowing whether the application came from Route A or Route B.

After Route A then Full Panel then Fund, the researcher gets 2 and the committee member gets 1.
After Route A then Full Panel then Decline, both get 0.
After Route B then Full Panel then Fund, the researcher gets 2 and the committee member gets 0.
After Route B then Full Panel then Decline, the researcher gets 0 and the committee member gets 3.
