V3 — Casting Memo

There are two players, a Casting Director and a Producer.
At the start, chance selects one of two hidden performer profiles: Profile A or Profile B.

The Casting Director learns which profile was selected.
The Casting Director then sends either Memo A (A) or Memo B (B).

After seeing the memo, the Producer chooses between Sign Now (U) and Pass for Now (D).

If the true profile is A and the Producer chooses Sign Now (U),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the Casting Director and reward 1 to the Producer.
If the true profile is A and the Producer chooses Pass for Now (D),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the Casting Director and reward 2 to the Producer.

If the true profile is B and the Producer chooses Sign Now (U),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the Casting Director and reward 2 to the Producer.
If the true profile is B and the Producer chooses Pass for Now (D),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the Casting Director and reward 1 to the Producer.
