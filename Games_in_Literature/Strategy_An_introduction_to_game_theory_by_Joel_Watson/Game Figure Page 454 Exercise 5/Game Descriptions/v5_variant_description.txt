V5 (cloud credit handoff)

Two teams each start with 1 cloud credit.
On each turn, the active team may stop the handoff or continue it.
Continuing means losing 1 of its own credits while the other team gains 2 credits.
If a team stops, both keep the credits currently shown on their side.
If nobody stops within five moves, both teams get 0.

Team 1 moves first.
Stopping immediately gives final rewards of 1 and 1.
Continuing changes the credits to 0 for Team 1 and 3 for Team 2.

Team 2 then chooses.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 0 and 3.
Continuing changes the credits to 2 and 2.

Team 1 then chooses again.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 2 and 2.
Continuing changes the credits to 1 and 4.

Team 2 then chooses again.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 1 and 4.
Continuing changes the credits to 3 and 3.

Team 1 then makes the final move.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 3 and 3.
Continuing means the five-move limit is reached without a stop, so both teams get 0.
