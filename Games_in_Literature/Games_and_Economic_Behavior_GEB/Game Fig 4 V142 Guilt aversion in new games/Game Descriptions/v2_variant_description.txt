V2 [Research assistant reimbursement]

A principal investigator, an administrator, and a lab assistant are involved in a budget decision.
The principal investigator first chooses between KeepPlan (Out) and UseAssistantBudget (In).

If the principal investigator chooses KeepPlan (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the principal investigator, 100 for the administrator, and 30 for the lab assistant.

If the principal investigator chooses UseAssistantBudget (In), 25 units are taken from the lab assistant’s own budget and handed to the administrator.
The administrator then chooses between SmallReimbursement (Left) and FullReimbursement (Right).

If the administrator chooses SmallReimbursement (Left), the administrator keeps 20 and sends 5 to the lab assistant.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the lab assistant receives 10.
The result is 170 for the principal investigator, 120 for the administrator, and 15 for the lab assistant.

If the administrator chooses FullReimbursement (Right), the administrator sends the full 25 to the lab assistant.
That amount is doubled, so the lab assistant receives 50.
The result is 170 for the principal investigator, 100 for the administrator, and 55 for the lab assistant.
