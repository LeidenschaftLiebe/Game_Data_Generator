V1 [Border patrol]

A surveillance drone first reveals whether the patrol unit is HeavyArmor or LightArmor.
HeavyArmor happens with probability 9/10, and LightArmor happens with probability 1/10.

The patrol commander then chooses between StayBack and Advance.

If the commander chooses StayBack, the shift ends there.
This gives 0 to the commander, and 0 to the observer.

If the commander chooses Advance, the observer chooses between HighAlert, LowAlert, and SideMove.
The observer cannot tell whether the patrol unit is HeavyArmor or LightArmor.

If the patrol unit is HeavyArmor and the observer chooses HighAlert, this gives 2 to the commander, and 1 to the observer.
If the patrol unit is HeavyArmor and the observer chooses LowAlert, this gives -1 to the commander, and 0 to the observer.
If the patrol unit is HeavyArmor and the observer chooses SideMove, this gives 1 to the commander, and -1 to the observer.

If the patrol unit is LightArmor and the observer chooses HighAlert, this gives 1 to the commander, and 0 to the observer.
If the patrol unit is LightArmor and the observer chooses LowAlert, this gives -1 to the commander, and 1 to the observer.
If the patrol unit is LightArmor and the observer chooses SideMove, this gives 3 to the commander, and -1 to the observer.
