V3 [Family restitution]

A family organizer, a relative handling the money, and another family member are involved in a transfer decision.
The organizer first chooses between LeaveAsIs (Out) and UseFamilyFunds (In).

If the organizer chooses LeaveAsIs (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 100 for the relative, and 30 for the other family member.

If the organizer chooses UseFamilyFunds (In), 25 units are taken from the other family member’s own money and handed to the relative.
The relative then chooses between ReturnSmallPart (Left) and ReturnFullPart (Right).

If the relative chooses ReturnSmallPart (Left), the relative keeps 20 and sends 5 to the other family member.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the other family member receives 10.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 120 for the relative, and 15 for the other family member.

If the relative chooses ReturnFullPart (Right), the relative sends the full 25 to the other family member.
That amount is doubled, so the other family member receives 50.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 100 for the relative, and 55 for the other family member.
