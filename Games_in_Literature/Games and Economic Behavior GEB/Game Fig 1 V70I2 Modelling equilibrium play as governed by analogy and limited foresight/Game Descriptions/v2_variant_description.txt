V2 — Glacier Survey

A survey mission begins in either Ice Shelf A or Ice Shelf B.
Each starting point occurs with probability 1/2.
After that, the expedition head chooses between Left Traverse (L) and Right Traverse (R).

If the mission starts at Ice Shelf A and the head chooses Left Traverse (L),
the data specialist chooses between Log Reading (l) and Return Early (r).
If the path is Ice Shelf A, Left Traverse (L), Log Reading (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the expedition head and reward 2 to the specialist.
If the path is Ice Shelf A, Left Traverse (L), Return Early (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the mission starts at Ice Shelf A and the head chooses Right Traverse (R),
the specialist again chooses between Log Reading (l) and Return Early (r).
If the path is Ice Shelf A, Right Traverse (R), Log Reading (l),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the head and reward 2 to the specialist.
If the path is Ice Shelf A, Right Traverse (R), Return Early (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the mission starts at Ice Shelf B and the head chooses Left Traverse (L),
the specialist chooses between Log Reading (l) and Return Early (r).
If the path is Ice Shelf B, Left Traverse (L), Log Reading (l),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the head and reward 2 to the specialist.
If the path is Ice Shelf B, Left Traverse (L), Return Early (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the mission starts at Ice Shelf B and the head chooses Right Traverse (R),
the specialist chooses between Climb Ridge (u) and Drop Beacon (d).
If the path is Ice Shelf B, Right Traverse (R), Climb Ridge (u), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.
If the path is Ice Shelf B, Right Traverse (R), Drop Beacon (d),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the head and reward 2 to the specialist.
