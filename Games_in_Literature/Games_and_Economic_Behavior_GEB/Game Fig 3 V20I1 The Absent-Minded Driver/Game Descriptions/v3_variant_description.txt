V3 — Hiker on a Foggy Mountain Road

A hiker starts on a mountain path and passes three trail exits.

The exits are TrailX [X], TrailY [Y], and TrailZ [Z].

At each one, the hiker chooses LeaveTrail [EXIT] or KeepWalking [CONT].

Heavy fog makes the three exits look the same, so the hiker cannot tell whether the current place is TrailX [X], TrailY [Y], or TrailZ [Z].

If the hiker leaves at TrailX [X], the reward is 7.

If the hiker leaves at TrailY [Y], the reward is 0.

If the hiker leaves at TrailZ [Z], the reward is 22.

If the hiker keeps walking past TrailZ [Z], the reward is 2.
