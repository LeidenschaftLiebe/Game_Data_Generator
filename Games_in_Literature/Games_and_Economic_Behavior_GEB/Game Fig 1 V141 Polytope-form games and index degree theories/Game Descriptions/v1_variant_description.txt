V1 [Market entry]

A startup founder moves first and chooses between StaySmall (L) and Expand (R).

If the founder chooses StaySmall (L), an investor then chooses between SafeFunding (l_1) and TightFunding (r_1).
SafeFunding (l_1) leads to 7 for the founder and 11 for the investor.
TightFunding (r_1) leads to 0 for the founder and 3 for the investor.

If the founder chooses Expand (R), the investor chooses between SignalOptimism (l) and SignalCaution (r).
The founder then makes one more choice between LaunchNow (L_1) and DelayLaunch (R_1).
At that point, the founder does not know which signal the investor chose.

Expand (R), SignalOptimism (l), LaunchNow (L_1) leads to 10 for the founder and 15 for the investor.
Expand (R), SignalOptimism (l), DelayLaunch (R_1) leads to 2 for the founder and 1 for the investor.

Expand (R), SignalCaution (r), LaunchNow (L_1) leads to 1 for the founder and 2 for the investor.
Expand (R), SignalCaution (r), DelayLaunch (R_1) leads to 8 for the founder and 6 for the investor.
