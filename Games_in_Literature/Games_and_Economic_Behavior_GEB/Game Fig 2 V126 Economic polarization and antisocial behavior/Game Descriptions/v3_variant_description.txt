V3 [Political alliance]

A smaller party and a major party are deciding whether to cooperate.

The smaller party chooses between ExploitAlliance, QuitTalks, and CooperateInGoodFaith.

At the same time, the major party chooses between EndTalks, ProtectItsSide, and OfferFairSplit.
The major party chooses without knowing which move the smaller party selected.

If the smaller party chooses ExploitAlliance and the major party chooses EndTalks, both get 0, 0.
If the smaller party chooses ExploitAlliance and the major party chooses ProtectItsSide, this gives the smaller party 0, and the major party 10.
If the smaller party chooses ExploitAlliance and the major party chooses OfferFairSplit, this gives the smaller party 9, and the major party 1.

If the smaller party chooses QuitTalks, the result is always 0, 0.

If the smaller party chooses CooperateInGoodFaith and the major party chooses EndTalks, both get 0, 0.
If the smaller party chooses CooperateInGoodFaith and the major party chooses ProtectItsSide, this gives the smaller party 1, and the major party 9.
If the smaller party chooses CooperateInGoodFaith and the major party chooses OfferFairSplit, this gives the smaller party 5, and the major party 5.
