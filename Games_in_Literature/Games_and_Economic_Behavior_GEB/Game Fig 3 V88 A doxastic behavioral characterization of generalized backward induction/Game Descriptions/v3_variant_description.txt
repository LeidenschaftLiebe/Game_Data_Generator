V3 — Film Release

A producer is deciding whether to shut down a film release idea or develop it further.
The producer moves first and chooses between Develop Project (a₁) and Drop Project (b₁).

If the producer chooses Drop Project (b₁), the campaign ends immediately with reward 2 to the producer,
reward 2 to the distributor, and reward 0 to the marketing advisor.

If the producer chooses Develop Project (a₁), the distributor moves next and chooses between
Festival Route (a₂) and Commercial Route (b₂).

If the distributor chooses Festival Route (a₂), the marketing advisor chooses between
Quiet Campaign (a₃) and Strong Campaign (b₃).
If Quiet Campaign (a₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer,
reward 1 to the distributor, and reward 0 to the advisor.
If Strong Campaign (b₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 3 to the producer,
reward 3 to the distributor, and reward 1 to the advisor.

If the distributor chooses Commercial Route (b₂), the marketing advisor chooses between
Limited Push (c₃) and Wide Push (d₃).
If Limited Push (c₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer,
reward 2 to the distributor, and reward 1 to the advisor.
If Wide Push (d₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 4 to the producer,
reward 0 to the distributor, and reward 0 to the advisor.
