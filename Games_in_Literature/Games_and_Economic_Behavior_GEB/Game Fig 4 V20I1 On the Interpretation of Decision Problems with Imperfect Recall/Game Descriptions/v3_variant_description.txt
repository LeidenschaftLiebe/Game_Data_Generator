V3 — Cave Explorer with Similar Chambers

An explorer first reaches a chamber and chooses GoLeft [L] or GoRight [R].

If the explorer chooses GoRight [R], the exploration ends immediately with reward 0.

If the explorer chooses GoLeft [L], the explorer reaches another chamber that looks identical to the first one.
There, the explorer again chooses GoLeft [L] or GoRight [R].

After that, the explorer reaches one of two later chambers, but cannot tell which one it is.

At the left later chamber, choosing LeftDoor [L] gives reward 1, while RightDoor [R] gives reward 0.

At the right later chamber, choosing LeftDoor [L] gives reward 0, while RightDoor [R] gives reward 3.
