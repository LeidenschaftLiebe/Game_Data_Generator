V4 — Influencer spotlight chain

Four creators are lined up for the platform’s top spotlight slot.

Creator 1 moves first and can Stay Supporting (Stay) or Go for Spotlight (Charge).

If Creator 1 chooses Stay Supporting (Stay), the process ends right away.
All four remain regular creators, and each receives reward 4.

If Creator 1 chooses Go for Spotlight (Charge), Creator 1 takes the spotlight,
and Creator 2 decides next.

Creator 2 can Stay Supporting (Stay) or Go for Spotlight (Charge).

If Creator 2 chooses Stay Supporting (Stay), the process ends with reward 8 to Creator 1,
and reward 4 to Creators 2, 3, and 4.

If Creator 2 chooses Go for Spotlight (Charge), Creator 2 takes the spotlight,
Creator 1 loses it, and Creator 3 moves next.

Creator 3 can Stay Supporting (Stay) or Go for Spotlight (Charge).

If Creator 3 chooses Stay Supporting (Stay), the process ends with reward 0 to Creator 1,
reward 8 to Creator 2, and reward 4 to Creators 3 and 4.

If Creator 3 chooses Go for Spotlight (Charge), Creator 3 takes the spotlight,
Creator 2 loses it, and Creator 4 gets the final move.

Creator 4 can Stay Supporting (Stay) or Go for Spotlight (Charge).

If Creator 4 chooses Stay Supporting (Stay), the process ends with reward 0 to Creators 1 and 2,
reward 8 to Creator 3, and reward 4 to Creator 4.

If Creator 4 chooses Go for Spotlight (Charge), Creator 4 gets the final spotlight.
The process ends with reward 0 to Creators 1, 2, and 3, and reward 8 to Creator 4.
