V4 [Mountain weather station]

A hidden reading first determines whether the station chief is PreparedChief or FragileChief.
PreparedChief happens with probability 9/10, and FragileChief happens with probability 1/10.

The chief then chooses between LowBeacon and HighBeacon.

After seeing the beacon, the patrol observer chooses between Confront and Ignore.

If the chief is PreparedChief and chooses LowBeacon, then Confront gives 0 to the chief, and 0 to the observer.
If the chief is PreparedChief and chooses LowBeacon, then Ignore gives 2 to the chief, and 1 to the observer.

If the chief is PreparedChief and chooses HighBeacon, then Confront gives 1 to the chief, and 0 to the observer.
If the chief is PreparedChief and chooses HighBeacon, then Ignore gives 3 to the chief, and 1 to the observer.

If the chief is FragileChief and chooses LowBeacon, then Confront gives 1 to the chief, and 1 to the observer.
If the chief is FragileChief and chooses LowBeacon, then Ignore gives 3 to the chief, and 0 to the observer.

If the chief is FragileChief and chooses HighBeacon, then Confront gives 0 to the chief, and 1 to the observer.
If the chief is FragileChief and chooses HighBeacon, then Ignore gives 2 to the chief, and 0 to the observer.
