V5 — Space mission planning

A mission commander moves first.
She can stick with a safe standard mission (A), which ends with reward 5 to each team involved.

Or she can pursue a more ambitious mission path (B) and send the next choice to the operations chief.

The operations chief can cancel the advanced mission plan (C) or approve the next stage (D).

Canceling it (C) gives reward 7 to the commander, reward 9 to the operations chief,
and a loss of 3 to the science team.

Approving the next stage (D) returns the choice to the commander.

The commander can choose a restricted mission profile (E) or a bold deep-space attempt (F).

The restricted profile (E) ends with reward 8 to the commander, reward 5 to the operations chief,
and reward 0 to the science team.

The deep-space attempt (F) gives the final choice to the science team.

The science team can back the full mission (G) or redirect it toward their own preferred objective (H).

Backing the full mission (G) leads to reward 11 to the commander, reward 11 to the operations chief,
and reward 9 to the science team.

Redirecting it (H) leads to reward 2 to the commander, reward 7 to the operations chief,
and reward 10 to the science team.
