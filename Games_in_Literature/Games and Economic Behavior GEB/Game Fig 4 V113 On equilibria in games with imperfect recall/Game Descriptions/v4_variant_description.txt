V4 [Wildfire breakout]

Nature first chooses, with probability 1/2 each, which driver-order is used.

In the left setup, Driver A moves first and chooses between ContinueLine and TakeExitGap.
If Driver A chooses TakeExitGap, then the Safety Officer later chooses between LineWatch and GapWatch.
LineWatch gives (1, 1, -1).
GapWatch gives (0, 0, 0).

If Driver A chooses ContinueLine, then Driver B chooses between ContinueLineAgain and TakeLateGap.
TakeLateGap gives (0, 0, 0).
If Driver B chooses ContinueLineAgain, the Safety Officer chooses between LineWatch and GapWatch.
LineWatch gives (0, 0, 0).
GapWatch gives (1, 1, -1).

In the right setup, Driver B moves first and Driver A moves second in the corresponding mirrored way.
