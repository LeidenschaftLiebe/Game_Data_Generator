V4 (record label collaboration)

At the outset, a major record label may be either a loyal partner or a predatory partner, with probability 0.5 for each.
The label knows which type it is.
The independent studio knows only that the two types are equally likely.

If the label is loyal, it chooses whether to Buy a larger stake in the joint release or Sell some of its current stake.
The studio observes whether the label chose Buy or Sell, but it does not observe whether the label is loyal or predatory.
If the loyal label chooses Buy, the studio then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide its master recordings and production notes.
If the studio reveals after seeing Buy from a loyal label, the final reward is 15 for the label and 12 for the studio.
If the studio hides after seeing Buy from a loyal label, the final reward is -1 for the label and 1 for the studio.

If the loyal label chooses Sell, the studio again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the studio reveals after seeing Sell from a loyal label, the final reward is 5 for the label and 6 for the studio.
If the studio hides after seeing Sell from a loyal label, the final reward is 5 for the label and -1 for the studio.

If the label is predatory, it also chooses whether to Buy a larger stake or Sell some of its current stake.
If the predatory label chooses Buy, the studio then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the studio reveals after seeing Buy from a predatory label, the final reward is 5 for the label and -6 for the studio.
If the studio hides after seeing Buy from a predatory label, the final reward is 5 for the label and 1 for the studio.

If the predatory label chooses Sell, the studio again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the studio reveals after seeing Sell from a predatory label, the final reward is 15 for the label and -14 for the studio.
If the studio hides after seeing Sell from a predatory label, the final reward is 0 for the label and -1 for the studio.
