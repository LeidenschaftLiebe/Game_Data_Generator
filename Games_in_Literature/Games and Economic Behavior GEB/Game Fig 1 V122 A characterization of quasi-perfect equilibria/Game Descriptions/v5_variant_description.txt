V5 [Cave rescue]

A rescue chief first chooses between UpperEntry (A), LowerEntry (B), and CallOffSearch (E).

If the rescue chief chooses CallOffSearch (E), the operation ends there.
This gives 3 to the rescue chief, and 0 to the lead climber.

If the rescue chief chooses LowerEntry (B), the lead climber chooses between AnchorFirst (a) and DescendFast (b).
AnchorFirst (a) gives 0 to the rescue chief, and 0 to the lead climber.
DescendFast (b) gives 1 to the rescue chief, and 1 to the lead climber.

If the rescue chief chooses UpperEntry (A), the rescue chief then chooses between LeftChamber (C) and RightChamber (D).

After LeftChamber (C), the lead climber chooses between AnchorFirst (a) and DescendFast (b).
AnchorFirst (a) gives 4 to the rescue chief, and 0 to the lead climber.
DescendFast (b) gives 2 to the rescue chief, and 0 to the lead climber.

After RightChamber (D), the lead climber chooses between AnchorFirst (a) and DescendFast (b).
AnchorFirst (a) gives 1 to the rescue chief, and 1 to the lead climber.
DescendFast (b) gives 0 to the rescue chief, and 0 to the lead climber.

When choosing between AnchorFirst (a) and DescendFast (b), the lead climber cannot tell which earlier situation led there.
