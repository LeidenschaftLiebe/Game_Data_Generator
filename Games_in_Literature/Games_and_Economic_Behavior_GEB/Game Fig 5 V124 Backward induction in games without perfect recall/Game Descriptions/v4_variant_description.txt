V4 [Flood barrier repair]

A city engineer and a river pilot are deciding how to secure a damaged flood barrier.

The city engineer first chooses between SealNow (X), NorthBank (T), and SouthBank (B).

If the city engineer chooses SealNow (X), the operation ends there.
This gives 4 to the city engineer, and 4 to the river pilot.

If the city engineer chooses NorthBank (T), the river pilot chooses between LeftChannel (L) and RightChannel (R).
If the city engineer chooses SouthBank (B), the river pilot again chooses between LeftChannel (L) and RightChannel (R).

When making that choice, the river pilot cannot tell whether the earlier bank choice was NorthBank (T) or SouthBank (B).

After that, the city engineer chooses between UpperGate (U) and DeepGate (D).
When making that later choice, the city engineer cannot tell which earlier pair of choices led there.

After NorthBank (T) and LeftChannel (L), UpperGate (U) gives 5 to the city engineer, and 0 to the river pilot, 
while DeepGate (D) gives 2 to the city engineer, and 1 to the river pilot.

After NorthBank (T) and RightChannel (R), UpperGate (U) gives 2 to the city engineer, and 0 to the river pilot, 
while DeepGate (D) gives 0 to the city engineer, and 1 to the river pilot.

After SouthBank (B) and LeftChannel (L), UpperGate (U) gives 0 to the city engineer, and 1 to the river pilot, 
while DeepGate (D) gives 2 to the city engineer, and 0 to the river pilot.

After SouthBank (B) and RightChannel (R), UpperGate (U) gives 2 to the city engineer, and 1 to the river pilot, 
while DeepGate (D) gives 5 to the city engineer, and 0 to the river pilot.
