A traveler is deciding how to get to the airport.
At the beginning, the traveler chooses between take the direct train (b) and take a cheaper two-step route (a).
If the direct train is chosen, the traveler arrives safely with outcome 1.
If the cheaper route is chosen, the traveler later faces another choice between make the transfer correctly (c) and miss the transfer (d).
Making the transfer correctly leads to outcome 1.
Missing the transfer leads to outcome 0.
The traveler may prefer the longer route in principle, but worries about making a mistake during the transfer.
