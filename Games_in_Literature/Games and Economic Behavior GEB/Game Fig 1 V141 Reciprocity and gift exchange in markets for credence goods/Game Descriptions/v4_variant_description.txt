V4 [Home appliance service]

A homeowner calls a repair company.
The repair expert first posts two prices: SmallFixPrice (p_l) = 4 and FullServicePrice (p_h) = 8.

The homeowner then chooses between CancelVisit (out) and BookVisit (in).
If the homeowner chooses CancelVisit (out), both sides get 1.6.

If the homeowner chooses BookVisit (in), the issue is either SevereIssue (high) or MinorIssue (low), each with probability 0.5.
The repair expert learns which one it is.

If the appliance has a SevereIssue (high), only FullService (q_h) solves it.
FullService (q_h) with FullServicePrice (p_h) gives 2 to the expert and 2 to the homeowner.
FullService (q_h) with SmallFixPrice (p_l) gives -2 to the expert and 6 to the homeowner.

If the expert instead provides SmallFix (q_l) for a severe issue, the issue remains.
Charging FullServicePrice (p_h) gives 6 to the expert and -8 to the homeowner.
Charging SmallFixPrice (p_l) gives 2 to the expert and -4 to the homeowner.

If the appliance has a MinorIssue (low), either FullService (q_h) or SmallFix (q_l) solves it.
FullService (q_h) with FullServicePrice (p_h) gives 2 and 2.
FullService (q_h) with SmallFixPrice (p_l) gives -2 and 6.

SmallFix (q_l) with FullServicePrice (p_h) gives 6 to the expert and 2 to the homeowner.
SmallFix (q_l) with SmallFixPrice (p_l) gives 2 to the expert and 6 to the homeowner.
