V3 [Family gift transfer]

A family organizer, a relative handling the money, and another family member are involved in a transfer decision.
The organizer first chooses between KeepMoney (Out) and HandOverMoney (In).

If the organizer chooses KeepMoney (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 100 for the relative, and 30 for the other family member.

If the organizer chooses HandOverMoney (In), 25 units from the organizer’s own money are handed to the relative.
The relative then chooses between SmallGift (Left) and FullGift (Right).

If the relative chooses SmallGift (Left), the relative keeps 20 and sends 5 to the other family member.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the other family member receives 10.
The result is 145 for the organizer, 120 for the relative, and 40 for the other family member.

If the relative chooses FullGift (Right), the relative sends the full 25 to the other family member.
That amount is doubled, so the other family member receives 50.
The result is 145 for the organizer, 100 for the relative, and 80 for the other family member.
