V4 — Robot Mission under Hidden Terrain State

A robot first chooses ReturnBase [T] or ContinueMission [G].

If the robot chooses ReturnBase [T], the mission ends immediately with reward 3.

If the robot chooses ContinueMission [G], terrain is leftTerrain [l] or rightTerrain [r], each with probability 1/2.

If the terrain is leftTerrain [l], the robot reaches Scan1 [d1].
If the terrain is rightTerrain [r], the robot reaches Scan2 [d2].
At Scan1 [d1] or Scan2 [d2], the robot chooses Abort [E] or Proceed [D].
Choosing Abort [E] gives reward 2.
Choosing Proceed [D] leads later to TargetLock [d3].

When making a move at TargetLock [d3], the robot cannot tell whether the earlier terrain state was leftTerrain [l] or rightTerrain [r], 
and cannot tell whether the current situation came from Scan1 [d1] or from Scan2 [d2].

If the robot reaches TargetLock [d3] after leftTerrain [l] and chooses LeftTarget [L], the reward is 5.
If the robot reaches TargetLock [d3] after leftTerrain [l] and chooses RightTarget [R], the reward is 0.

If the robot reaches TargetLock [d3] after rightTerrain [r] and chooses LeftTarget [L], the reward is 0.
If the robot reaches TargetLock [d3] after rightTerrain [r] and chooses RightTarget [R], the reward is 6.
