V1 [Office bonus draw]

Two coworkers are placed in a one-shot office bonus decision.
At the start, chance selects who will decide: Worker 1 Selected (Player 1) or Worker 2 Selected (Player 2), each with probability 1/2.

The selected worker then chooses between KeepSureBonus (Don’t Roll) and UseBonusDraw (Roll).
If Worker 1 is selected and chooses KeepSureBonus (Don’t Roll), the result is 14 for Worker 1 and 0 for Worker 2.
If Worker 2 is selected and chooses KeepSureBonus (Don’t Roll), the result is 0 for Worker 1 and 14 for Worker 2.

If the selected worker chooses UseBonusDraw (Roll), chance determines the final transfer.
With probability 1/6, the result is 10 for the selected worker and 0 for the other worker.
With probability 5/6, the result is 10 for the selected worker and 12 for the other worker.
