11. The Smuggler's Pact

Two smugglers are caught at the border and questioned separately. Each must decide whether to deny everything or confess against the other.

If both deny, they face a light penalty.
If one confesses while the other denies, the confessor avoids punishment while the denier receives a heavy sentence.
If both confess, they each face a medium penalty.






12. The Banker's Secret

Two bankers are accused of insider trading and interrogated separately. Each must decide whether to protect the other or expose them.

If both protect each other, they receive only a light fine.
If one exposes while the other protects, the exposer walks free while the other faces a heavy fine.
If both expose, they each receive a medium fine.
