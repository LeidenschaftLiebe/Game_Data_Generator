V2 — Campaign Spending Choice

A leading campaign chooses its level of spending first, and the other campaign reacts after that.

The leading campaign chooses HeavySpend [S] or StandardSpend [C].

The other campaign then chooses whether to PollAndTrack [Observe] or GoInBlind [Not Observe].
Polling and tracking cost 30 points.

If it pays this cost, it learns the first campaign’s spending choice before making its own move.
Otherwise, it chooses without that information.

The reacting campaign then chooses SharpResponse [s] or CalmResponse [c].

If the leading campaign chose HeavySpend [S], the other campaign paid to observe, and then chose SharpResponse [s], 
the leading campaign gets 500 and the reacting campaign gets 170.
If the leading campaign chose HeavySpend [S], the other campaign paid to observe, and then chose CalmResponse [c], 
the leading campaign gets 300 and the reacting campaign gets 70.

If the leading campaign chose HeavySpend [S], the other campaign did not observe, and then chose SharpResponse [s], 
the leading campaign gets 500 and the reacting campaign gets 200.
If the leading campaign chose HeavySpend [S], the other campaign did not observe, and then chose CalmResponse [c], 
the leading campaign gets 300 and the reacting campaign gets 100.

If the leading campaign chose StandardSpend [C], the other campaign did not observe, and then chose SharpResponse [s], 
the leading campaign gets 600 and the reacting campaign gets 300.
If the leading campaign chose StandardSpend [C], the other campaign did not observe, and then chose CalmResponse [c], 
the leading campaign gets 400 and the reacting campaign gets 400.

If the leading campaign chose StandardSpend [C], the other campaign paid to observe, and then chose SharpResponse [s], 
the leading campaign gets 600 and the reacting campaign gets 270.
If the leading campaign chose StandardSpend [C], the other campaign paid to observe, and then chose CalmResponse [c], 
the leading campaign gets 400 and the reacting campaign gets 370.
