V5 — Talent Recruitment

A hiring manager is deciding how to proceed with a recruitment plan.

The hiring manager moves first and chooses between Close Search (O),
Quiet Search (L), and Aggressive Search (R).

If the hiring manager chooses Close Search (O), the process ends immediately.
The hiring manager gets reward 2 and the HR director gets reward 6.

If the hiring manager chooses Quiet Search (L) or Aggressive Search (R),
the HR director moves next.

The HR director cannot tell whether the hiring manager chose
Quiet Search (L) or Aggressive Search (R).

The HR director then chooses between Cautious Approval (A)
and Full Approval (B).

If the path is Quiet Search (L), Cautious Approval (A),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the hiring manager and reward 1 to the HR director.

If the path is Quiet Search (L), Full Approval (B),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the hiring manager and reward 2 to the HR director.

If the path is Aggressive Search (R), Cautious Approval (A),
the outcome gives reward -1 to the hiring manager and reward 3 to the HR director.

If the path is Aggressive Search (R), Full Approval (B),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the hiring manager and reward 2 to the HR director.
