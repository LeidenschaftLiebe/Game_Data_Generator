V1 — Rescue Routing

A rescue coordinator is deciding how to route an emergency response.
The coordinator moves first and chooses between North Route (a1) and South Route (b1).

If the coordinator chooses North Route (a1), the logistics chief moves next and chooses between
Send Ground Team (a2) and Hold Support Team (b2).
If the logistics chief chooses Send Ground Team (a2), the field specialist then chooses between
Immediate Entry (a3) and Cautious Entry (b3).
If the path is North Route (a1), Send Ground Team (a2), Immediate Entry (a3),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the coordinator, reward 2 to the logistics chief, and reward 2 to the specialist.
If the path is North Route (a1), Send Ground Team (a2), Cautious Entry (b3),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the coordinator, reward 1 to the logistics chief, and reward 1 to the specialist.

If the logistics chief chooses Hold Support Team (b2), the game ends immediately.
The outcome then gives reward 1 to the coordinator, reward 0 to the logistics chief, and reward 0 to the specialist.

If the coordinator chooses South Route (b1), the field specialist moves next and chooses between
Bridge Access (c2), Tunnel Access (d2), and Air Access (e2).
If the path is South Route (b1), Bridge Access (c2), the outcome gives reward 3 to the coordinator,
reward 0 to the logistics chief, and reward 1 to the specialist.
If the path is South Route (b1), Tunnel Access (d2), the outcome gives reward 0 to the coordinator,
reward 0 to the logistics chief, and reward 2 to the specialist.
If the path is South Route (b1), Air Access (e2), the outcome gives reward 0 to all three players.
