V1 — Product Recall

A manufacturer may face one of two internal product cases.
Nature first determines whether the case is Minor Defect with probability 0.1 or Major Defect with probability 0.9.

If the case is Minor Defect, the manufacturer chooses between
Stay Quiet (Out1) and Report It (In1).
If the manufacturer chooses Stay Quiet (Out1), the matter ends with reward 0 to the manufacturer
and reward 0 to the regulator.

If the case is Major Defect, the manufacturer chooses between
Stay Quiet (Out2) and Report It (In2).
If the manufacturer chooses Stay Quiet (Out2), the matter ends with reward 0 to the manufacturer
and reward 0 to the regulator.

If the manufacturer chooses Report It (In1/In2), the regulator then chooses between
Urgent Action (U), Monitor Only (M), and Dismiss It (D).
When the regulator moves, the regulator knows a report was made, but does not know which defect case occurred.

If the case was Minor Defect and the regulator chooses Urgent Action (U),
the manufacturer gets reward -1 and the regulator gets reward 3.
If the case was Minor Defect and the regulator chooses Monitor Only (M),
the manufacturer gets reward -1 and the regulator gets reward 2.
If the case was Minor Defect and the regulator chooses Dismiss It (D),
the manufacturer gets reward 2 and the regulator gets reward 0.

If the case was Major Defect and the regulator chooses Urgent Action (U),
the manufacturer gets reward -3 and the regulator gets reward 0.
If the case was Major Defect and the regulator chooses Monitor Only (M),
the manufacturer gets reward -1 and the regulator gets reward 2.
If the case was Major Defect and the regulator chooses Dismiss It (D),
the manufacturer gets reward 3 and the regulator gets reward 3.
