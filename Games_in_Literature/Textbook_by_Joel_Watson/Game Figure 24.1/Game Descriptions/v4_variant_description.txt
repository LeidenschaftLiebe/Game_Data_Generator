V4 (museum mystery parcel)

A museum staff member is randomly one of two types, helpful or disruptive, with probability 0.5 for each.
The staff member knows which type he is.

If the staff member is helpful, he chooses whether to hand over no parcel or to offer a wrapped parcel to a curator.
If he hands over no parcel, the final reward is 0 for the staff member and 0 for the curator.
If he offers the parcel, the curator then chooses whether to open it or refuse it.
If the curator opens a parcel from a helpful staff member, the final reward is 1 for the staff member and 1 for the curator.
If the curator refuses a parcel from a helpful staff member, the final reward is -1 for the staff member and 0 for the curator.

If the staff member is disruptive, he also chooses whether to hand over no parcel or to offer a wrapped parcel.
If he hands over no parcel, the final reward is 0 for the staff member and 0 for the curator.
If he offers the parcel, the curator again chooses whether to open it or refuse it.
The curator cannot tell whether the parcel comes from a helpful staff member or a disruptive staff member.
If the curator opens a parcel from a disruptive staff member, the final reward is 1 for the staff member and -1 for the curator.
If the curator refuses a parcel from a disruptive staff member, the final reward is -1 for the staff member and 0 for the curator.
