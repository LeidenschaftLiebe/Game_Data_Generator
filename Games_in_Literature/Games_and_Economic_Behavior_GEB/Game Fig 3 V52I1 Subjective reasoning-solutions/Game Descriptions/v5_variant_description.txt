V5 — Museum Exhibit Decision

Alice is a museum curator deciding what to do with a newly arrived artwork.
She first chooses DisplayNow [l], RestoreFirst [m], or StoreAway [r].

If Alice chooses RestoreFirst [m] or StoreAway [r], the artwork goes to Bob in logistics.
Bob does not know which of those two choices Alice made.
Bob then chooses ShipToGallery [L] or KeepBack [R].

If Alice chooses DisplayNow [l], the artwork goes to Emma, the gallery planner.
If Alice chooses RestoreFirst [m] and Bob chooses ShipToGallery [L], the artwork also goes to Emma.

When Emma receives the artwork, she cannot tell whether it came directly for display or after restoration and shipment.
Emma then chooses MainHall [p] or SideHall [q].

If Alice chose DisplayNow [l], then whether Emma chooses MainHall [p] or SideHall [q], Alice gets 0, Bob gets 2, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose RestoreFirst [m] and Bob chose ShipToGallery [L], then whether Emma chooses MainHall [p] or SideHall [q], 
Alice gets 2, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose RestoreFirst [m] and Bob chose KeepBack [R], then Alice gets 1, Bob gets 1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose StoreAway [r] and Bob chose ShipToGallery [L], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets -1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose StoreAway [r] and Bob chose KeepBack [R], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.
