V1 (market entry)

A firm first decides whether to go into a market or stay out.
If it stays out, the interaction ends immediately and each side gets 1.

If it goes in, the rival firm then decides whether to go in as well or stay out.
If the rival stays out, both sides end up worse off, with -1 for the first firm and -1 for the rival.
If the rival also goes in, the first firm then chooses between a hard competitive move and a softer one.
The hard move gives 4 to the first firm and 0 to the rival.
The softer move gives 3 to the first firm and 2 to the rival.
