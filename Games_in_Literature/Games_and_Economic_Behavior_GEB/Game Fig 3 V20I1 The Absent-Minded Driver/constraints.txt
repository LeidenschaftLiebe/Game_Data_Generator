Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:

There is one player, Driver, who moves at three successive decision nodes.
All three Driver decision nodes are in the same information set.
At each decision node, the available actions are EXIT and CONT.
The first EXIT leads to payoff 7.
The second EXIT leads to payoff 0.
The third EXIT leads to payoff 22.
If the player chooses CONT at all three decision nodes, the final payoff is 2.
