V4 (food delivery app entry)

A new food delivery app first decides whether to launch in a city already served by a dominant app.
If the new app stays out, the interaction ends immediately and the final reward is 0 for the entrant and 4 for the incumbent app.

If the new app launches, the incumbent app then decides whether to tolerate the new rival or fight back with a costly coupon war.
If the incumbent tolerates the entry, the final reward is 2 for the entrant and 2 for the incumbent.
If the incumbent starts a coupon war, the final reward is -1 for the entrant and -1 for the incumbent.
