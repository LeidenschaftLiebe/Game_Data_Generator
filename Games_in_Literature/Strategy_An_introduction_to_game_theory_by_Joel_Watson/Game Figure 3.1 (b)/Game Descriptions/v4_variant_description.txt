V4 (software product entry)

A software company first decides whether to enter a new product niche or stay out.
If it stays out, the game ends immediately with 2 for the first company and 2 for the incumbent.
If it enters, the incumbent then decides whether to stay out of direct competition or enter the niche as well.
If the incumbent stays out, the outcome is 1 for the entrant and 3 for the incumbent.
If the incumbent also enters, the entrant then chooses between two competitive responses.
The first response gives 4 to the entrant and 2 to the incumbent.
The second response gives 3 to the entrant and 4 to the incumbent.
