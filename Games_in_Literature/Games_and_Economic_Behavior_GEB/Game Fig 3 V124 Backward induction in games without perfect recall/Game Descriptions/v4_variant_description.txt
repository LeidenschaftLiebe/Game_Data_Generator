V4 [Arctic base transfer]

A base commander and a route planner are deciding how to move supplies between Arctic stations.

The base commander moves first and chooses between StopTransfer (T) and AttemptTransfer (B).

If the base commander chooses StopTransfer (T), the operation ends there.
This gives the base commander 2, and the route planner 6.

If the base commander chooses AttemptTransfer (B), the route planner chooses between UpperIceRoute (U) and DeepIceRoute (D).

Later, the route planner chooses between LeftCorridor (L) and RightCorridor (R),
but at that later choice the route planner cannot tell whether the earlier route choice was UpperIceRoute (U) or DeepIceRoute (D).

After UpperIceRoute (U), LeftCorridor (L) gives the base commander 4, and the route planner 4,
while RightCorridor (R) gives the base commander 0, and the route planner 0.

After DeepIceRoute (D), LeftCorridor (L) gives the base commander 0, and the route planner 0,
while RightCorridor (R) gives the base commander 1, and the route planner 1.
