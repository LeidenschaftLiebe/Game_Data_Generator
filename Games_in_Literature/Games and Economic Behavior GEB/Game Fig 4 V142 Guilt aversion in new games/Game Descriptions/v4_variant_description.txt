V4 [Platform refund case]

A merchant, a platform officer, and a customer are involved in a payment dispute.
The merchant first chooses between DropClaim (Out) and UseCustomerFunds (In).

If the merchant chooses DropClaim (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the merchant, 100 for the platform officer, and 30 for the customer.

If the merchant chooses UseCustomerFunds (In), 25 units are taken from the customer’s own balance and handed to the platform officer.
The platform officer then chooses between SmallRefund (Left) and FullRefund (Right).

If the platform officer chooses SmallRefund (Left), the platform officer keeps 20 and sends 5 to the customer.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the customer receives 10.
The result is 170 for the merchant, 120 for the platform officer, and 15 for the customer.

If the platform officer chooses FullRefund (Right), the platform officer sends the full 25 to the customer.
That amount is doubled, so the customer receives 50.
The result is 170 for the merchant, 100 for the platform officer, and 55 for the customer.
