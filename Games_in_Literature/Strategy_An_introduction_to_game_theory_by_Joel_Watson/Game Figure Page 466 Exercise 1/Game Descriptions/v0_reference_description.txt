Textbook Original Description: 

Here is a description of the simplest poker game. There are two players and only two cards in the deck, an Ace (A) and a King (K). 
First, the deck is shuffled and one of the two cards is dealt to player 1. That is, nature chooses the card for player 1. 
It is the Ace with probability 1/2 and the King with probability 1/2. Player 2 does not receive a card. 

Player 1 observes his card and then chooses whether to bid (B) or fold (F). 
If he folds, then the game ends with player 1 getting a payoff of −1 and 
player 2 getting a payoff of 1 (that is, player 1 loses his ante to player 2). 
If player 1 bids, then player 2 must decide whether to bid or fold. 

When player 2 makes this decision, she knows that player 1 bid, but she has not observed player 1’s card. 
The game ends after player 2’s action. 
If player 2 folds, then the payoff vector is (1, −1), meaning player 1 gets 1 and player 2 gets −1. 
If player 2 bids, then the payoff depends on player 1’s card; 
if player 1 holds the Ace, then the payoff vector is (2, −2); 
if player 1 holds the King, then the payoff vector is (−2, 2).

V0 (closest to the textbook)

There are two players and only two cards in the deck: an Ace and a King.
At the start, one of those two cards is dealt to Player 1.
The card is an Ace with probability 0.5 and a King with probability 0.5.
Player 1 sees the card.
Player 2 does not.

Player 1 then chooses whether to bid or fold.
If Player 1 folds, the game ends immediately with a final reward of -1 for Player 1 and 1 for Player 2.

If Player 1 bids, Player 2 must respond by either bidding or folding.
Player 2 knows that Player 1 bid, but does not know whether Player 1 holds the Ace or the King.

If Player 2 folds after Player 1 bids, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and -1 for Player 2.

If Player 2 bids after Player 1 bids, the result depends on Player 1’s card.
If Player 1 holds the Ace, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and -2 for Player 2.
If Player 1 holds the King, the final reward is -2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
