Game descriptions for matching actions (match is a win for player 2 and mismatch is a win for player 1). 

V21 - V30: 

21. Doctor vs. Patient

A doctor prescribes a treatment, and the patient chooses whether to follow through.

The doctor can prescribe pills or injections.
The patient can take pills or take injections.

If the doctor prescribes pills, the patient succeeds by taking pills, but fails by taking injections.

If the doctor prescribes injections, the patient succeeds by taking injections, but fails by taking pills.
