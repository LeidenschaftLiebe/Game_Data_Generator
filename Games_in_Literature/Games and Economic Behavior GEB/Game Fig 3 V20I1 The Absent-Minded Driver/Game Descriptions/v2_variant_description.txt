V2 — Space Probe Passing Three Stations

A probe starts its route and moves past three docking stations.

The stations are StationX [X], StationY [Y], and StationZ [Z].

At each station, the controller chooses DockNow [EXIT] or KeepFlying [CONT].

Because the station displays are confusing, the controller cannot tell whether the probe is at StationX [X], StationY [Y], or StationZ [Z].
So the same decision is used at all three stations.

If the probe docks at StationX [X], the reward is 7.

If the probe docks at StationY [Y], the reward is 0.

If the probe docks at StationZ [Z], the reward is 22.

If the probe keeps flying past StationZ [Z], the reward is 2.
