V4 (cybersecurity breach response)

At the outset, a security lead may be either fully prepared or overstretched, with probability 0.5 for each case.
The security lead knows which condition applies.
The attacker does not observe the security lead’s condition.

If the security lead is fully prepared, the lead chooses whether to remain in the control room or step out publicly and confront the breach.
If the lead remains in the control room, the attacker then chooses whether to back off or escalate.
If the attacker backs off after the lead remains in the control room, the final reward is 1 for the security lead and 0 for the attacker.
If the attacker escalates after the lead remains in the control room, the final reward is 0 for the security lead and -2 for the attacker.

If the lead steps out publicly while fully prepared, the attacker again chooses whether to back off or escalate.
The attacker observes that the lead stepped out, but still does not know whether the lead is fully prepared or overstretched.
If the attacker backs off after the lead steps out while fully prepared, the final reward is 1 for the security lead and 0 for the attacker.
If the attacker escalates after the lead steps out while fully prepared, the final reward is 0 for the security lead and -2 for the attacker.

If the security lead is overstretched, the lead chooses whether to remain in the control room or step out publicly.
If the lead remains in the control room while overstretched, the attacker then chooses whether to back off or escalate.
If the attacker backs off there, the final reward is 1 for the security lead and 0 for the attacker.
If the attacker escalates there, the final reward is -1 for the security lead and 1 for the attacker.

If the lead steps out publicly while overstretched, the attacker again chooses whether to back off or escalate.
The attacker observes the public step out, but does not know the lead’s true condition.
If the attacker backs off there, the final reward is 0.5 for the security lead and 0 for the attacker.
If the attacker escalates there, the final reward is -1.5 for the security lead and 1 for the attacker.
