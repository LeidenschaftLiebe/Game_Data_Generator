V5 [Film production]

A producer and a studio partner are deciding whether to make a film together.

The studio partner chooses between GrabControl, AbandonProject, and WorkTogether.

At the same time, the producer chooses between CancelFilm, KeepProtection, and SplitFairly.
The producer chooses without observing the studio partner’s move.

If the studio partner chooses GrabControl and the producer chooses CancelFilm, both get 0, 0.
If the studio partner chooses GrabControl and the producer chooses KeepProtection, this gives the studio partner 0, and the producer 10.
If the studio partner chooses GrabControl and the producer chooses SplitFairly, this gives the studio partner 9, and the producer 1.

If the studio partner chooses AbandonProject, the result is always 0, 0.

If the studio partner chooses WorkTogether and the producer chooses CancelFilm, both get 0, 0.
If the studio partner chooses WorkTogether and the producer chooses KeepProtection, this gives the studio partner 1, and the producer 9.
If the studio partner chooses WorkTogether and the producer chooses SplitFairly, this gives the studio partner 5, and the producer 5.
