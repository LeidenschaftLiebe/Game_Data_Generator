V1 (music festival partnership)

An organizer first chooses between partnership plan A, partnership plan B, and partnership plan C.
If the organizer chooses partnership plan C, the process ends immediately and each side gets a final reward of 5.

If the organizer chooses partnership plan A, a sponsor then chooses between response X and response Y.
If the sponsor chooses response X after partnership plan A, both sides receive a final reward of 8.
If the sponsor chooses response Y after partnership plan A, both sides receive 0.

If the organizer chooses partnership plan B, the sponsor again chooses between response X and response Y.
At that point, the sponsor cannot tell whether the organizer previously chose partnership plan A or partnership plan B.
If the sponsor chooses response X after partnership plan B, both sides receive a final reward of 2.
If the sponsor chooses response Y after partnership plan B, both sides receive a final reward of 6.
