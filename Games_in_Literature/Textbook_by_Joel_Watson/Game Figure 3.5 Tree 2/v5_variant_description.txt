V5 (fashion brand strategy)

A fashion brand first chooses between strategy A and strategy B.
A competing brand then chooses between response C and response D.
The competing brand has to make that choice without knowing whether the first brand chose strategy A or strategy B.

If the first brand had chosen strategy A, then either response leads to the same reward: 1 for the first brand and 2 for the competitor.
If the first brand had chosen strategy B and the competitor picks response C, the final reward is 3 for the first brand and 1 for the competitor.
If the first brand had chosen strategy B and the competitor picks response D, the final reward is 2 for the first brand and 4 for the competitor.
