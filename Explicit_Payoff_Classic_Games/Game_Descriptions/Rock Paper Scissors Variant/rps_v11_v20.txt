11. Battle of Elements

Two players simultaneously choose ice, flame, or tree.

If one plays ice and the other plays tree, the ice player gains +5, while the tree player receives –5.
If one plays tree and the other plays flame, the tree player secures +5, while the flame player is penalized –5.
If one plays flame and the other plays ice, the flame player earns +5, while the ice player suffers –5.
If both choose the same element, both receive 0.
