V3 [Airport screening]

A traveler and a screening officer are going through an airport procedure.
The traveler first chooses between Lane A, Lane B, and Cancel Trip.
If the traveler cancels the trip, the process ends there.
The traveler gets 1 and the officer gets 0.

If the traveler chooses Lane A or Lane B,
the officer then chooses between Secondary Screening (d), Fast Clearance (e), and Normal Clearance (f),
without knowing which lane was used.

If the officer chooses Fast Clearance (e), the process ends immediately.
After Lane A then Fast Clearance, the traveler gets 0 and the officer gets 5.
After Lane B then Fast Clearance, the traveler gets 0 and the officer gets 4.

If the officer chooses Normal Clearance (f), the process also ends immediately.
After Lane A then Normal Clearance, the traveler gets 1 and the officer gets 4.
After Lane B then Normal Clearance, the traveler gets 1 and the officer gets 5.

If the officer chooses Secondary Screening (d),
the officer later chooses between Clear (g) and Stop (h),
still without knowing whether the traveler came from Lane A or Lane B.

After Lane A then Secondary Screening then Clear, the traveler gets 2 and the officer gets 1.
After Lane A then Secondary Screening then Stop, both get 0.
After Lane B then Secondary Screening then Clear, the traveler gets 2 and the officer gets 0.
After Lane B then Secondary Screening then Stop, the traveler gets 0 and the officer gets 3.
