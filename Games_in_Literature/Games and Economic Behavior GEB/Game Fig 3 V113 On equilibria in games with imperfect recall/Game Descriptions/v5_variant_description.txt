V5 [Wildfire perimeter break]

A fire crew driver first chooses between PushOut [T] and StandDown [S].
If the driver stands down, the result is 0, 0.

If the driver pushes out, the safety officer chooses between WaitForRoute [W] and StopImmediately [I].
StopImmediately [I] gives -1, -1.
WaitForRoute [W] sends the driver into a repeated route-choice problem.

The driver repeatedly chooses between ContinueLine [C] and TakeExitGap [E], 
without knowing whether it is the earlier chance or the later one.

If the driver takes the earlier exit gap, the safety officer later chooses between LineWatch [H] and GapWatch [E].
LineWatch [H] gives 1, -1.
GapWatch [E] gives 0, 0.

If the driver continues once and then takes the later exit gap, the result is 0, 0.

If the driver continues twice, the safety officer again chooses between LineWatch [H] and GapWatch [E].
LineWatch [H] gives 0, 0.
GapWatch [E] gives 1, -1.
