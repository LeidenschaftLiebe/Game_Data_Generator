V0 — Optional BoS II

Player 1 moves first and chooses No or Yes.

After that, player 2 chooses O or B.

Then player 1 moves again and chooses O or B.
At this later move, player 1 knows whether the first move was No or Yes, but does not know whether player 2 chose O or B.

If the first move was No and both later choices are O, then player 1 gets 2 and player 2 gets 1.
If the first move was No and both later choices are B, then player 1 gets 1 and player 2 gets 2.
If the first move was No and the later choices do not match, then both players get 0.

If the first move was Yes and both later choices are O, then player 1 gets 2 and player 2 gets 1.
If the first move was Yes and both later choices are B, then player 1 gets 1 and player 2 gets 2.
If the first move was Yes and the later choices do not match, then both players get 0.
