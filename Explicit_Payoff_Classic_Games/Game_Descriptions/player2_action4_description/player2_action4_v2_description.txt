Game Description 1: College Admissions Panel

In the heart of college admissions panel, critical choices carve the path to triumph.

**Player1** evaluates the following strategies:
- essay review
- GPA filter
- interview round
- extracurricular scoring

Meanwhile, **Player2** plots their countermoves:
- diversity adjustment
- legacy boost
- rural quota
- athlete slot

When both players commit to their choices, these outcomes unfold:

- If **Player1** selects **essay review**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **diversity adjustment**, the resulting payoff is (-17, 17).
  - And **Player2** counters with **legacy boost**, the resulting payoff is (95, -95).
  - And **Player2** counters with **rural quota**, the resulting payoff is (65, -65).
  - And **Player2** counters with **athlete slot**, the resulting payoff is (4, -4).

- If **Player1** selects **GPA filter**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **diversity adjustment**, the resulting payoff is (15, -15).
  - And **Player2** counters with **legacy boost**, the resulting payoff is (-2, 2).
  - And **Player2** counters with **rural quota**, the resulting payoff is (-91, 91).
  - And **Player2** counters with **athlete slot**, the resulting payoff is (-50, 50).

- If **Player1** selects **interview round**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **diversity adjustment**, the resulting payoff is (73, -73).
  - And **Player2** counters with **legacy boost**, the resulting payoff is (20, -20).
  - And **Player2** counters with **rural quota**, the resulting payoff is (-86, 86).
  - And **Player2** counters with **athlete slot**, the resulting payoff is (35, -35).

- If **Player1** selects **extracurricular scoring**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **diversity adjustment**, the resulting payoff is (9, -9).
  - And **Player2** counters with **legacy boost**, the resulting payoff is (13, -13).
  - And **Player2** counters with **rural quota**, the resulting payoff is (-49, 49).
  - And **Player2** counters with **athlete slot**, the resulting payoff is (1, -1).

In this dynamic contest, foresight and adaptation decide the victor.
