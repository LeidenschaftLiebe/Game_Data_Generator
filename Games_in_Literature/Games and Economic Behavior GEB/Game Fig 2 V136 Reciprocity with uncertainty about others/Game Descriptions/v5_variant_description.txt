V5 [Creative collaboration]

An artist moves first and chooses between ShareDraft (C) and KeepDraft (D).
A second artist then observes that choice and chooses between ShareIdeas (c) and KeepIdeas (d).

If the first artist chooses ShareDraft (C) and the second artist chooses ShareIdeas (c), the result is 2, 2.
If the first artist chooses ShareDraft (C) and the second artist chooses KeepIdeas (d), the result is 0, 3.

If the first artist chooses KeepDraft (D) and the second artist chooses ShareIdeas (c), the result is 3, 0.
If the first artist chooses KeepDraft (D) and the second artist chooses KeepIdeas (d), the result is 1, 1.
