V5 — Transfer Review

A club begins by choosing between End Search (a), Domestic Route (b), and International Route (c).
If the club chooses End Search (a), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the club,
reward 0 to the recruitment head, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.

If the club chooses Domestic Route (b) or International Route (c), the recruitment head moves next.
The recruitment head cannot tell which scouting route was chosen.
The recruitment head then chooses between Track D (d) and Track E (e).

After that, the board reviewer moves.
The board reviewer cannot tell which earlier route led there.
The board reviewer then chooses between Approve Deal (s), Revise Deal (t), and Delay Deal (u).

If the path is Domestic Route (b), Track D (d), Approve Deal (s),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the club, reward 1 to the recruitment head, and reward 1 to the board reviewer.
If the path is Domestic Route (b), Track D (d), Revise Deal (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club, reward 1 to the recruitment head, and reward 2 to the board reviewer.
If the path is Domestic Route (b), Track D (d), Delay Deal (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club, reward 2 to the recruitment head, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.

If the path is Domestic Route (b), Track E (e), Approve Deal (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club, reward 0 to the recruitment head, and reward 2 to the board reviewer.
If the path is Domestic Route (b), Track E (e), Revise Deal (t),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club, reward 0 to the recruitment head, and reward 1 to the board reviewer.
If the path is Domestic Route (b), Track E (e), Delay Deal (u),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the club, reward 2 to the recruitment head, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.

If the path is International Route (c), Track D (d), Approve Deal (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club, reward 1 to the recruitment head, and reward 1 to the board reviewer.
If the path is International Route (c), Track D (d), Revise Deal (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club, reward 1 to the recruitment head, and reward 2 to the board reviewer.
If the path is International Route (c), Track D (d), Delay Deal (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club, reward 2 to the recruitment head, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.

If the path is International Route (c), Track E (e), Approve Deal (s),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club, reward 0 to the recruitment head, and reward 2 to the board reviewer.
If the path is International Route (c), Track E (e), Revise Deal (t),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the club, reward 0 to the recruitment head, and reward 1 to the board reviewer.
If the path is International Route (c), Track E (e), Delay Deal (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club, reward 2 to the recruitment head, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.
