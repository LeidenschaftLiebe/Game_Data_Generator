V3 [Submarine navigation]

A captain and a sonar officer are deciding how to navigate a submarine passage.

The captain moves first and chooses between SurfaceReturn (T) and ContinueDive (B).

If the captain chooses SurfaceReturn (T), the mission ends there.
This gives the captain 2, and the sonar officer 6.

If the captain chooses ContinueDive (B), the sonar officer chooses between UpperChannel (U) and DeepChannel (D).

Later, the sonar officer chooses between LeftRoute (L) and RightRoute (R),
but at that later point the sonar officer cannot tell whether the earlier choice was UpperChannel (U) or DeepChannel (D).

After UpperChannel (U), LeftRoute (L) gives the captain 4, and the sonar officer 4,
while RightRoute (R) gives the captain 0, and the sonar officer 0.

After DeepChannel (D), LeftRoute (L) gives the captain 0, and the sonar officer 0,
while RightRoute (R) gives the captain 1, and the sonar officer 1.
