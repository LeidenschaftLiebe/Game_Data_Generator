V1 [Breakfast café]

A café owner’s appetite is either Strong (s) with probability 0.9 or Weak (w) with probability 0.1.

After learning that appetite, the owner chooses between Bagel (B) and Quiche (Q).
A food critic then sees whether the owner picked Bagel (B) or Quiche (Q), but does not know whether the appetite was strong or weak.

If the owner picks Bagel (B) after Strong (s), the critic chooses between NoFeature (NF) and Feature (F).
NoFeature (NF) gives 3 to the owner and 1 to the critic.
Feature (F) gives 1 to the owner and 0 to the critic.

If the owner picks Bagel (B) after Weak (w), NoFeature (NF) gives 2 and 0,
while Feature (F) gives 0 and 1.

If the owner picks Quiche (Q) after Strong (s), NoFeature (NF) gives 2 and 1,
while Feature (F) gives 0 and 0.

If the owner picks Quiche (Q) after Weak (w), NoFeature (NF) gives 3 and 0,
while Feature (F) gives 1 and 1.
