V5 — Mountain Expedition

An expedition leader is deciding how to run a climbing mission.
The leader moves first and chooses between Advance Plan (a1) and Base Camp Hold (b1).

If the leader chooses Base Camp Hold (b1), the mission ends immediately with reward 2 to the leader,
reward 2 to the route captain, and reward 0 to the weather specialist.

If the leader chooses Advance Plan (a1), the route captain moves next and chooses between
Ridge Approach (a2) and Valley Approach (b2).

If the route captain chooses Ridge Approach (a2), the weather specialist then chooses between
Summit Push (a3) and Short Probe (b3).
If the path is a1, a2, a3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the leader, reward 1 to the route captain,
and reward 0 to the weather specialist.
If the path is a1, a2, b3, the outcome gives reward 3 to the leader, reward 3 to the route captain,
and reward 1 to the weather specialist.

If the route captain chooses Valley Approach (b2), the weather specialist then chooses between
Camp Shift (c3) and Night Climb (d3).
If the path is a1, b2, c3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the leader, reward 2 to the route captain,
and reward 1 to the weather specialist.
If the path is a1, b2, d3, the outcome gives reward 4 to the leader, reward 0 to the route captain,
and reward 0 to the weather specialist.
