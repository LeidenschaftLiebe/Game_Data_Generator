V5 (sports media rights)

A sports network first chooses between bid strategy A, bid strategy B, and bid strategy C.
If the network chooses bid strategy C, the contest ends immediately and each side gets a final reward of 5.

If the network chooses bid strategy A, a competing broadcaster then chooses between move X and move Y.
If the broadcaster chooses move X after bid strategy A, each side gets a final reward of 8.
If the broadcaster chooses move Y after bid strategy A, each side gets 0.

If the network chooses bid strategy B, the broadcaster again chooses between move X and move Y.
At that point, the broadcaster cannot tell whether the network previously chose bid strategy A or bid strategy B.
If the broadcaster chooses move X after bid strategy B, each side gets a final reward of 2.
If the broadcaster chooses move Y after bid strategy B, each side gets a final reward of 6.
