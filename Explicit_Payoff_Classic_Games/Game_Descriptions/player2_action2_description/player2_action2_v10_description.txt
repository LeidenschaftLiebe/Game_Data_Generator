Game Description 1: A Fantasy Duel

Under the bright lights of a fantasy duel, every decision shapes the future.

**Player1** stands ready to unleash either:
- A masterful **ice storm**, or
- A daring **thunder strike**.

Meanwhile, **Player2** prepares their countermove:
- A nimble **mana barrier**, or
- A cunning **healing chant**.

Every pairing of choices leads to different consequences:

- If Player1 chooses **ice storm**:
  - And Player2 responds with **mana barrier**, the resulting payoff is (10, -10).
  - And Player2 responds with **healing chant**, the resulting payoff is (-10, 10).

- If Player1 chooses **thunder strike**:
  - And Player2 responds with **mana barrier**, the resulting payoff is (-20, 20).
  - And Player2 responds with **healing chant**, the resulting payoff is (20, -20).

The sharpest strategy and the boldest timing will claim victory.
