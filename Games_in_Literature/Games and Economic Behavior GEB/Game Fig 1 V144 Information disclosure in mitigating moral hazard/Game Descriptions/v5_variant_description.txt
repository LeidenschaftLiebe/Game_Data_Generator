V5 [Delivery assignment]

A courier is deciding whether to accept a delivery assignment from a dispatcher.
Chance first determines whether the assignment is EasyTask or DifficultTask, each with probability 0.50.

The courier does not know the assignment difficulty when making the first decision.
So the courier first chooses between AcceptAssignment (IN) and RejectAssignment (OUT).

If the courier chooses RejectAssignment (OUT), the interaction ends immediately.
The courier gets 30 and the dispatcher gets 25.

If the courier chooses AcceptAssignment (IN), the courier then chooses between GoAllOut (HIGH) and TakeItEasy (LOW).
The courier still does not know whether the assignment is easy or difficult at that point.

If the assignment is easy and the courier chooses GoAllOut (HIGH), the result is 20 for the courier and 50 for the dispatcher.
If the assignment is easy and the courier chooses TakeItEasy (LOW), the result is 50 for the courier and 35 for the dispatcher.

If the assignment is difficult and the courier chooses GoAllOut (HIGH), the result is 20 for the courier and 50 for the dispatcher.
If the assignment is difficult and the courier chooses TakeItEasy (LOW), the result is 0 for the courier and 35 for the dispatcher.
