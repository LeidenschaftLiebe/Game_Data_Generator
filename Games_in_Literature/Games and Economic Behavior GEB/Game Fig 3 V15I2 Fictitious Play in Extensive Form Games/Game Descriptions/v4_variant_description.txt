V4 — Museum Expansion Proposal

Director 1 moves first and chooses BuildWing [A] or Postpone [D].

If the director chooses BuildWing [A], board chair 2 then chooses ApproveBudget [a] or RequestCuts [d].

If the director chooses Postpone [D], the matter goes to donor 3.
If the director chooses BuildWing [A] and the board chair chooses RequestCuts [d], the matter also goes to donor 3.

When donor 3 moves, the donor cannot tell whether the current situation came from Postpone [D] or from BuildWing [A] followed by RequestCuts [d].
The donor then chooses BackMuseum [l] or DeclineSupport [r].

If donor 3 is acting after Postpone [D] and chooses BackMuseum [l], the rewards are 3, 2, and 2.
If donor 3 is acting after Postpone [D] and chooses DeclineSupport [r], the rewards are 0, 0, and 0.
