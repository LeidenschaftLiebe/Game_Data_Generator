11) Agricultural Development Program

A regional officer receives funds to boost rural farming.
They can either keep the resources for central administration or trust a local cooperative to manage them.
If trusted, the cooperative expands agricultural output and generates much greater value.
Later, the cooperative may share a fair portion with the officer’s department or keep the entire benefit.





12) Cultural Exchange Fund

If sent, the collaboration flourishes and multiplies the total impact.
The partner may return a generous share or keep the expanded benefit.
