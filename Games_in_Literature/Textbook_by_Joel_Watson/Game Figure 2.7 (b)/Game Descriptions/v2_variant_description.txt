V2 (online course platforms)

Two online course platforms are competing for the same group of learners.
Each platform must decide on its tuition level without seeing the other platform’s decision in advance.
If both platforms charge a premium tuition, each earns 1 million.
If Platform 1 charges premium tuition while Platform 2 discounts, Platform 1 earns nothing and Platform 2 earns 2 million.
If Platform 1 discounts while Platform 2 stays premium, Platform 1 earns 2 million and Platform 2 earns nothing.
If both discount, they divide the market more evenly and each earns 0.5 million.
