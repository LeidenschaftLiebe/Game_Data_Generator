V3 (museum digitization deal)

At the outset, a technology vendor may be either a trustworthy partner or an opportunistic partner, with probability 0.5 for each.
The vendor knows which type it is.
The museum knows only that the two types are equally likely.

If the vendor is trustworthy, it chooses whether to Buy a larger stake in the digitization project or Sell part of its existing stake.
The museum observes whether the vendor chose Buy or Sell, but it does not observe whether the vendor is trustworthy or opportunistic.
If the trustworthy vendor chooses Buy, the museum then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide its archive data and restoration methods.
If the museum reveals after seeing Buy from a trustworthy vendor, the final reward is 15 for the vendor and 12 for the museum.
If the museum hides after seeing Buy from a trustworthy vendor, the final reward is -1 for the vendor and 1 for the museum.

If the trustworthy vendor chooses Sell, the museum again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the museum reveals after seeing Sell from a trustworthy vendor, the final reward is 5 for the vendor and 6 for the museum.
If the museum hides after seeing Sell from a trustworthy vendor, the final reward is 5 for the vendor and -1 for the museum.

If the vendor is opportunistic, it also chooses whether to Buy a larger stake or Sell part of its existing stake.
If the opportunistic vendor chooses Buy, the museum then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the museum reveals after seeing Buy from an opportunistic vendor, the final reward is 5 for the vendor and -6 for the museum.
If the museum hides after seeing Buy from an opportunistic vendor, the final reward is 5 for the vendor and 1 for the museum.

If the opportunistic vendor chooses Sell, the museum again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the museum reveals after seeing Sell from an opportunistic vendor, the final reward is 15 for the vendor and -14 for the museum.
If the museum hides after seeing Sell from an opportunistic vendor, the final reward is 0 for the vendor and -1 for the museum.
