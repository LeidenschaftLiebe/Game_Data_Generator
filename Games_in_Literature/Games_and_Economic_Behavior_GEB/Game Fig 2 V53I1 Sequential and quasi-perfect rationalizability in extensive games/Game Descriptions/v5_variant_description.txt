V5 — Vineyard Expansion

A vineyard owner is deciding how to handle a new expansion plan.
The owner first chooses among Leave It As Is (L), Moderate Expansion (M), and Major Expansion (R).

If the owner chooses Leave It As Is (L), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the owner and reward 2 to the operations manager.

If the owner chooses Moderate Expansion (M) or Major Expansion (R), the operations manager then chooses between
Left Schedule (l) and Right Schedule (r).
When the operations manager moves, the operations manager does not know whether the owner chose
Moderate Expansion (M) or Major Expansion (R).

If the owner chose Moderate Expansion (M) and the operations manager chooses Left Schedule (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the owner and reward 1 to the manager.
If the owner chose Moderate Expansion (M) and the operations manager chooses Right Schedule (r),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the owner and reward 0 to the manager.

If the owner chose Major Expansion (R) and the operations manager chooses Left Schedule (l),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the owner and reward 0 to the manager.
If the owner chose Major Expansion (R) and the operations manager chooses Right Schedule (r),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the owner and reward 3 to the manager.
