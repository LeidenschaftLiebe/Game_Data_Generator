V2 [Glacier breach mission]

A ridge captain moves first and chooses between HoldLine and CrossCrevasse.
A rope specialist sees that choice and then chooses between HoldLine and CrossCrevasse.

If both hold the line, this gives 1 to the ridge captain, and 2 to the rope specialist.

If the ridge captain holds the line but the rope specialist crosses alone, this gives 3 to the ridge captain, and 0 to the rope specialist.

If the ridge captain crosses but the rope specialist refuses to follow, this gives 0 to the ridge captain, and 3 to the rope specialist.

If both cross together, this gives 3 to the ridge captain, and 2 to the rope specialist.
