V0 (Microsoft and Celera, closest to the prompt)

At the outset, Microsoft may be one of two types, Friend or Enemy, with probability 0.5 for each.
Microsoft knows its own type.
Celera knows only that the two types are equally likely.

After learning its type, Microsoft chooses whether to Invest or Sabotage.
Celera then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide its proprietary trade secrets.
When Celera makes that choice, Celera does not know Microsoft’s type and does not observe whether Microsoft chose Invest or Sabotage.

If Microsoft is Enemy and chooses Invest, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 5 for Microsoft and 1 for Celera.
If Microsoft is Enemy and chooses Invest, then Hide leads to a final reward of 0 for Microsoft and 3 for Celera.

If Microsoft is Enemy and chooses Sabotage, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 8 for Microsoft and -1 for Celera.
If Microsoft is Enemy and chooses Sabotage, then Hide leads to a final reward of 7 for Microsoft and 0 for Celera.

If Microsoft is Friend and chooses Invest, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 9 for Microsoft and 9 for Celera.
If Microsoft is Friend and chooses Invest, then Hide leads to a final reward of 0 for Microsoft and 0 for Celera.

If Microsoft is Friend and chooses Sabotage, then Reveal leads to a final reward of 2 for Microsoft and 0 for Celera.
If Microsoft is Friend and chooses Sabotage, then Hide leads to a final reward of 0 for Microsoft and 4 for Celera.
