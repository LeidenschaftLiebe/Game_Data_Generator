V0 (closest to the book)

Two players are negotiating over how to divide two dollars.
Player 1 moves first and chooses whether to offer all of the money to Player 2, half of the money, or none of the money.
These three choices are labeled Offer H, M, and L.

If Player 1 makes offer H, then Player 2 chooses whether to accept or reject that offer.
If Player 2 accepts offer H, the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 2 rejects offer H, the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.

If Player 1 makes offer M, then Player 2 chooses whether to accept or reject that offer.
If Player 2 accepts offer M, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and 1 for Player 2.
If Player 2 rejects offer M, the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.

If Player 1 makes offer L, then Player 2 chooses whether to accept or reject that offer.
If Player 2 accepts offer L, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If Player 2 rejects offer L, the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
