V3 (museum restoration wing)

At the outset, the museum director may be either an ordinary director or a cooperative director.
The ordinary director occurs with probability 3/4.
The cooperative director occurs with probability 1/4.
The director learns which type applies.
The donor partner does not observe the director’s type.

If the director is ordinary, the director first chooses whether to invest in the restoration project or not.
If the ordinary director does not invest, the game ends with a final reward of 0 for the director and 0 for the donor partner.
If the ordinary director invests, the donor partner then chooses whether to invest as well or not.
The donor partner does not know whether the investor is the ordinary director or the cooperative director.
If the donor partner does not invest after the ordinary director invests, the final reward is -2 for the director and 0 for the donor partner.
If the donor partner invests after the ordinary director invests, the director then chooses whether to keep the restored wing mainly for private prestige or share the benefits.
If the ordinary director keeps the benefits selfishly, the final reward is 6 for the director and -2 for the donor partner.
If the ordinary director shares the benefits, the final reward is 2 for the director and 2 for the donor partner.

If the director is cooperative, the director first chooses whether to invest in the restoration project or not.
If the cooperative director does not invest, the game ends with a final reward of 0 for the director and 0 for the donor partner.
If the cooperative director invests, the donor partner then chooses whether to invest as well or not.
The donor partner again does not know whether the investor is ordinary or cooperative.
If the donor partner does not invest after the cooperative director invests, the final reward is 1 for the director and 0 for the donor partner.
If the donor partner invests after the cooperative director invests, the director then chooses whether to keep the restored wing selfishly or share it.
If the cooperative director keeps it selfishly, the final reward is 1 for the director and -2 for the donor partner.
If the cooperative director shares it, the final reward is 2 for the director and 2 for the donor partner.
