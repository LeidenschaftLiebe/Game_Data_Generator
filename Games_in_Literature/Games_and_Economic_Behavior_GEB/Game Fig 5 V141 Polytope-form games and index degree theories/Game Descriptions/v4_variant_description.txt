V4 [Product launch messaging]

A startup’s product readiness is either Strong (s) with probability 0.9 or Weak (w) with probability 0.1.

After learning that privately, the founder chooses between BoldLaunch (B) and QuietLaunch (Q).
A reviewer sees whether the launch message was bold or quiet, but does not know the real readiness level.

If the founder chooses BoldLaunch (B) after Strong (s), the reviewer chooses between NoFlag (NF) and Flag (F).
NoFlag (NF) gives 3 to the founder and 1 to the reviewer.
Flag (F) gives 1 to the founder and 0 to the reviewer.

If the founder chooses BoldLaunch (B) after Weak (w), NoFlag (NF) gives 2 and 0,
while Flag (F) gives 0 and 1.

If the founder chooses QuietLaunch (Q) after Strong (s), NoFlag (NF) gives 2 and 1,
while Flag (F) gives 0 and 0.

If the founder chooses QuietLaunch (Q) after Weak (w), NoFlag (NF) gives 3 and 0,
while Flag (F) gives 1 and 1.
