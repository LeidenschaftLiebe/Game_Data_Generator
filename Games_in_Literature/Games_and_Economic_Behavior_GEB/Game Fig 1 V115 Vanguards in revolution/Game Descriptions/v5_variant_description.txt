V5 [Coral reef rescue]

A dive chief moves first and chooses between WaitOffshore and EnterReef.
A navigation diver sees that choice and then chooses between WaitOffshore and EnterReef.

If both wait offshore, this gives 1 to the dive chief, and 2 to the navigation diver.

If the dive chief waits offshore but the navigation diver enters alone, this gives 3 to the dive chief, and 0 to the navigation diver.

If the dive chief enters but the navigation diver does not follow, this gives 0 to the dive chief, and 3 to the navigation diver.

If both enter together, this gives 3 to the dive chief, and 2 to the navigation diver.
