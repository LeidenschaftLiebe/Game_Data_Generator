V5 [Factory quality check]

A production manager and a quality inspector are handling a batch of goods.
The manager first chooses between the full process and the direct shipment route.
If the manager chooses the full process, the manager next chooses between a careful station and a rushed station.
If the manager chooses the careful station, the manager then chooses between scrapping the batch and sending it to final inspection.
If the manager scraps the batch there, both sides get 0.

If the manager sends it to final inspection, the inspector must decide without knowing whether the batch came from 
the careful station, the rushed station, or the direct shipment route.
If the manager chooses the rushed station, the inspector faces that same blind choice.
If the manager chooses the direct shipment route at the start, the inspector again faces that same blind choice.

At the inspector’s move, choosing pass gives both sides 1 after the careful-inspection path, 
gives the manager 1 and the inspector 0 after the rushed path, and gives both sides 1 after the direct shipment route.
Choosing recheck gives the manager 1 and the inspector 0 after the careful-inspection path, 
gives both sides 1 after the rushed path, and gives the manager 1 and the inspector 0 after the direct shipment route.
Choosing fail gives the manager 1 and the inspector -1 after the careful-inspection path, 
gives the manager 1 and the inspector -1 after the rushed path, and gives the manager 0 and the inspector -1 after the direct shipment route.
