V4 — Security Audit

A firm begins by choosing between Close Incident (a), Internal Escalation (b), and External Escalation (c).
If the firm chooses Close Incident (a), the matter ends immediately with reward 2 to the firm,
reward 0 to the compliance team, and reward 0 to the auditor.

If the firm chooses Internal Escalation (b) or External Escalation (c), the compliance team moves next.
The compliance team cannot tell which escalation route was chosen.
The compliance team then chooses between Route D (d) and Route E (e).

After that, the auditor moves.
The auditor cannot tell which earlier route led there.
The auditor then chooses between Approve (s), Demand Changes (t), and Delay Certification (u).

If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route D (d), Approve (s),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm, reward 1 to the compliance team, and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route D (d), Demand Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm, reward 1 to the compliance team, and reward 2 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route D (d), Delay Certification (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 2 to the compliance team, and reward 0 to the auditor.

If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route E (e), Approve (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm, reward 0 to the compliance team, and reward 2 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route E (e), Demand Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 0 to the compliance team, and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route E (e), Delay Certification (u),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the firm, reward 2 to the compliance team, and reward 0 to the auditor.

If the path is External Escalation (c), Route D (d), Approve (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm, reward 1 to the compliance team, and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route D (d), Demand Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm, reward 1 to the compliance team, and reward 2 to the auditor.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route D (d), Delay Certification (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 2 to the compliance team, and reward 0 to the auditor.

If the path is External Escalation (c), Route E (e), Approve (s),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 0 to the compliance team, and reward 2 to the auditor.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route E (e), Demand Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm, reward 0 to the compliance team, and reward 1 to the auditor.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route E (e), Delay Certification (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the firm, reward 2 to the compliance team, and reward 0 to the auditor.
