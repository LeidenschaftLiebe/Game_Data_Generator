V3 — City Development Decision

A city can move to River Park [x1], Transit Hub [x2], or keep the current arrangement As Is [x0].

At the start, an administrative rule selects which project takes priority if both are endorsed.
The order is either ParkFirst [ (x1, x2) ] or TransitFirst [ (x2, x1) ].

Then the parks department chooses Endorse [1] or Decline [0] for River Park [x1].

After that, the transit department chooses Endorse [1] or Decline [0] for Transit Hub [x2].

The parks department does not know which priority order was chosen.
The transit department does not know which priority order was chosen, and also does not know the earlier choice of the parks department.

If neither department endorses its project, the result is As Is [x0], and both departments get 0.

If only the parks department endorses, the result is River Park [x1].
The parks department gets 2 and the transit department gets 1.

If only the transit department endorses, the result is Transit Hub [x2].
The parks department gets 1 and the transit department gets 2.

If both departments endorse, the selected priority order decides which project is adopted.
