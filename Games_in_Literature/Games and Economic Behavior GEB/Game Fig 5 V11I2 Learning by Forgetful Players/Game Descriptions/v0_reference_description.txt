V0 — Burn 0 or Burn 1

Player 3 moves first and chooses burn 0 or burn 1.

If player 3 chooses burn 0, player 1 then chooses L or R.

After that, player 2 chooses l or r.
When player 2 moves after burn 0, player 2 cannot tell whether player 1 chose L or R.

If the earlier choices were burn 0 and L, then l gives 3 to player 1, 3 to player 2, and 3 to player 3.
In that same case, r gives 0 to all three players.

If the earlier choices were burn 0 and R, then l gives 0 to all three players.
In that same case, r gives 1 to player 1, 1 to player 2, and 1 to player 3.

If player 3 chooses burn 1, player 1 then chooses L or R.

After that, player 2 chooses l or r.
When player 2 moves after burn 1, player 2 cannot tell whether player 1 chose L or R.

If the earlier choices were burn 1 and L, then l gives 3 to player 1, 3 to player 2, and 2 to player 3.
In that same case, r gives 0 to player 1, 0 to player 2, and -1 to player 3.

If the earlier choices were burn 1 and R, then l gives 0 to player 1, 0 to player 2, and -1 to player 3.
In that same case, r gives 1 to player 1, 1 to player 2, and 0 to player 3.
