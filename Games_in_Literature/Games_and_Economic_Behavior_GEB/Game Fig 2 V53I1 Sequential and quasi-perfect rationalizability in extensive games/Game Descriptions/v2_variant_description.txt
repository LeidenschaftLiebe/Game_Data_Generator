V2 — Rescue Deployment

A rescue chief is deciding how to deploy a response team.
The chief first chooses among Hold Position (L), Limited Deployment (M), and Full Deployment (R).

If the chief chooses Hold Position (L), the situation ends immediately with reward 2 to the chief and reward 2 to the field coordinator.

If the chief chooses Limited Deployment (M) or Full Deployment (R), the field coordinator then chooses between
Left Response (l) and Right Response (r).
When the field coordinator moves, the field coordinator does not know whether the chief chose
Limited Deployment (M) or Full Deployment (R).

If the chief chose Limited Deployment (M) and the field coordinator chooses Left Response (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the chief and reward 1 to the coordinator.
If the chief chose Limited Deployment (M) and the field coordinator chooses Right Response (r),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the chief and reward 0 to the coordinator.

If the chief chose Full Deployment (R) and the field coordinator chooses Left Response (l),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the chief and reward 0 to the coordinator.
If the chief chose Full Deployment (R) and the field coordinator chooses Right Response (r),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the chief and reward 3 to the coordinator.
