V1 [Startup funding]

A founder is deciding how much early control and budget to place in an outside partner’s hands.

The founder moves first and chooses one of four levels:
NoPilot (N), SmallPilot (L), MediumPilot (M), or FullLaunch (H).

If the founder chooses NoPilot (N), this gives the founder 24, and the partner 15.

If the founder chooses SmallPilot (L), the partner chooses between Deliver (R) and Exploit (D).
If the partner chooses Deliver (R), this gives the founder 32, and the partner 22.
If the partner chooses Exploit (D), this gives the founder 20, and the partner 42.

If the founder chooses MediumPilot (M), the partner again chooses between Deliver (R) and Exploit (D).
If the partner chooses Deliver (R), this gives the founder 44, and the partner 36.
If the partner chooses Exploit (D), this gives the founder 12, and the partner 72.

If the founder chooses FullLaunch (H), the partner again chooses between Deliver (R) and Exploit (D).
If the partner chooses Deliver (R), this gives the founder 56, and the partner 42.
If the partner chooses Exploit (D), this gives the founder 0, and the partner 80.
