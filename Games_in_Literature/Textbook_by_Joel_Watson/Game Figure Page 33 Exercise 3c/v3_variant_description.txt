V3 (software launch)

A software company first decides between launch path U and launch path D.
If it chooses launch path U, the rival company then chooses between response A and response B.
If the rival chooses response A, the final reward is 3 for the first company and 3 for the rival.
If the rival chooses response B, the final reward is 5 for the first company and 4 for the rival.

If the first company chooses launch path D, the rival then chooses between response C and response D.
If the rival chooses response D, the final result is 2 for the first company and 2 for the rival.
If the rival chooses response C, the first company then chooses between follow-up option E and follow-up option F.
Follow-up option E gives 6 to the first company and 2 to the rival.
Follow-up option F gives 2 to the first company and 6 to the rival.
