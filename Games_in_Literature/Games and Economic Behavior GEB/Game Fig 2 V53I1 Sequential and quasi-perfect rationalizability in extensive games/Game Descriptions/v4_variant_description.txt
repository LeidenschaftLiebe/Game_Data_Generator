V4 — Space Test

A mission controller is deciding how to proceed with a system test.
The controller first chooses among Lock Current Setup (L), Medium Test (M), and Risky Test (R).

If the controller chooses Lock Current Setup (L), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the controller and reward 2 to the systems analyst.

If the controller chooses Medium Test (M) or Risky Test (R), the analyst then chooses between
Left Calibration (l) and Right Calibration (r).
When the analyst moves, the analyst does not know whether the controller chose
Medium Test (M) or Risky Test (R).

If the controller chose Medium Test (M) and the analyst chooses Left Calibration (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the controller and reward 1 to the analyst.
If the controller chose Medium Test (M) and the analyst chooses Right Calibration (r),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the controller and reward 0 to the analyst.

If the controller chose Risky Test (R) and the analyst chooses Left Calibration (l),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the controller and reward 0 to the analyst.
If the controller chose Risky Test (R) and the analyst chooses Right Calibration (r),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the controller and reward 3 to the analyst.
