V1 — Salvage Route

A recovery team is dropped into one of two field conditions, North Sector or South Sector.
Each condition occurs with probability 1/2.
After seeing the condition, the route leader chooses between Left Passage (L) and Right Passage (R).

If the team is in North Sector and the leader chooses Left Passage (L),
the extraction officer chooses between Lift Cargo (l) and Retreat (r).
If the path is North Sector, Left Passage (L), Lift Cargo (l),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the route leader and reward 2 to the extraction officer.
If the path is North Sector, Left Passage (L), Retreat (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the team is in North Sector and the leader chooses Right Passage (R),
the extraction officer again chooses between Lift Cargo (l) and Retreat (r).
If the path is North Sector, Right Passage (R), Lift Cargo (l),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the leader and reward 2 to the officer.
If the path is North Sector, Right Passage (R), Retreat (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the team is in South Sector and the leader chooses Left Passage (L),
the extraction officer chooses between Lift Cargo (l) and Retreat (r).
If the path is South Sector, Left Passage (L), Lift Cargo (l),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the leader and reward 2 to the officer.
If the path is South Sector, Left Passage (L), Retreat (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the team is in South Sector and the leader chooses Right Passage (R),
the extraction officer chooses between Ascend Rope (u) and Descend Shaft (d).
If the path is South Sector, Right Passage (R), Ascend Rope (u), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.
If the path is South Sector, Right Passage (R), Descend Shaft (d),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the leader and reward 2 to the officer.
