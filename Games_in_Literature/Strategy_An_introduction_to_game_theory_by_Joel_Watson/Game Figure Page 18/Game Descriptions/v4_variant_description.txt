V4 (restaurant market entry)

A restaurant group is deciding whether to open a new location in an area already served by an established restaurant.
The established restaurant sees whether the new group enters.
If the new group stays out, the established restaurant decides whether to advertise by itself.
If it advertises while alone, the entrant gets 0 and the established restaurant gets 4.
If it does not advertise while alone, the entrant gets 0 and the established restaurant gets 3.5.
If the new group enters, then both restaurants decide whether to advertise.
The entrant chooses whether to advertise without knowing whether the established restaurant chose to advertise.
If both advertise, each restaurant gets 3.
If only the established restaurant advertises, the entrant gets 1 and the established restaurant gets 6.
If only the entrant advertises, the entrant gets 6 and the established restaurant gets 1.
If neither advertises, each restaurant gets 5.
