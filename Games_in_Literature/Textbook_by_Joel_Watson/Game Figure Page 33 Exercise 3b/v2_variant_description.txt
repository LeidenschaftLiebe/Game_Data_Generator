V2 (restaurant expansion)

A restaurant owner first decides whether to open in a neighborhood or stay out.
If the owner stays out, the matter ends right away and each side gets 1.

If the owner opens, the rival restaurant then decides whether to enter the competition or stay out.
If the rival stays out, both sides end up with -1.
If the rival also enters, the first owner then chooses between an aggressive operating plan and a moderate one.
The aggressive plan gives 4 to the first owner and 0 to the rival.
The moderate plan gives 3 to the first owner and 2 to the rival.
