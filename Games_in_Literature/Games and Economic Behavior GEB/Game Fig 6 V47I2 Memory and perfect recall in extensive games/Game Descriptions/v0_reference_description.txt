V0 — Maze with Fading Entry Memory

A guide first takes player 1 to entry A or entry B.

At the first junction, player 1 chooses left1 or right1.

At the second junction, player 1 chooses left2 or right2.

From entry A, choosing left1 leads to a dead end no matter whether the next move is left2 or right2.
From entry A, choosing right1 and then right2 also leads to a dead end.
From entry A, choosing right1 and then left2 brings the player to a later junction.

From entry B, choosing left1 leads to a dead end no matter whether the next move is left2 or right2.
From entry B, choosing right1 and then right2 leads to a dead end.
From entry B, choosing right1 and then left2 brings the player to a later junction.

At that later junction, player 1 remembers the earlier turns but no longer knows whether the path began at entry A or entry B.

Then player 1 chooses left3 or right3.

If the player reached the later junction from entry A, then left3 leads to EXIT and right3 is blocked.
If the player reached the later junction from entry B, then left3 is blocked and right3 leads to EXIT.

Reaching EXIT gives player 1 a reward of 1.
Any blocked path gives player 1 a reward of 0.
