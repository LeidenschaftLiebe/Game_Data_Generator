V5 [Community aid fund]

A local organizer, a treasurer, and a resident are involved in a support decision.
The organizer first chooses between KeepResources (Out) and EntrustResources (In).

If the organizer chooses KeepResources (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 100 for the treasurer, and 30 for the resident.

If the organizer chooses EntrustResources (In), 25 units from the organizer’s own resources are handed to the treasurer.
The treasurer then chooses between SmallAid (Left) and FullAid (Right).

If the treasurer chooses SmallAid (Left), the treasurer keeps 20 and sends 5 to the resident.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the resident receives 10.
The result is 145 for the organizer, 120 for the treasurer, and 40 for the resident.

If the treasurer chooses FullAid (Right), the treasurer sends the full 25 to the resident.
That amount is doubled, so the resident receives 50.
The result is 145 for the organizer, 100 for the treasurer, and 80 for the resident.
