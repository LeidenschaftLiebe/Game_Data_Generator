31. Shepherd vs. Wolf

A shepherd guards his flock while a wolf prowls nearby.

The shepherd can stay by the hill pasture or the river meadow.
The wolf can prowl the hill pasture or the river meadow.

If both choose the hill pasture, or both choose the river meadow, the shepherd protects the sheep and wins with the same result.

If the shepherd stays at the hill while the wolf prowls the meadow, or the other way around, the wolf seizes a sheep and wins with the same result.
