Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints

The expert moves first by posting the fixed price pair, then the consumer chooses between out and in.
If the consumer chooses in, chance selects high or low with probability 1/2 each, then the expert chooses the service and the charged price.
Seed payoff constraint: after out, both players get 8/5.
High-severity anchor: after high, then q_l, then p_h, the payoffs are 6, -8.
Low-severity anchor: after low, then q_l, then p_l, the payoffs are 2, 6.
