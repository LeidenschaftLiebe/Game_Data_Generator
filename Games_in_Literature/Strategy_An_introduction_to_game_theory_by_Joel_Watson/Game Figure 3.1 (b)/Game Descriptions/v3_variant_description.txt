V3 (streaming platform launch)

A streaming company first decides whether to launch in a new content segment or stay out.
If it stays out, the game ends with 2 for the first platform and 2 for the rival platform.
If it launches, the rival platform then decides whether to stay out or launch too.
If the rival stays out, the outcome is 1 for the first platform and 3 for the rival.
If the rival also launches, the first platform then chooses between two post-launch strategies.
One strategy leads to 4 for the first platform and 2 for the rival.
The other leads to 3 for the first platform and 4 for the rival.
