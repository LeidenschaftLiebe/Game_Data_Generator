V2 — Medical Decision

A doctor is choosing among three treatment paths.

The doctor moves first and chooses between No Procedure (O),
Limited Treatment (L), and Aggressive Treatment (R).

If the doctor chooses No Procedure (O), the case ends immediately.
The doctor gets reward 2 and the hospital gets reward 6.

If the doctor chooses Limited Treatment (L) or Aggressive Treatment (R),
the hospital administration moves next.

The administration cannot tell whether the doctor chose
Limited Treatment (L) or Aggressive Treatment (R).

The administration then chooses between Restrict Resources (A)
and Approve Resources (B).

If the path is Limited Treatment (L), Restrict Resources (A),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the doctor and reward 1 to the hospital.

If the path is Limited Treatment (L), Approve Resources (B),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the doctor and reward 2 to the hospital.

If the path is Aggressive Treatment (R), Restrict Resources (A),
the outcome gives reward -1 to the doctor and reward 3 to the hospital.

If the path is Aggressive Treatment (R), Approve Resources (B),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the doctor and reward 2 to the hospital.
