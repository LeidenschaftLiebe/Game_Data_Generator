V2 (streaming entrant vs established platform)

A new streaming platform first decides whether to enter a market already served by a dominant platform.
If the entrant stays out, the interaction ends immediately and the final reward is 0 for the entrant and 4 for the incumbent platform.

If the entrant launches, the incumbent platform then decides whether to coexist with the newcomer or fight with an aggressive discount campaign.
If the incumbent chooses to coexist, the final reward is 2 for the entrant and 2 for the incumbent.
If the incumbent chooses to fight, the final reward is -1 for the entrant and -1 for the incumbent.
