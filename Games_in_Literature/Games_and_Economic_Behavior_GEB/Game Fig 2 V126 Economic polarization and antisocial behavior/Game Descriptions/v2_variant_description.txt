V2 [Research collaboration]

A lead researcher and a collaborator are deciding whether to work together on a project.

The collaborator chooses between TakeCredit, LeaveProject, and ContributeHonestly.

At the same time, the lead researcher chooses between ShutItDown, KeepControl, and ShareCredit.
The lead researcher chooses without observing the collaborator’s move.

If the collaborator chooses TakeCredit and the lead researcher chooses ShutItDown, both get 0, 0.
If the collaborator chooses TakeCredit and the lead researcher chooses KeepControl, this gives the collaborator 0, and the lead researcher 10.
If the collaborator chooses TakeCredit and the lead researcher chooses ShareCredit, this gives the collaborator 9, and the lead researcher 1.

If the collaborator chooses LeaveProject, the result is always 0, 0.

If the collaborator chooses ContributeHonestly and the lead researcher chooses ShutItDown, both get 0, 0.
If the collaborator chooses ContributeHonestly and the lead researcher chooses KeepControl, this gives the collaborator 1, and the lead researcher 9.
If the collaborator chooses ContributeHonestly and the lead researcher chooses ShareCredit, this gives the collaborator 5, and the lead researcher 5.
