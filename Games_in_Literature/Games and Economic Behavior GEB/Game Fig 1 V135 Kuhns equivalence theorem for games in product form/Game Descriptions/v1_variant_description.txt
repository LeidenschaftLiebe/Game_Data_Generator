V1 [Market signal and response]

Market conditions first turn out to be either StrongDemand (omega+) or WeakDemand (omega-).

The operations lead then observes that condition and chooses between LaunchLeft (L) and LaunchRight (R).

The marketing lead then observes both the market condition and the operations choice,
and chooses between TopPush (T) and BasePush (B).

If the condition is StrongDemand (omega+), then:
LaunchLeft (L), TopPush (T) gives 3, 2.
LaunchLeft (L), BasePush (B) gives 1, 4.
LaunchRight (R), TopPush (T) gives 4, 1.
LaunchRight (R), BasePush (B) gives 2, 3.

If the condition is WeakDemand (omega-), then:
LaunchLeft (L), TopPush (T) gives 2, 3.
LaunchLeft (L), BasePush (B) gives 4, 1.
LaunchRight (R), TopPush (T) gives 1, 4.
LaunchRight (R), BasePush (B) gives 3, 2.
