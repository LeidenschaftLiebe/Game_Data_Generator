V1 — Venture Screening

There are four people in a startup screening process.

Investor 1 moves first and chooses between PassDeal (Out1) and OpenReview (In1).

If Investor 1 chooses PassDeal (Out1), the process ends immediately.
All four people receive reward 0.

If Investor 1 chooses OpenReview (In1), Partner 2 moves next and chooses between
DropCase (Out2) and SendAhead (In2).

If Partner 2 chooses DropCase (Out2), the process ends with reward -1 to Investor 1
and reward 0 to the other three.

If Partner 2 chooses SendAhead (In2), Analyst 3 moves and chooses between
Recommend (L3) and RejectInternally (R3).

Committee Member 4 then moves last, but cannot tell which of Analyst 3’s two actions was taken.

Committee Member 4 chooses between Approve (L4) and Block (R4).

If the path is OpenReview (In1), SendAhead (In2), Recommend (L3), Approve (L4),
each of the four receives reward 1.

If the path is OpenReview (In1), SendAhead (In2), Recommend (L3), Block (R4),
Investor 1 gets 1, Partner 2 gets -5, and the last two get 0.

If the path is OpenReview (In1), SendAhead (In2), RejectInternally (R3), Approve (L4),
Investor 1 gets 1, Partner 2 gets -5, and the last two get 0.

If the path is OpenReview (In1), SendAhead (In2), RejectInternally (R3), Block (R4),
Investor 1 gets 1, Partner 2 gets -5, and Players 3 and 4 each get 1.
