V5 [Delivery assignment]

A dispatcher learns whether an assignment is EasyTask or DifficultTask, each with probability 0.50.
After learning that, the dispatcher chooses between NoDisclosure and ImmediateDisclosure.

If the dispatcher chooses ImmediateDisclosure, the courier learns the assignment difficulty before deciding whether to enter.
If the dispatcher chooses NoDisclosure, the courier does not learn the assignment difficulty before deciding.

The courier then chooses between AcceptAssignment (IN) and RejectAssignment (OUT).
If the courier chooses RejectAssignment (OUT), the courier gets 30 and the dispatcher gets 25.

If the courier chooses AcceptAssignment (IN), the courier then chooses between GoAllOut (HIGH) and TakeItEasy (LOW).
On the NoDisclosure side, the courier still does not know whether the assignment is easy or difficult when choosing effort.
On the ImmediateDisclosure side, the courier does know.

If the assignment is easy and the courier chooses GoAllOut (HIGH), the result is 20 for the courier and 50 for the dispatcher.
If the assignment is easy and the courier chooses TakeItEasy (LOW), the result is 50 for the courier and 35 for the dispatcher.

If the assignment is difficult and the courier chooses GoAllOut (HIGH), the result is 20 for the courier and 50 for the dispatcher.
If the courier chooses TakeItEasy (LOW), the result is 0 for the courier and 35 for the dispatcher.
