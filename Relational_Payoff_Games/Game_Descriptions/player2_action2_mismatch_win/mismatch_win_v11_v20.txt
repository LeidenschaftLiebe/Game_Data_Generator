Game descriptions for mismatching actions (mismatch is a win for player 2 and match is a win for player 1). 

V11 - V20: 

11. Fisherman vs. Fish

A fisherman casts nets while fish search for food.

The fisherman can set his nets at the river mouth or the shallow cove.
The fish can swim to the river mouth or the shallow cove.

If they both choose the river mouth, or both choose the shallow cove, the fisherman catches the fish and wins with the same outcome.

If the fisherman sets nets at the river mouth while the fish swim to the cove, or vice versa, the fish escape and wins with the same outcome.






12. Security Guard vs. Shoplifter

A guard patrols a store while a shoplifter looks for an exit.

The guard can watch the front entrance or the side door.
The shoplifter can try to sneak out through the front entrance or the side door.

If both choose the front entrance, or both choose the side door, the guard intercepts the shoplifter and wins with the same outcome.

If the guard watches the front entrance while the shoplifter runs to the side door, or the other way around, the shoplifter escapes and wins with the same outcome.






13. Falcon vs. Pigeon

A falcon hunts while a pigeon searches for safety.

The falcon can dive at the grain field or the farmyard.
The pigeon can fly to the grain field or the farmyard.

If both go to the grain field, or both go to the farmyard, the falcon captures the pigeon and wins with the same outcome.

If the falcon dives at the grain field while the pigeon flies to the farmyard, or vice versa, the pigeon escapes and wins with the same outcome.






14. Customs Officer vs. Smuggler

If the officer guards the road while the smuggler takes the trail, or the other way around, the smuggler gets through and wins with the same outcome.

20. Bouncer vs. Gatecrasher

At a party, a bouncer protects the entrances while someone tries to sneak in.

The bouncer can block the main door or the side gate.
