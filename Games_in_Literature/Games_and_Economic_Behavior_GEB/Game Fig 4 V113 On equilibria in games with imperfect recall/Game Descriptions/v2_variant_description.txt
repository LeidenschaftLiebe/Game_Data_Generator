V2 [Orbital debris chase]

Nature first chooses, with probability 1/2 each, which pilot-order is used.

In the left setup, Pilot A moves first and chooses between StayCourse and BreakOrbit.
If Pilot A chooses BreakOrbit, then the Interceptor later chooses between TrackMainLine and TrapExit.
TrackMainLine gives (1, 1, -1).
TrapExit gives (0, 0, 0).

If Pilot A chooses StayCourse, then Pilot B chooses between StayCourseAgain and BreakLate.
BreakLate gives (0, 0, 0).
If Pilot B chooses StayCourseAgain, the Interceptor chooses between TrackMainLine and TrapExit.
TrackMainLine gives (0, 0, 0).
TrapExit gives (1, 1, -1).

In the right setup, Pilot B moves first and Pilot A moves second in the same mirrored pattern.
