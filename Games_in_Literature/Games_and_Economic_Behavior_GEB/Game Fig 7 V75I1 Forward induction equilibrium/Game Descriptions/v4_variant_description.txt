V4 — Satellite Repair

A mission controller is deciding how to handle a satellite fault.
The mission controller first chooses between Repair Live (T) and Do Not Repair Live (Not T).

If the mission controller chooses Repair Live (T), the diagnostic specialist moves later.
If the mission controller chooses Do Not Repair Live (Not T), the ground commander moves next and chooses between
Limited Test (L) and Restore Service (R).

If the ground commander chooses Restore Service (R), the process ends immediately with reward 1 to the controller,
reward 2 to the ground commander, and reward 2 to the specialist.

If the ground commander chooses Limited Test (L), the mission controller moves again and chooses between
Bypass Fault (B) and Manual Calibration (M).
If the mission controller chooses Bypass Fault (B), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the controller,
reward 0 to the ground commander, and reward 0 to the specialist.

If the mission controller chooses Manual Calibration (M), the specialist then moves.

When the specialist moves, the specialist cannot tell whether the earlier path was
Repair Live (T) or Do Not Repair Live (Not T), Limited Test (L), Manual Calibration (M).
The specialist then chooses between Approve Patch (U) and Deny Patch (D).

If the path is Repair Live (T), Approve Patch (U), the outcome gives reward 3 to the controller,
reward 1 to the ground commander, and reward 1 to the specialist.
If the path is Repair Live (T), Deny Patch (D), the outcome gives reward 0 to all three players.

If the path is Do Not Repair Live (Not T), Limited Test (L), Manual Calibration (M), Approve Patch (U),
the outcome gives reward 0 to all three players.
If the path is Do Not Repair Live (Not T), Limited Test (L), Manual Calibration (M), Deny Patch (D),
the outcome gives reward 1 to each player.
