V5 (scholarship allocation)

Two students are deciding how to split a small scholarship fund.
Student 1 moves first and chooses whether to give all of it to Student 2, split it equally, or keep all of it.

If Student 1 offers all of it to Student 2, Student 2 then chooses whether to accept or reject.
If Student 2 accepts, the final return is 0 for Student 1 and 2 for Student 2.
If Student 2 rejects, both students end up with 0.

If Student 1 offers an equal split, Student 2 again chooses whether to accept or reject.
If Student 2 accepts, the final return is 1 for each student.
If Student 2 rejects, both students end up with 0.

If Student 1 offers nothing to Student 2, Student 2 again chooses whether to accept or reject.
If Student 2 accepts, the final return is 2 for Student 1 and 0 for Student 2.
If Student 2 rejects, both students end up with 0.
