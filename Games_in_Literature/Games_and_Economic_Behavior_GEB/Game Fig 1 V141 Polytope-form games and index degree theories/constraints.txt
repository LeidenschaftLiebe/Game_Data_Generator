Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints

Player 1 moves first. If Player 1 chooses L, Player 2 then chooses between l_1 and r_1. 
If Player 1 chooses R, Player 2 chooses between l and r, and then Player 1 moves again.
The two final Player 1 decision nodes after R_l and R_r are in the same infoset.
