V1 (battery-sharing rover game)

Two rover teams each start with 1 battery pack.
On a turn, a team may stop the process or continue it.
Continuing means giving up 1 of its own battery packs while sending 2 packs to the other team.
If someone stops, the teams keep whatever battery packs they currently hold.
If nobody stops by the fifth move, both sides end up with nothing.

Team 1 moves first.
Stopping right away gives final rewards of 1 and 1.
If Team 1 continues, the battery counts become 0 for Team 1 and 3 for Team 2.

Team 2 then chooses whether to stop or continue.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 0 and 3.
Continuing shifts the counts to 2 for Team 1 and 2 for Team 2.

Team 1 then moves again.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 2 and 2.
Continuing changes the counts to 1 for Team 1 and 4 for Team 2.

Team 2 then moves again.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 1 and 4.
Continuing changes the counts to 3 for Team 1 and 3 for Team 2.

Team 1 then makes the final move.
Stopping there gives final rewards of 3 and 3.
Continuing pushes the process past the fifth move, so both teams get 0.
