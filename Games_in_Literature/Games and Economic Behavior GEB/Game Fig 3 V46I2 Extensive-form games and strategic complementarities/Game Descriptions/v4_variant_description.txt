V4 — Optional Marketing Style

The campaign lead moves first and chooses HoldOff [No] or GoPublic [Yes].

After that, the media adviser chooses Optimistic [O] or Bold [B].

Then the campaign lead chooses Optimistic [O] or Bold [B].
At this later move, the campaign lead knows whether the first move was HoldOff [No] or GoPublic [Yes], but does not know which style the media adviser chose.

If the first move was HoldOff [No] and both later choices are Optimistic [O], the campaign lead gets 2 and the media adviser gets 1.
If the first move was HoldOff [No] and both later choices are Bold [B], the campaign lead gets 1 and the media adviser gets 2.
If the first move was HoldOff [No] and the later choices do not match, both get 0.

If the first move was GoPublic [Yes] and both later choices are Optimistic [O], the campaign lead gets 2 and the media adviser gets 1.
If the first move was GoPublic [Yes] and both later choices are Bold [B], the campaign lead gets 1 and the media adviser gets 2.
If the first move was GoPublic [Yes] and the later choices do not match, both get 0.
