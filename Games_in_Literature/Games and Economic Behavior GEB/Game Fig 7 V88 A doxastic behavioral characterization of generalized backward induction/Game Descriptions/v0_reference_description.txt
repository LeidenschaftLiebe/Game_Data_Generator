V0 — Forward and Backward Belief

There are two players.
Player 1 moves first and chooses between a, b, and c.

After that, Player 2 moves.
Player 2 cannot tell whether Player 1 chose a, b, or c.
Player 2 then chooses between d and e.

If the path is a, d, the outcome gives reward 2 to Player 1 and reward 1 to Player 2.
If the path is a, e, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1 and reward 0 to Player 2.

If the path is b, d, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1 and reward 1 to Player 2.
If the path is b, e, the outcome gives reward 2 to Player 1 and reward 0 to Player 2.

If the path is c, d, the outcome gives reward 0 to Player 1 and reward 0 to Player 2.
If the path is c, e, the outcome gives reward 0 to Player 1 and reward 1 to Player 2.
