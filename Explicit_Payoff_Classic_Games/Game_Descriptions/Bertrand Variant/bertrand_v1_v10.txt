1) Airline Tickets (Domestic Route)

Two airlines, AeroFly and SkyJet, sell identical economy tickets.
High price is $200 and Low price is $150, with unit cost $100.
Market demand at $200 is 20 thousand tickets.
Market demand at $150 is 30 thousand tickets.

If one sets Low and the other High, the Low-price airline sells 30 thousand tickets, while the High-price airline earns nothing.
