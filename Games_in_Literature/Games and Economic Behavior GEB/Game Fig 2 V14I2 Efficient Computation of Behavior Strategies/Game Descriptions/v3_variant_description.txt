V3 — Research Program under Two Experimental States

A random experimental state is selected first.

With probability 1/3, the project enters StateV [v].
With probability 2/3, it enters StateVPrime [v′].

If the project enters StateV [v], researcher 1 chooses CarefulTest [l] or AggressiveTest [r].

If researcher 1 chooses CarefulTest [l], both sides get 0.

If the project enters StateVPrime [v′], researcher 1 chooses ScaleUp [L] or EndNow [R].

If researcher 1 chooses EndNow [R], researcher 1 gets 3/2 and sponsor 2 gets -3/2.

If researcher 1 chooses AggressiveTest [r] at StateV [v], or ScaleUp [L] at StateVPrime [v′], sponsor 2 then chooses FundA [c] or FundB [d].

When sponsor 2 moves, sponsor 2 cannot tell whether the current situation came after AggressiveTest [r] at StateV [v] or after ScaleUp [L] at StateVPrime [v′].

If the current situation came after AggressiveTest [r] at StateV [v] and sponsor 2 chooses FundA [c], the payoffs are 3 and -3.
If the current situation came after AggressiveTest [r] at StateV [v] and sponsor 2 chooses FundB [d], the payoffs are -3 and 3.

If the current situation came after ScaleUp [L] at StateVPrime [v′] and sponsor 2 chooses FundA [c], the payoffs are -3 and 3.
If the current situation came after ScaleUp [L] at StateVPrime [v′] and sponsor 2 chooses FundB [d], the payoffs are 6 and -6.
