V5 — Transfer Decision

A club begins by choosing between Local Search (a) and International Search (b).
If Local Search (a) is chosen, the recruitment lead chooses between
End Search (c) and Escalate Review (d).

If End Search (c) is chosen, the process ends with reward 2 to the club,
reward 2 to recruitment, reward 1 to the scouting unit, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.
If Escalate Review (d) is chosen, the board reviewer moves later.

If International Search (b) is chosen first, the scouting unit chooses between
Escalate Review (e) and Pursue Directly (f).
If Pursue Directly (f) is chosen, the process ends with reward 1 to the club,
reward 0 to recruitment, reward 2 to the scouting unit, and reward 0 to the board reviewer.

When the board reviewer moves, the reviewer cannot tell whether the earlier path was
Local Search (a), Escalate Review (d) or International Search (b), Escalate Review (e).
The reviewer then chooses between Approve Deal (g), Revise Deal (h), and Block Deal (k).

If the path is Local Search (a), Escalate Review (d), Approve Deal (g),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the club, reward 1 to recruitment,
reward 0 to the scouting unit, and reward 0 to the reviewer.
If the path is Local Search (a), Escalate Review (d), Revise Deal (h),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the club, reward 3 to recruitment,
reward 1 to the scouting unit, and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is Local Search (a), Escalate Review (d), Block Deal (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the club, reward 4 to recruitment,
reward 0 to the scouting unit, and reward 1 to the reviewer.

If the path is International Search (b), Escalate Review (e), Approve Deal (g),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the club, reward 2 to recruitment,
reward 3 to the scouting unit, and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is International Search (b), Escalate Review (e), Revise Deal (h),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the club, reward 3 to recruitment,
reward 1 to the scouting unit, and reward 1 to the reviewer.
If the path is International Search (b), Escalate Review (e), Block Deal (k),
the outcome gives reward 4 to the club, reward 3 to recruitment,
reward 4 to the scouting unit, and reward 0 to the reviewer.
