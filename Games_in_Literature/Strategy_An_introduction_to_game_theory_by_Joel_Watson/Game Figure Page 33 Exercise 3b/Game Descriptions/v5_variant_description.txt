V5 (fashion brand expansion)

A fashion brand first decides whether to enter a new segment or stay out.
If it stays out, the interaction ends immediately and each brand gets 1.

If it enters, the competing brand then decides whether to enter too or stay out.
If the competing brand stays out, both sides end up with -1.
If the competing brand also enters, the first brand then chooses between an aggressive move and a more balanced move.
The aggressive move gives 4 to the first brand and 0 to the competitor.
The balanced move gives 3 to the first brand and 2 to the competitor.
