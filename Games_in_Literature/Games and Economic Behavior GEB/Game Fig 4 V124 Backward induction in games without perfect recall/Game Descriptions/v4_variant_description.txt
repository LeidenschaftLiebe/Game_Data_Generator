V4 [Orbital debris mission]

A mission director and a flight planner are deciding how to clear dangerous orbital debris.

The mission director moves first and chooses between HoldMission (T) and ActiveSweep (B).

If the mission director chooses HoldMission (T), then the flight planner chooses between UpperArc (U) and DeepArc (D).
If the flight planner chooses UpperArc (U), this gives 2 to the mission director, and 2 to the flight planner.
If the flight planner chooses DeepArc (D), this gives 0 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight planner.

If the mission director chooses ActiveSweep (B), then the flight planner chooses between LeftOrbit (L) and RightOrbit (R).

If the flight planner chooses RightOrbit (R), the result is immediate.
This gives 1 to the mission director, and 1 to the flight planner.

If the flight planner chooses LeftOrbit (L), the flight planner later chooses between UpperArc (U) and DeepArc (D).
If the flight planner chooses UpperArc (U), this gives 0 to the mission director, and 0 to the flight planner.
If the flight planner chooses DeepArc (D), this gives 3 to the mission director, and 3 to the flight planner.

The two UpperArc (U) or DeepArc (D) nodes are linked, so the flight planner cannot tell whether 
that later choice follows HoldMission (T) or follows ActiveSweep (B) and then LeftOrbit (L).
