V3 — Fashion Launch

A brand lead is deciding how to handle a collection launch.
The brand lead first chooses between Launch This Week (T) and Not This Week (Not T).

If the brand lead chooses Launch This Week (T), the fashion critic moves later.
If the brand lead chooses Not This Week (Not T), the retail manager moves next and chooses between
Soft Prep (L) and Store Rollout (R).

If the retail manager chooses Store Rollout (R), the process ends immediately with reward 1 to the brand lead,
reward 2 to the retail manager, and reward 2 to the critic.

If the retail manager chooses Soft Prep (L), the brand lead moves again and chooses between
Box Stock (B) and Make Tweaks (M).
If the brand lead chooses Box Stock (B), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the brand lead,
reward 0 to the retail manager, and reward 0 to the critic.

If the brand lead chooses Make Tweaks (M), the critic then moves.

When the critic moves, the critic cannot tell whether the earlier path was
Launch This Week (T) or Not This Week (Not T), Soft Prep (L), Make Tweaks (M).
The critic then chooses between Celebrate It (U) and Pan It (D).

If the path is Launch This Week (T), Celebrate It (U), the outcome gives reward 3 to the brand lead,
reward 1 to the retail manager, and reward 1 to the critic.
If the path is Launch This Week (T), Pan It (D), the outcome gives reward 0 to all three players.

If the path is Not This Week (Not T), Soft Prep (L), Make Tweaks (M), Celebrate It (U),
the outcome gives reward 0 to all three players.
If the path is Not This Week (Not T), Soft Prep (L), Make Tweaks (M), Pan It (D),
the outcome gives reward 1 to each player.
