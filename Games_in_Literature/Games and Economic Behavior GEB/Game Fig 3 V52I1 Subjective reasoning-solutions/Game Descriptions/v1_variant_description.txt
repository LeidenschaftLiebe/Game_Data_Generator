V1 — Product Launch Review

Alice is a product director deciding how to handle a new feature.
She first chooses DirectLaunch [l], RevisePlan [m], or CancelTrack [r].

If Alice chooses RevisePlan [m] or CancelTrack [r], the file goes to Bob in internal review.
Bob must act without knowing which of those two earlier choices Alice made.
Bob then chooses Approve [L] or Block [R].

If Alice chooses DirectLaunch [l], the case goes to Emma in communications.
If Alice chooses RevisePlan [m] and Bob chooses Approve [L], the case also goes to Emma.

When Emma receives the case, she cannot tell whether it came from a direct launch or from a revised plan that Bob approved.
Emma then chooses CampaignA [p] or CampaignB [q].

If Alice chose DirectLaunch [l], then whether Emma chooses CampaignA [p] or CampaignB [q], Alice gets 0, Bob gets 2, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose RevisePlan [m] and Bob chose Approve [L], then whether Emma chooses CampaignA [p] or CampaignB [q], Alice gets 2, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose RevisePlan [m] and Bob chose Block [R], then Alice gets 1, Bob gets 1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose CancelTrack [r] and Bob chose Approve [L], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets -1, and Emma gets 0.

If Alice chose CancelTrack [r] and Bob chose Block [R], then Alice gets -1, Bob gets 0, and Emma gets 0.
