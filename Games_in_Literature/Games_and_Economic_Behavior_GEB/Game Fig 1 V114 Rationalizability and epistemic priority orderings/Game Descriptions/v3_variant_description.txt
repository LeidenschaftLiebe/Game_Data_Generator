V3 [Smuggling checkpoint]

A courier first chooses between StayLegal and OfferBribe.

If the courier chooses StayLegal, the situation ends there.
This gives 0 to the courier, and 0 to the checkpoint officer.

If the courier chooses OfferBribe, the checkpoint officer chooses between TurnInCourier and AcceptBribe.

If the checkpoint officer chooses TurnInCourier, this gives -2 to the courier, and 0 to the checkpoint officer.

If the checkpoint officer chooses AcceptBribe, the courier then chooses between ConfessAfterward and MoveCargo.

If the courier chooses ConfessAfterward, this gives -1 to the courier, and -3 to the checkpoint officer.

If the courier chooses MoveCargo, this gives 1 to the courier, and 1 to the checkpoint officer.
