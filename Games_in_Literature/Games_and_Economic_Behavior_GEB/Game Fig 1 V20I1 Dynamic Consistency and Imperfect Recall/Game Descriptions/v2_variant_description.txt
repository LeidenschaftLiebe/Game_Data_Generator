V2 — Film Production under Hidden Audience Response

A producer first chooses SellNow [T] or MakeFilm [G].

If the producer chooses SellNow [T], the process ends immediately with reward 3.

If the producer chooses MakeFilm [G], early audience reaction is warm [l] or cold [r], each with probability 1/2.

If the reaction is warm [l], the producer reaches Stage1 [d1].
If the reaction is cold [r], the producer reaches Stage2 [d2].
At Stage1 [d1] or Stage2 [d2], the producer chooses WrapUp [E] or KeepInvesting [D].
Choosing WrapUp [E] gives reward 2.
Choosing KeepInvesting [D] leads later to FinalEdit [d3].

When making a move at FinalEdit [d3], the producer cannot tell whether the earlier audience reaction was warm [l] or cold [r], 
and cannot tell whether the current situation came from Stage1 [d1] or from Stage2 [d2].

If the producer reaches FinalEdit [d3] after warm [l] and chooses CutLeft [L], the reward is 5.
If the producer reaches FinalEdit [d3] after warm [l] and chooses CutRight [R], the reward is 0.

If the producer reaches FinalEdit [d3] after cold [r] and chooses CutLeft [L], the reward is 0.
If the producer reaches FinalEdit [d3] after cold [r] and chooses CutRight [R], the reward is 6.
