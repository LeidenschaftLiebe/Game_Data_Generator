V2 [Research project]

At the start, a hidden draw determines the planning type of the lead researcher and the coauthor.
The lead researcher’s type is 01, 11, or 21, each with probability 1/3.
The coauthor’s type is 02 with probability 1/10 and 12 with probability 9/10.

So the hidden type-combination probabilities are:
(01,02) = 1/30, (01,12) = 3/10, (11,02) = 1/30, (11,12) = 3/10, (21,02) = 1/30, and (21,12) = 3/10.

The lead researcher knows only the lead researcher’s own type, not the coauthor’s type.
The coauthor knows only the coauthor’s own type, not the lead researcher’s type.

The lead researcher first chooses between StartProject (Invest1) and DropIt (Out1).

If the lead researcher chooses DropIt (Out1), the result is 5, 0.

If the lead researcher chooses StartProject (Invest1), the coauthor chooses between CommitTime (Invest2) and StepAway (Out2).

When making that choice, the coauthor has observed StartProject (Invest1),
but still does not know whether the lead researcher is type 01, 11, or 21.

If the coauthor chooses StepAway (Out2), the result is 0, 6.

If the coauthor chooses CommitTime (Invest2), the lead researcher chooses between FinishTogether (Invest3) and ExitLate (Out3).

When making that last choice, the lead researcher has observed both earlier realized actions,
but still does not know whether the coauthor is type 02 or 12.

At the last layer, every hidden type branch uses the same two final results.
FinishTogether (Invest3) gives 8, 8, and ExitLate (Out3) gives 7, 0.
