V1 (music festival planning)

A festival organizer first chooses between lineup plan A, lineup plan B, and lineup plan C.
If the organizer chooses lineup plan C, the planning process ends immediately and each side gets a final reward of 1.

If the organizer chooses lineup plan A, a headline sponsor then chooses between response X and response Y.
If the sponsor chooses response X after lineup plan A, the organizer gets 3 and the sponsor gets 0.
If the sponsor chooses response Y after lineup plan A, the organizer gets 0 and the sponsor gets 3.

If the organizer chooses lineup plan B, the sponsor again chooses between response X and response Y.
At that point, the sponsor cannot tell whether the organizer had selected lineup plan A or lineup plan B.
If the sponsor chooses response X after lineup plan B, the organizer gets 0 and the sponsor gets 3.
If the sponsor chooses response Y after lineup plan B, the organizer gets 3 and the sponsor gets 0.
