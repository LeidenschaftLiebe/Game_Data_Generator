V1 — Product Escalation

A company begins by choosing between Shut Plan (a), Internal Escalation (b), and External Escalation (c).
If it chooses Shut Plan (a), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the company,
reward 0 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the reviewer.

If the company chooses Internal Escalation (b) or External Escalation (c), the operations lead moves next.
The operations lead cannot tell which of those two escalation routes was chosen.
The operations lead then chooses between Route D (d) and Route E (e).

After that, the reviewer moves.
The reviewer cannot tell which earlier route was taken.
The reviewer then chooses between Approve Fast (s), Request Changes (t), and Hold Back (u).

If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route D (d), Approve Fast (s),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the company, reward 1 to the operations lead, and reward 1 to the reviewer.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route D (d), Request Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the company, reward 1 to the operations lead, and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route D (d), Hold Back (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the company, reward 2 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the reviewer.

If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route E (e), Approve Fast (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the company, reward 0 to the operations lead, and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route E (e), Request Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the company, reward 0 to the operations lead, and reward 1 to the reviewer.
If the path is Internal Escalation (b), Route E (e), Hold Back (u),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the company, reward 2 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the reviewer.

If the path is External Escalation (c), Route D (d), Approve Fast (s),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the company, reward 1 to the operations lead, and reward 1 to the reviewer.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route D (d), Request Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the company, reward 1 to the operations lead, and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route D (d), Hold Back (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the company, reward 2 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the reviewer.

If the path is External Escalation (c), Route E (e), Approve Fast (s),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the company, reward 0 to the operations lead, and reward 2 to the reviewer.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route E (e), Request Changes (t),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the company, reward 0 to the operations lead, and reward 1 to the reviewer.
If the path is External Escalation (c), Route E (e), Hold Back (u),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the company, reward 2 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the reviewer.
