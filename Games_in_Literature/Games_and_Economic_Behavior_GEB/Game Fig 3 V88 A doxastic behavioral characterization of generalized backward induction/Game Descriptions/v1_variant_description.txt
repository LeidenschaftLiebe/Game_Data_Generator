V1 — Product Pipeline

A company is deciding whether to stop a product plan or send it down an internal review path.
The company moves first and chooses between Internal Review (a₁) and Cancel Plan (b₁).

If the company chooses Cancel Plan (b₁), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the company,
reward 2 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the technical advisor.

If the company chooses Internal Review (a₁), the operations lead moves next and chooses between
Team Review (a₂) and Expert Review (b₂).

If the operations lead chooses Team Review (a₂), the technical advisor chooses between
Approve Early (a₃) and Push Harder (b₃).
If Approve Early (a₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the company,
reward 1 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the advisor.
If Push Harder (b₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 3 to the company,
reward 3 to the operations lead, and reward 1 to the advisor.

If the operations lead chooses Expert Review (b₂), the technical advisor chooses between
Limited Greenlight (c₃) and Full Greenlight (d₃).
If Limited Greenlight (c₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the company,
reward 2 to the operations lead, and reward 1 to the advisor.
If Full Greenlight (d₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 4 to the company,
reward 0 to the operations lead, and reward 0 to the advisor.
