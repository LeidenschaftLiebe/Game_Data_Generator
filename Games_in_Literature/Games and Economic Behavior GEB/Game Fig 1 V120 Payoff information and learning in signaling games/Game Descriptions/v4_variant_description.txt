V4 [Wildfire drone operation]

A systems check first determines whether the drone team is HighBattery or LowBattery.
HighBattery happens with probability 9/10, and LowBattery happens with probability 1/10.

The drone operator then chooses between ReturnBase and EnterSmoke.

If the drone operator chooses ReturnBase, the flight ends there.
This gives 0 to the operator, and 0 to the analyst.

If the drone operator chooses EnterSmoke, the analyst chooses between ClimbPath, DropPath, and FlankPath.
The analyst cannot tell whether the drone team is HighBattery or LowBattery.

If the drone team is HighBattery and the analyst chooses ClimbPath, this gives 2 to the operator, and 1 to the analyst.
If the drone team is HighBattery and the analyst chooses DropPath, this gives -1 to the operator, and 0 to the analyst.
If the drone team is HighBattery and the analyst chooses FlankPath, this gives 1 to the operator, and -1 to the analyst.

If the drone team is LowBattery and the analyst chooses ClimbPath, this gives 1 to the operator, and 0 to the analyst.
If the drone team is LowBattery and the analyst chooses DropPath, this gives -1 to the operator, and 1 to the analyst.
If the drone team is LowBattery and the analyst chooses FlankPath, this gives 3 to the operator, and -1 to the analyst.
