V3 (fashion week production)

A creative director first decides whether to keep a fashion season modest or push it into a major production cycle.
If the director keeps the modest plan, the season ends immediately and the final reward is 2 for the creative director, 2 for the production manager, and 2 for the show stylist.

If the director pushes for the major cycle, the production manager then chooses among setup A, setup B, and setup C.
If the production manager chooses setup C, the season ends immediately with a final reward of 4 for the creative director, 3 for the production manager, and 1 for the show stylist.

If the production manager chooses setup A, the stylist then chooses between presentation X and presentation Y.
Presentation X after setup A leads to a final reward of 3 for the creative director, 2 for the production manager, and 1 for the stylist.
Presentation Y after setup A leads to a final reward of 5 for the creative director, 0 for the production manager, and 0 for the stylist.

If the production manager chooses setup B, the stylist again chooses between presentation X and presentation Y.
At that point, the stylist cannot tell whether the season reached this stage after setup A or setup B.
Presentation X after setup B leads to a final reward of 1 for the creative director, 2 for the production manager, and 6 for the stylist.
Presentation Y after setup B leads to a final reward of 7 for the creative director, 5 for the production manager, and 5 for the stylist.
