Game Description 1: A Winter Survival Game

Under the bright lights of a winter survival game, every decision shapes the future.

**Player1** stands ready to unleash either:
- A masterful **igloo shelter**, or
- A daring **snowshoe sprint**.

Meanwhile, **Player2** prepares their countermove:
- A nimble **flare signal**, or
- A cunning **ice fishing**.

Every pairing of choices leads to different consequences:

- If Player1 chooses **igloo shelter**:
  - And Player2 responds with **flare signal**, the resulting payoff is (3, -3).
  - And Player2 responds with **ice fishing**, the resulting payoff is (2, -2).

- If Player1 chooses **snowshoe sprint**:
  - And Player2 responds with **flare signal**, the resulting payoff is (3, -3).
  - And Player2 responds with **ice fishing**, the resulting payoff is (5, -5).

The sharpest strategy and the boldest timing will claim victory.
