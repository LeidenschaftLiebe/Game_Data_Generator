V2 (space station storage game)

A crew member first decides whether to place a tool on the upper rack or on the deck.
A second crew member sees that choice.
Then the second crew member decides whether to place his own tool on the upper rack or on the deck.

If the second crew member places his tool on the upper rack, the round ends and all three people get 0.
If he places it on the deck, a third crew member has to guess whether the first tool is on the upper rack.
The third crew member answers yes or no.
That third crew member can observe the second move, but not the first one.

So when the guess is made, the third crew member does not know which earlier placement by the first crew member actually happened.

If the first tool is on the upper rack and the guess is yes, the guess is correct.
The final reward is -1 for the first crew member, 0 for the second, and 1 for the third.
If the first tool is on the upper rack and the guess is no, the guess is wrong.
The final reward is 1 for the first crew member, 0 for the second, and -1 for the third.

If the first tool is on the deck and the guess is yes, the guess is wrong.
The final reward is 1 for the first crew member, 0 for the second, and -1 for the third.
If the first tool is on the deck and the guess is no, the guess is correct.
The final reward is -1 for the first crew member, 0 for the second, and 1 for the third.
