V2 — Museum Service Corridors

A staff manager first sends the technician to Wing A or Wing B.

At the first service turn, the technician chooses TakeFirstLeft [left1] or TakeFirstRight [right1].

At the second service turn, the technician chooses TakeSecondLeft [left2] or TakeSecondRight [right2].

From Wing A, choosing TakeFirstLeft [left1] always reaches a locked corridor.
From Wing A, choosing TakeFirstRight [right1] and then TakeSecondRight [right2] also reaches a locked corridor.
From Wing A, choosing TakeFirstRight [right1] and then TakeSecondLeft [left2] reaches the final door.

From Wing B, choosing TakeFirstLeft [left1] always reaches a locked corridor.
From Wing B, choosing TakeFirstRight [right1] and then TakeSecondRight [right2] reaches a locked corridor.
From Wing B, choosing TakeFirstRight [right1] and then TakeSecondLeft [left2] reaches the final door.

At the final door, the technician remembers the earlier turns but cannot tell whether the route began in Wing A or Wing B.

Then the technician chooses LeftDoor [left3] or RightDoor [right3].

If the route began in Wing A, LeftDoor [left3] leads to the target room and RightDoor [right3] is blocked.
If the route began in Wing B, LeftDoor [left3] is blocked and RightDoor [right3] leads to the target room.

Reaching the target room gives reward 1.
Any blocked route gives reward 0.
