V1 [Startup partnership]

A startup founder and a distribution partner are about to work together on a launch.

The distribution partner moves in a way that represents the risky environment and chooses between 
GrabAdvantage (Exploit), WalkAway (Exit), and Cooperate (Collaborate).

At the same time, the founder chooses between CancelDeal (Exit), ProtectTerms (Defensive), and ShareUpside (Generous).
The founder must choose without knowing which move the partner picked.

If the partner chooses GrabAdvantage (Exploit) and the founder chooses CancelDeal (Exit), both get 0, 0.
If the partner chooses GrabAdvantage (Exploit) and the founder chooses ProtectTerms (Defensive), this gives the partner 0, and the founder 10.
If the partner chooses GrabAdvantage (Exploit) and the founder chooses ShareUpside (Generous), this gives the partner 9, and the founder 1.

If the partner chooses WalkAway (Exit), the result is always 0, 0.

If the partner chooses Cooperate (Collaborate) and the founder chooses CancelDeal (Exit), both get 0, 0.
If the partner chooses Cooperate (Collaborate) and the founder chooses ProtectTerms (Defensive), this gives the partner 1, and the founder 9.
If the partner chooses Cooperate (Collaborate) and the founder chooses ShareUpside (Generous), this gives the partner 5, and the founder 5.
