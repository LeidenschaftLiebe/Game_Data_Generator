V1 (museum gallery game)

A museum is running a live puzzle show for visitors.
Mia quietly hides a prize in Gallery A or Gallery B before the audience enters.
The host sees where the prize is hidden.
Then Jordan comes in, and the host says either “North” or “South.”
After hearing that cue, Jordan picks one of the two galleries.
If Jordan picks the correct gallery, Jordan gets 100 and Mia gets 0.
If Jordan picks the wrong gallery, Mia gets 100 and Jordan gets 0.
The host gets 10 whenever Jordan picks Gallery A and 0 whenever Jordan picks Gallery B.

If Mia hides the prize in Gallery A and the host says North, Jordan’s choice settles the result.
Picking Gallery A gives final rewards of 0 for Mia, 10 for the host, and 100 for Jordan.
Picking Gallery B gives final rewards of 100 for Mia, 0 for the host, and 0 for Jordan.

If Mia hides the prize in Gallery A and the host says South, the same two gallery choices remain.
Picking Gallery A gives final rewards of 0 for Mia, 10 for the host, and 100 for Jordan.
Picking Gallery B gives final rewards of 100 for Mia, 0 for the host, and 0 for Jordan.

If Mia hides the prize in Gallery B and the host says North, Jordan chooses between the two galleries.
Picking Gallery A gives final rewards of 100 for Mia, 10 for the host, and 0 for Jordan.
Picking Gallery B gives final rewards of 0 for Mia, 0 for the host, and 100 for Jordan.

If Mia hides the prize in Gallery B and the host says South, Jordan again chooses between the two galleries.
Picking Gallery A gives final rewards of 100 for Mia, 10 for the host, and 0 for Jordan.
Picking Gallery B gives final rewards of 0 for Mia, 0 for the host, and 100 for Jordan.
