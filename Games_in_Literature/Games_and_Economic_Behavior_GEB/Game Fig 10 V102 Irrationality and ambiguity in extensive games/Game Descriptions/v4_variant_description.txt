V4 — Cybersecurity Response

A firm is deciding how to respond to a security threat.

The firm moves first and chooses between Shut System Down (O),
Patch Internally (L), and Public Rollout Fix (R).

If the firm chooses Shut System Down (O), the matter ends immediately.
The firm gets reward 2 and the outside auditor gets reward 6.

If the firm chooses Patch Internally (L) or Public Rollout Fix (R),
the outside auditor moves next.

The outside auditor cannot tell whether the firm chose
Patch Internally (L) or Public Rollout Fix (R).

The outside auditor then chooses between Cautious Review (A)
and Cooperative Review (B).

If the path is Patch Internally (L), Cautious Review (A),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the firm and reward 1 to the auditor.

If the path is Patch Internally (L), Cooperative Review (B),
the outcome gives reward 3 to the firm and reward 2 to the auditor.

If the path is Public Rollout Fix (R), Cautious Review (A),
the outcome gives reward -1 to the firm and reward 3 to the auditor.

If the path is Public Rollout Fix (R), Cooperative Review (B),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the firm and reward 2 to the auditor.
