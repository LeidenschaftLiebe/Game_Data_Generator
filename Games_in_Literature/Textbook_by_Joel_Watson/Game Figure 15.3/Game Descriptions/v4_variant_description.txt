V4 (esports tournament design)

A league organizer moves first and chooses between the Upper-bracket format and the Double-elimination format.

If the organizer chooses the Upper-bracket format, a team owner then chooses between Arena setup A and Arena setup B.
If the team owner chooses Arena setup A, the organizer then chooses between an Evening final and a Fan-event final.
The Evening final gives a final reward of 3 for the organizer, 3 for the team owner, and 6 for the sponsor.
The Fan-event final gives a final reward of 1 for the organizer, 5 for the team owner, and 7 for the sponsor.

If the team owner chooses Arena setup B under the Upper-bracket format, the sponsor then chooses between a Global package and a Highlight package.
The Global package gives a final reward of 2 for the organizer, 0 for the team owner, and 3 for the sponsor.
The Highlight package gives a final reward of 7 for the organizer, 7 for the team owner, and 2 for the sponsor.

If the organizer chooses the Double-elimination format, the team owner then chooses between Arena setup C and Arena setup D.
If the team owner chooses Arena setup D, the final reward is 6 for the organizer, 2 for the team owner, and 4 for the sponsor.
If the team owner chooses Arena setup C, the sponsor again chooses between the Global package and the Highlight package.
At that point, the sponsor cannot tell whether it is responding after Arena setup B or Arena setup C.
If the sponsor chooses the Global package after Arena setup C, the final reward is 0 for the organizer, 6 for the team owner, and 1 for the sponsor.
If the sponsor chooses the Highlight package after Arena setup C, the final reward is 8 for the organizer, 6 for the team owner, and 0 for the sponsor.
