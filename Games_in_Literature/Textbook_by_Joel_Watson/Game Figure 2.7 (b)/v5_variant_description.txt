V5 (hotel booking platforms)

Two hotel-booking platforms are competing for the same travelers.
Each platform chooses whether to keep booking fees high or low, and each does so without seeing the other platform’s choice first.
If both platforms keep fees high, each earns 1 million.
If Platform 1 keeps fees high while Platform 2 lowers its fees, Platform 1 earns nothing and Platform 2 earns 2 million.
If Platform 1 lowers its fees while Platform 2 keeps them high, Platform 1 earns 2 million and Platform 2 earns nothing.
If both platforms lower fees, neither gains a strong advantage and each earns 0.5 million.
