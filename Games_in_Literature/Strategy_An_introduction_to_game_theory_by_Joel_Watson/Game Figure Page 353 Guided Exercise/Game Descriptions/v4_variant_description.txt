V4 (sports recovery decision)

An athlete moves first and chooses between the light-training plan and the delayed-treatment plan.

If the athlete chooses the light-training plan, the medical staff then chooses between treatment A and treatment B.
If the medical staff chooses treatment A after the light-training plan, the final reward is 0 for the athlete and 4 for the medical staff.
If the medical staff chooses treatment B after the light-training plan, the final reward is 4 for the athlete and 0 for the medical staff.

If the athlete chooses the delayed-treatment plan, a random medical development occurs.
With probability 0.5, the situation moves to branch P.
With probability 0.5, the situation moves to branch Q.

If branch P occurs, the medical staff chooses between treatment A and treatment B.
The medical staff cannot tell whether this choice comes after the light-training plan or after branch P.
If the medical staff chooses treatment A after branch P, the final reward is 4 for the athlete and 0 for the medical staff.
If the medical staff chooses treatment B after branch P, the final reward is 0 for the athlete and 4 for the medical staff.

If branch Q occurs, the medical staff chooses between treatment X and treatment Y.
If the medical staff chooses treatment X after branch Q, the final reward is 2 for the athlete and 2 for the medical staff.
If the medical staff chooses treatment Y after branch Q, the final reward is 0 for the athlete and 0 for the medical staff.
