V5 [Crowdfunding delivery]

A backer and a project creator are deciding whether to proceed with a pledge.
The creator moves first and sends one of three messages: NoPromise (m(E)), WillDeliverReward (m(S)), or WillKeepFunds (m(K)).

After seeing the message, the backer chooses between Pledge (I) and KeepMoney (N).
If the backer chooses KeepMoney (N), the process ends immediately and the result is 2 for the backer and 0 for the creator.

If the backer chooses Pledge (I), the creator then chooses between DeliverReward (S) and KeepFunds (K).
After NoPromise (m(E)), DeliverReward (S) gives 4 to the backer and 4 to the creator, 
while KeepFunds (K) gives 0 to the backer and 8 to the creator.

After WillDeliverReward (m(S)), DeliverReward (S) gives 4 to the backer and 4 to the creator.
If the creator instead chooses KeepFunds (K), the creator is fined for dishonest communication.
With f = 2, that result is 0 for the backer and 6 for the creator.

After WillKeepFunds (m(K)), KeepFunds (K) gives 0 to the backer and 8 to the creator.
If the creator instead chooses DeliverReward (S), the creator is fined because the later action does not match the message.
With f = 2, that result is 4 for the backer and 2 for the creator.
