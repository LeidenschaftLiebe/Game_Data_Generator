V3 — Emergency Aid

A community organizer and a volunteer are deciding on support for a local relief effort.

The organizer moves first and chooses between Limited Aid and Full Aid.

If the organizer chooses Limited Aid, later events split into three cases,
with probabilities 0.01, 0.98, and 0.01.

If case 1 happens after Limited Aid, the volunteer chooses between
Accept Quietly and Object.

If the path is Limited Aid, 1, Accept Quietly, the outcome is
Organizer: $4.50 and Volunteer: $2.50.

If the path is Limited Aid, 1, Object, the outcome is
Organizer: $3.00 and Volunteer: $2.00.

If case 2 happens after Limited Aid, the outcome is
Organizer: $4.50 and Volunteer: $2.50.

If case 3 happens after Limited Aid, the outcome is again
Organizer: $4.50 and Volunteer: $2.50.

If the organizer chooses Full Aid, the same three cases follow
with probabilities 0.01, 0.98, and 0.01.

If case 1 happens after Full Aid, the outcome is
Organizer: $4.00 and Volunteer: $4.00.

If case 2 happens after Full Aid, the outcome is
Organizer: $4.00 and Volunteer: $4.00.

If case 3 happens after Full Aid, the volunteer chooses between
Acknowledge and Volunteer Extra Time.

If the path is Full Aid, 3, Acknowledge, the outcome is
Organizer: $4.00 and Volunteer: $4.00.

If the path is Full Aid, 3, Volunteer Extra Time, the outcome is
Organizer: $5.50 and Volunteer: $3.50.
