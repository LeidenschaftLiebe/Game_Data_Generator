V5 — Wildlife Protection

A park director is deciding how to organize a conservation response.
The park director moves first and chooses between Habitat Plan (a1) and Patrol Plan (b1).

If the park director chooses Habitat Plan (a1), the operations ranger moves next and chooses between
Deploy Survey Team (a2) and Cancel Survey Team (b2).
If the operations ranger chooses Deploy Survey Team (a2), the field biologist then chooses between
Immediate Tagging (a3) and Observation Only (b3).
If the path is a1, a2, a3, the outcome gives reward 2 to the director, reward 2 to the ranger, and reward 2 to the biologist.
If the path is a1, a2, b3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the director, reward 1 to the ranger, and reward 1 to the biologist.

If the operations ranger chooses Cancel Survey Team (b2), the game ends immediately.
The outcome then gives reward 1 to the director, reward 0 to the ranger, and reward 0 to the biologist.

If the park director chooses Patrol Plan (b1), the field biologist moves next and chooses between
River Patrol (c2), Cliff Patrol (d2), and No Patrol (e2).
If the path is b1, c2, the outcome gives reward 3 to the director, reward 0 to the ranger, and reward 1 to the biologist.
If the path is b1, d2, the outcome gives reward 0 to the director, reward 0 to the ranger, and reward 2 to the biologist.
If the path is b1, e2, the outcome gives reward 0 to all three players.
