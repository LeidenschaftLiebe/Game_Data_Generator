V3 (museum network project)

A museum director first chooses between project A and project B.
If the director chooses project B, the project ends immediately with a final reward of 5 for the director and 4 for the donor liaison.

If the director chooses project A, the donor liaison then chooses between partnership track C and partnership track D.
If the donor liaison chooses partnership track D, the director then chooses between exhibit plan I and exhibit plan J.
Exhibit plan I gives a final reward of 9 to the director and 2 to the donor liaison.
Exhibit plan J gives a final reward of 4 to the director and 5 to the donor liaison.

If the donor liaison chooses partnership track C, the director then chooses between gallery plan G and gallery plan H.
Gallery plan H gives a final reward of 7 to the director and 3 to the donor liaison.
Gallery plan G leads to one more choice by the donor liaison between sponsorship option E and sponsorship option F.

If the donor liaison chooses sponsorship option E, the director then chooses between presentation K and presentation L.
Presentation K gives a final reward of 2 to the director and 4 to the donor liaison.
Presentation L gives a final reward of 3 to the director and 6 to the donor liaison.

If the donor liaison chooses sponsorship option F, the director then chooses between presentation M and presentation N.
Presentation M gives a final reward of 6 to the director and 7 to the donor liaison.
Presentation N gives a final reward of 8 to the director and 1 to the donor liaison.
