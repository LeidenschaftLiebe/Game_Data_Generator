V5 (wildlife documentary production)

A producer moves first and chooses between the Uplands filming plan and the Delta filming plan.

If the producer chooses the Uplands plan, a field coordinator then chooses between Crew A and Crew B.
If the coordinator chooses Crew A, the producer then chooses between an Expedition cut and a Family cut.
The Expedition cut gives a final reward of 3 for the producer, 3 for the coordinator, and 6 for the distributor.
The Family cut gives a final reward of 1 for the producer, 5 for the coordinator, and 7 for the distributor.

If the coordinator chooses Crew B under the Uplands plan, the distributor then chooses between a Global release and a Heritage release.
The Global release gives a final reward of 2 for the producer, 0 for the coordinator, and 3 for the distributor.
The Heritage release gives a final reward of 7 for the producer, 7 for the coordinator, and 2 for the distributor.

If the producer chooses the Delta plan, the coordinator then chooses between Crew C and Crew D.
If the coordinator chooses Crew D, the final reward is 6 for the producer, 2 for the coordinator, and 4 for the distributor.
If the coordinator chooses Crew C, the distributor again chooses between the Global release and the Heritage release.
At that point, the distributor cannot tell whether the choice came after Crew B or Crew C.
If the distributor chooses the Global release after Crew C, the final reward is 0 for the producer, 6 for the coordinator, and 1 for the distributor.
If the distributor chooses the Heritage release after Crew C, the final reward is 8 for the producer, 6 for the coordinator, and 0 for the distributor.
