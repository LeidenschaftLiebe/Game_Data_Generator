Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:
1) Check the move order: chance moves first, then the Expert (Player 1), then the Decision Maker (Player 2).
2) Check the chance probabilities: Gamma0 and Gamma1 each occur with probability 1/2.
3) Check that the Decision Maker has one information set after recommendation 0 and one information set after recommendation 1, 
and in each case observes the recommendation but not the state.
4) Check the anchor payoff: the outcome after Gamma1 -> 1 -> a1 is (0, 0).
