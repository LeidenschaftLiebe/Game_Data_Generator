V4 (sports media rights strategy)

A media executive first chooses between the worldwide rights push and the regional rights push.

If the executive chooses the worldwide rights push, the negotiation partner then chooses between package Alpha and package Beta.
If the partner chooses package Alpha, the executive then chooses between broadcast format X and broadcast format Y.
When making that choice, the executive cannot tell whether the partner previously selected package Alpha or package Beta.
Broadcast format X after package Alpha gives a final reward of 3 to the executive and 0 to the partner.
Broadcast format Y after package Alpha gives a final reward of 8 to the executive and 5 to the partner.

If the partner chooses package Beta, the executive again chooses between broadcast format X and broadcast format Y at that same hidden-information point.
Broadcast format X after package Beta gives a final reward of 4 to the executive and 6 to the partner.
Broadcast format Y after package Beta gives a final reward of 2 to the executive and 1 to the partner.

If the executive chooses the regional rights push, the partner then chooses between contract C and contract D.
Contract C gives a final reward of 6 to the executive and 4 to the partner.
Contract D gives a final reward of 3 to the executive and 2 to the partner.
