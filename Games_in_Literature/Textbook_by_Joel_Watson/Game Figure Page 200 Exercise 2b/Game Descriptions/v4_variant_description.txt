V4 (museum expansion)

A museum director first chooses between the urban-annex plan and the digital-annex plan.

If the director chooses the urban-annex plan, the operations chief then chooses between funding model A and funding model B.
If the operations chief chooses funding model A, the final reward is 2 for the director and 3 for the operations chief.
If the operations chief chooses funding model B, the final reward is 5 for the director and 4 for the operations chief.

If the director chooses the digital-annex plan, the operations chief then chooses between staffing plan C and staffing plan D.
If the operations chief chooses staffing plan D, the final reward is 0 for the director and 2 for the operations chief.
If the operations chief chooses staffing plan C, the director then chooses between exhibit format E and exhibit format F.
Exhibit format E gives a final reward of 6 to the director and 2 to the operations chief.
Exhibit format F gives a final reward of 2 to the director and 6 to the operations chief.
