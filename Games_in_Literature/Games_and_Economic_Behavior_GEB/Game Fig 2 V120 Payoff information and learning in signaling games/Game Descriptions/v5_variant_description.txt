V5 [Desert convoy]

A hidden evaluation first determines whether the convoy leader is ToughLeader or HesitantLeader.
ToughLeader happens with probability 9/10, and HesitantLeader happens with probability 1/10.

The leader then chooses between DustRoute and StoneRoute.

After seeing that choice, the border scout chooses between Block and StandAside.

If the leader is ToughLeader and chooses DustRoute, then Block gives 0 to the leader, and 0 to the scout.
If the leader is ToughLeader and chooses DustRoute, then StandAside gives 2 to the leader, and 1 to the scout.

If the leader is ToughLeader and chooses StoneRoute, then Block gives 1 to the leader, and 0 to the scout.
If the leader is ToughLeader and chooses StoneRoute, then StandAside gives 3 to the leader, and 1 to the scout.

If the leader is HesitantLeader and chooses DustRoute, then Block gives 1 to the leader, and 1 to the scout.
If the leader is HesitantLeader and chooses DustRoute, then StandAside gives 3 to the leader, and 0 to the scout.

If the leader is HesitantLeader and chooses StoneRoute, then Block gives 0 to the leader, and 1 to the scout.
If the leader is HesitantLeader and chooses StoneRoute, then StandAside gives 2 to the leader, and 0 to the scout.
