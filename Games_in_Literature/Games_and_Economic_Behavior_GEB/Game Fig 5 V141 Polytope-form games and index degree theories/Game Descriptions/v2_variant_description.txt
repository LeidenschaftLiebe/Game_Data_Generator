V2 [Job interview]

A candidate’s confidence level is either Strong (s) with probability 0.9 or Worried (w) with probability 0.1.

After learning that privately, the candidate chooses between BoldPitch (B) and QuietPitch (Q).
The recruiter sees whether the candidate made the bold or quiet pitch, but does not know the confidence level.

If the candidate makes BoldPitch (B) after Strong (s), the recruiter chooses between NoFollowup (NF) and Followup (F).
NoFollowup (NF) gives 3 to the candidate and 1 to the recruiter.
Followup (F) gives 1 to the candidate and 0 to the recruiter.

If the candidate makes BoldPitch (B) after Worried (w), NoFollowup (NF) gives 2 and 0,
while Followup (F) gives 0 and 1.

If the candidate makes QuietPitch (Q) after Strong (s), NoFollowup (NF) gives 2 and 1,
while Followup (F) gives 0 and 0.

If the candidate makes QuietPitch (Q) after Worried (w), NoFollowup (NF) gives 3 and 0,
while Followup (F) gives 1 and 1.
