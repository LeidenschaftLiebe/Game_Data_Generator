V2 (fashion show production)

A creative director first chooses between the runway-focused plan and the designer-focused plan.

If the director chooses the runway-focused plan, the production manager then chooses between lighting setup A and lighting setup B.
If the production manager chooses lighting setup A, the final reward is 2 for the creative director and 3 for the production manager.
If the production manager chooses lighting setup B, the final reward is 5 for the creative director and 4 for the production manager.

If the director chooses the designer-focused plan, the production manager then chooses between schedule C and schedule D.
If the production manager chooses schedule D, the final reward is 0 for the creative director and 2 for the production manager.
If the production manager chooses schedule C, the creative director then chooses between finale format E and finale format F.
Finale format E gives a final reward of 6 to the creative director and 2 to the production manager.
Finale format F gives a final reward of 2 to the creative director and 6 to the production manager.
