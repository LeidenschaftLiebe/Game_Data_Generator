V0 — Principal-Agent with Inflation

There are two players, Principal and Agent.
The Principal moves first and chooses between Low and High.

After that, chance determines the economic condition.
With probability 1/2, there is No-Inflation.
With probability 1/2, there is Inflation.

If the Principal chooses Low and the economy is No-Inflation, the Agent then chooses between low and high effort.
If the path is Low, No-Inflation, low, the outcome gives reward 4 to the Principal and reward 4 to the Agent.
If the path is Low, No-Inflation, high, the outcome gives reward 12 to the Principal and reward 3 to the Agent.

If the Principal chooses Low and the economy is Inflation, the Agent again chooses between low and high effort.
If the path is Low, Inflation, low, the outcome gives reward 4 to the Principal and reward 2 to the Agent.
If the path is Low, Inflation, high, the outcome gives reward 12 to the Principal and reward 1 to the Agent.

If the Principal chooses High and the economy is No-Inflation, the Agent chooses between low and high effort.
If the path is High, No-Inflation, low, the outcome gives reward 2 to the Principal and reward 6 to the Agent.
If the path is High, No-Inflation, high, the outcome gives reward 10 to the Principal and reward 5 to the Agent.

If the Principal chooses High and the economy is Inflation, the Agent chooses between low and high effort.
If the path is High, Inflation, low, the outcome gives reward 2 to the Principal and reward 4 to the Agent.
If the path is High, Inflation, high, the outcome gives reward 10 to the Principal and reward 3 to the Agent.
