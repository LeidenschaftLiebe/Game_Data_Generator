V5 [Film production]

Production conditions first turn out to be either SmoothShoot (omega+) or TroubledShoot (omega-).

The producer observes that and chooses between LocationLeft (L) and LocationRight (R).

The director then observes both the production condition and the location choice,
and chooses between TenseCut (T) and BalancedCut (B).

If conditions are SmoothShoot (omega+), then:
LocationLeft (L), TenseCut (T) gives 3, 2.
LocationLeft (L), BalancedCut (B) gives 1, 4.
LocationRight (R), TenseCut (T) gives 4, 1.
LocationRight (R), BalancedCut (B) gives 2, 3.

If conditions are TroubledShoot (omega-), then:
LocationLeft (L), TenseCut (T) gives 2, 3.
LocationLeft (L), BalancedCut (B) gives 4, 1.
LocationRight (R), TenseCut (T) gives 1, 4.
LocationRight (R), BalancedCut (B) gives 3, 2.
