V3 (software release)

A software company first decides whether to follow launch path A or launch path B.
If it follows launch path A, the process ends immediately and the final reward is 1 for the company and 2 for the competitor.
If it follows launch path B, the competitor then chooses between reaction C and reaction D.
Reaction C leads to 3 for the company and 1 for the competitor.
Reaction D leads to 2 for the company and 4 for the competitor.
