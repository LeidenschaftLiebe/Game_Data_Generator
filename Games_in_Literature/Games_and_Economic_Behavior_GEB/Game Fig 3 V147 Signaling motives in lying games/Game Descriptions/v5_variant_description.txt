V5 [Warehouse inspection]

A worker is deciding whether to enter a route that may later allow flexible reporting of stock quality.
The worker first chooses between RoutineCheck (i = 0) and FlexibleCheck (i = 1).

After RoutineCheck (i = 0), chance leads to the flexible reporting stage with probability 1/10 
and to the fixed route with probability 9/10.
After FlexibleCheck (i = 1), chance leads to the flexible reporting stage with probability 3/5 
and to another fixed route with probability 2/5.

Chance then determines the true stock condition: LowGrade (j = 1) or HighGrade (j = 2), each with probability 1/2.
If the worker ends up on a fixed route, the result follows the true condition: 
0 after LowGrade (j = 1) and 2 after HighGrade (j = 2).

If the worker reaches the flexible reporting stage, they choose between ReportLow (a = 1) and ReportHigh (a = 2).
After LowGrade (j = 1), ReportLow (a = 1) gives 0 and ReportHigh (a = 2) gives 2.
After HighGrade (j = 2), ReportLow (a = 1) gives 0 and ReportHigh (a = 2) gives 2.
