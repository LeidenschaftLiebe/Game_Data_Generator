V3 — Archive Inspection

An archive project may begin under either Plan A or Plan B.
Nature selects the plan, with probability 1/2 for each.

If the project begins under Plan A, the archive director makes an A-plan choice between End Plan (Ea) and Continue Plan (Ca).
Choosing End Plan (Ea) ends the process with reward 1 to the archive director and reward 0 to the reviewer.
Choosing Continue Plan (Ca) passes the move to the reviewer.

If the project begins under Plan B, the archive director makes a B-plan choice between End Plan (Eb) and Continue Plan (Cb).
Choosing End Plan (Eb) ends the process with reward 1 to the archive director and reward 0 to the reviewer.
Choosing Continue Plan (Cb) passes the move to the reviewer.

If Continue Plan (Ca) or Continue Plan (Cb) is chosen, the reviewer then chooses among
Left Assessment (L), Right Assessment (R), and Reopen Review (C2).
At that moment, the reviewer knows the archive director continued, but does not know whether the earlier situation was Plan A or Plan B.

If the earlier situation was Plan A and the reviewer chooses Left Assessment (L), the game ends with reward 0 to the archive director and reward 3 to the reviewer.
If the earlier situation was Plan A and the reviewer chooses Right Assessment (R), the game ends with reward 3 to the archive director and reward 0 to the reviewer.
If the earlier situation was Plan A and the reviewer chooses Reopen Review (C2), the archive director gets another turn in the B-plan situation.

If the earlier situation was Plan B and the reviewer chooses Right Assessment (R), the game ends with reward 0 to the archive director and reward 3 to the reviewer.
If the earlier situation was Plan B and the reviewer chooses Left Assessment (L), the game ends with reward 3 to the archive director and reward 0 to the reviewer.
If the earlier situation was Plan B and the reviewer chooses Reopen Review (C2), the archive director gets another turn in the A-plan situation.

When the archive director later returns to the B-plan situation, the archive director again chooses between End Plan (Eb) and Continue Plan (Cb).
Either choice then ends the process with reward 0 to the archive director and reward 2 to the reviewer.

When the archive director later returns to the A-plan situation, the archive director again chooses between End Plan (Ea) and Continue Plan (Ca).
Either choice then ends the process with reward 0 to the archive director and reward 2 to the reviewer.

Whenever the archive director is making an A-plan choice, 
the archive director cannot tell whether it is the earlier A situation or the later one reached after Reopen Review (C2).
Likewise, whenever the archive director is making a B-plan choice, 
the archive director cannot tell whether it is the earlier B situation or the later one reached after Reopen Review (C2).
