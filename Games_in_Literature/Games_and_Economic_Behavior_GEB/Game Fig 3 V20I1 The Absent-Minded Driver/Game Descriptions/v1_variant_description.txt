V1 — Delivery Van with Confusing Off-Ramps

A delivery van leaves the depot and keeps driving along a highway.

The van may arrive at three similar-looking off-ramps called RampX [X], RampY [Y], and RampZ [Z].

At each ramp, the driver chooses TakeRamp [EXIT] or StayOnHighway [CONT].

The driver cannot tell which of the three ramps it is.
So the same choice rule is used each time.

If the driver takes RampX [X], the reward is 7.

If the driver takes RampY [Y], the reward is 0.

If the driver takes RampZ [Z], the reward is 22.

If the driver stays on the highway even after RampZ [Z], the reward is 2.
