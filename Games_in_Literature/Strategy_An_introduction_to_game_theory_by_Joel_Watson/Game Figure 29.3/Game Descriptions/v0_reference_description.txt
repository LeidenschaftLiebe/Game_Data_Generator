V0 (closest to the textbook)

At the outset, Player 1 may be either the ordinary type or the cooperative type.
The ordinary type occurs with probability 3/4.
The cooperative type occurs with probability 1/4.
Player 1 learns which type has occurred.
Player 2 does not observe Player 1’s type.

If Player 1 is the ordinary type, Player 1 first chooses whether to invest or not.
If the ordinary type does not invest, the game ends with a final reward of 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If the ordinary type invests, Player 2 then chooses whether to invest or not.
Player 2 does not know whether the investor is the ordinary type or the cooperative type.
If Player 2 does not invest after the ordinary type invests, the final reward is -2 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If Player 2 invests after the ordinary type invests, Player 1 then chooses whether to be selfish or benevolent.
If the ordinary type is selfish after both players invest, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and -2 for Player 2.
If the ordinary type is benevolent after both players invest, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.

If Player 1 is the cooperative type, Player 1 first chooses whether to invest or not.
If the cooperative type does not invest, the game ends with a final reward of 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If the cooperative type invests, Player 2 then chooses whether to invest or not.
Player 2 again does not know whether the investor is the ordinary type or the cooperative type.
If Player 2 does not invest after the cooperative type invests, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
If Player 2 invests after the cooperative type invests, Player 1 then chooses whether to be selfish or benevolent.
If the cooperative type is selfish after both players invest, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and -2 for Player 2.
If the cooperative type is benevolent after both players invest, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
