V2 (music industry hiring)

At the outset, an applicant may be either a high-talent performer or a low-talent performer.
The high-talent type occurs with probability 1/3.
The low-talent type occurs with probability 2/3.
The applicant learns her own type.
The record label does not observe the applicant’s type.

The applicant then chooses whether to earn a costly elite conservatory credential or not.
The label observes whether the applicant earned the credential, but it does not observe her true talent.
After that, the label chooses between placing her in a headline artist role or a back-office music support role.

If the applicant is high-talent and does not earn the credential, the label chooses between the headline artist role and the support role.
If the label gives the headline artist role there, the final reward is 10 for the applicant and 10 for the label.
If the label gives the support role there, the final reward is 4 for the applicant and 4 for the label.

If the applicant is low-talent and does not earn the credential, the label again chooses between the headline artist role and the support role.
The label cannot tell whether the no-credential signal came from the high-talent or low-talent applicant.
If the label gives the headline artist role there, the final reward is 10 for the applicant and 0 for the label.
If the label gives the support role there, the final reward is 4 for the applicant and 4 for the label.

If the applicant is high-talent and earns the credential, the label chooses between the headline artist role and the support role.
If the label gives the headline artist role there, the final reward is 6 for the applicant and 10 for the label.
If the label gives the support role there, the final reward is 0 for the applicant and 4 for the label.

If the applicant is low-talent and earns the credential, the label again chooses between the headline artist role and the support role.
The label cannot tell whether the credential signal came from the high-talent or low-talent applicant.
If the label gives the headline artist role there, the final reward is 3 for the applicant and 0 for the label.
If the label gives the support role there, the final reward is -3 for the applicant and 4 for the label.
