V2 (restaurant expansion)

A restaurant owner first decides between expansion option U and expansion option D.
If the owner chooses option U, the rival restaurant then chooses between reaction A and reaction B.
If the rival chooses reaction A, the final result is 3 for the owner and 3 for the rival.
If the rival chooses reaction B, the final result is 5 for the owner and 4 for the rival.

If the owner chooses option D, the rival then chooses between reaction C and reaction D.
If the rival chooses reaction D, the final return is 2 for the owner and 2 for the rival.
If the rival chooses reaction C, the owner then chooses between operating plan E and operating plan F.
Operating plan E gives 6 to the owner and 2 to the rival.
Operating plan F gives 2 to the owner and 6 to the rival.
