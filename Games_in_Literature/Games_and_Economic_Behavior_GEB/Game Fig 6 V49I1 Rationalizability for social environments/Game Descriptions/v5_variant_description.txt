V5 — Research Funding Route

A lab can move to AI Initiative [x1], Bio Initiative [x2], or remain at Current Allocation [x0].

At the start, the institute’s internal rule selects which initiative has priority if both are proposed.
The order is either AIFirst [ (x1, x2) ] or BioFirst [ (x2, x1) ].

Then the AI lead chooses Propose [1] or DoNotPropose [0] for AI Initiative [x1].

After that, the Bio lead chooses Propose [1] or DoNotPropose [0] for Bio Initiative [x2].

The AI lead does not know which priority order was selected.
The Bio lead does not know which priority order was selected, and also does not know the earlier choice of the AI lead.

If neither lead proposes an initiative, the result is Current Allocation [x0], and both leads get 0.

If only the AI lead proposes, the result is AI Initiative [x1].
The AI lead gets 2 and the Bio lead gets 1.

If only the Bio lead proposes, the result is Bio Initiative [x2].
The AI lead gets 1 and the Bio lead gets 2.

If both leads propose their initiatives, the selected priority order determines which initiative is funded.
