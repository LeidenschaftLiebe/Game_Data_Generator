V2 [Research grant diversion]

A principal investigator, an administrator, and a lab assistant are involved in a budget decision.
The principal investigator first chooses between KeepPlan (Out) and UseAssistantBudget (In).

If the principal investigator chooses KeepPlan (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the principal investigator, 100 for the administrator, and 30 for the lab assistant.

If the principal investigator chooses UseAssistantBudget (In), 25 units are taken from the lab assistant’s budget and handed to the administrator.
The administrator then chooses between SmallTransfer (Left) and LargeTransfer (Right).

If the administrator chooses SmallTransfer (Left), the administrator keeps 20 and sends 5 to the principal investigator.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the principal investigator receives 10.
The result is 180 for the principal investigator, 120 for the administrator, and 5 for the lab assistant.

If the administrator chooses LargeTransfer (Right), the administrator sends the full 25 to the principal investigator.
That amount is doubled, so the principal investigator receives 50.
The result is 220 for the principal investigator, 100 for the administrator, and 5 for the lab assistant.
