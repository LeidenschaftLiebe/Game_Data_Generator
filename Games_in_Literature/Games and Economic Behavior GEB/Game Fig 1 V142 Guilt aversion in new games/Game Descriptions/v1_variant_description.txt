V1 [Startup funding]

A founder, an investor, and an outside observer are involved in a small funding decision.
The founder moves first and chooses between KeepFunds (Out) and InvestFunds (In).

If the founder chooses KeepFunds (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the founder, 100 for the investor, and 30 for the observer.

If the founder chooses InvestFunds (In), 25 units come out of the founder’s own resources and are handed to the investor.
The investor then chooses between PartialReturn (Left) and FullReturn (Right).

If the investor chooses PartialReturn (Left), the investor keeps 20 and sends back 5 to the founder.
That returned amount is doubled, so the founder receives 10.
The result is 155 for the founder, 120 for the investor, and 30 for the observer.

If the investor chooses FullReturn (Right), the investor sends the full 25 back to the founder.
That amount is doubled, so the founder receives 50.
The result is 195 for the founder, 100 for the investor, and 30 for the observer.
