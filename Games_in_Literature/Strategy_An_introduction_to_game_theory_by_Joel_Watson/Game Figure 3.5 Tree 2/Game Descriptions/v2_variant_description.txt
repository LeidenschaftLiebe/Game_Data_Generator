V2 (restaurant expansion)

A restaurant owner first chooses between expansion option A and expansion option B.
The rival then chooses between reaction C and reaction D.
The rival must choose without knowing which expansion option the owner selected.

If the owner had chosen option A, then either rival reaction leads to the same payoff: 1 for the owner and 2 for the rival.
If the owner had chosen option B and the rival takes reaction C, the final reward is 3 for the owner and 1 for the rival.
If the owner had chosen option B and the rival takes reaction D, the final reward is 2 for the owner and 4 for the rival.
