V5 (cybersecurity alliance)

At the outset, a large cybersecurity firm may be either a genuine ally or an opportunistic infiltrator, with probability 0.5 for each.
The large firm knows which type it is.
The smaller security lab knows only that the two types are equally likely.

If the large firm is a genuine ally, it chooses whether to Buy a larger stake in the alliance or Sell some of its current holdings.
The smaller lab observes whether the large firm chose Buy or Sell, but it does not observe whether the large firm is a genuine ally or an opportunistic infiltrator.
If the genuine ally chooses Buy, the smaller lab then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide its detection models and internal defenses.
If the smaller lab reveals after seeing Buy from a genuine ally, the final reward is 15 for the large firm and 12 for the smaller lab.
If the smaller lab hides after seeing Buy from a genuine ally, the final reward is -1 for the large firm and 1 for the smaller lab.

If the genuine ally chooses Sell, the smaller lab again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the smaller lab reveals after seeing Sell from a genuine ally, the final reward is 5 for the large firm and 6 for the smaller lab.
If the smaller lab hides after seeing Sell from a genuine ally, the final reward is 5 for the large firm and -1 for the smaller lab.

If the large firm is an opportunistic infiltrator, it also chooses whether to Buy a larger stake or Sell some of its current holdings.
If the opportunistic infiltrator chooses Buy, the smaller lab then chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the smaller lab reveals after seeing Buy from an opportunistic infiltrator, the final reward is 5 for the large firm and -6 for the smaller lab.
If the smaller lab hides after seeing Buy from an opportunistic infiltrator, the final reward is 5 for the large firm and 1 for the smaller lab.

If the opportunistic infiltrator chooses Sell, the smaller lab again chooses whether to Reveal or Hide.
If the smaller lab reveals after seeing Sell from an opportunistic infiltrator, the final reward is 15 for the large firm and -14 for the smaller lab.
If the smaller lab hides after seeing Sell from an opportunistic infiltrator, the final reward is 0 for the large firm and -1 for the smaller lab.
