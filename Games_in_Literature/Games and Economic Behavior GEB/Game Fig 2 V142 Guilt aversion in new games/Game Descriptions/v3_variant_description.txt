V3 [Family asset decision]

A family organizer, a relative handling the money, and another family member are involved in a transfer decision.
The organizer first chooses between LeaveAsIs (Out) and UseRelativeFunds (In).

If the organizer chooses LeaveAsIs (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 100 for the relative handling the money, and 30 for the other family member.

If the organizer chooses UseRelativeFunds (In), 25 units are taken from the other family member’s money and handed to the relative handling the money.
That relative then chooses between ReturnSmallPart (Left) and ReturnFullPart (Right).

If the relative chooses ReturnSmallPart (Left), the relative keeps 20 and sends 5 to the organizer.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the organizer receives 10.
The result is 180 for the organizer, 120 for the relative, and 5 for the other family member.

If the relative chooses ReturnFullPart (Right), the relative sends the full 25 to the organizer.
That amount is doubled, so the organizer receives 50.
The result is 220 for the organizer, 100 for the relative, and 5 for the other family member.
