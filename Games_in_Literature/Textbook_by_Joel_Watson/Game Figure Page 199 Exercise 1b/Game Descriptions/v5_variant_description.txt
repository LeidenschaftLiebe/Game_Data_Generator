V5 (wildlife documentary production)

A producer first chooses between filming plan A and filming plan B.

If the producer chooses filming plan A, the distributor then chooses between release option C and release option D.
If the distributor chooses release option C, the producer then chooses between edit G and edit H.
Edit G gives a final reward of 3 to the producer and 8 to the distributor.
Edit H gives a final reward of 7 to the producer and 9 to the distributor.

If the distributor chooses release option D, the producer then chooses between edit I and edit J.
Edit I gives a final reward of 1 to the producer and 2 to the distributor.
Edit J gives a final reward of 2 to the producer and 1 to the distributor.

If the producer chooses filming plan B, the distributor then chooses between release option E and release option F.
If the distributor chooses release option E, the producer then chooses between edit K and edit L.
Edit K gives a final reward of 10 to the producer and 4 to the distributor.
Edit L gives a final reward of 0 to the producer and 5 to the distributor.

If the distributor chooses release option F, the producer then chooses between edit M and edit N.
Edit M gives a final reward of 4 to the producer and 0 to the distributor.
Edit N gives a final reward of 8 to the producer and 3 to the distributor.
