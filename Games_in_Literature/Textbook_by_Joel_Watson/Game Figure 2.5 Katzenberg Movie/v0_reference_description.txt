V0 Reference (Katzenberg movies game, closest to the textbook) 

Katzenberg is deciding what to do after leaving Disney. 

If he decides to stay at Disney, the rivalry never begins and the outcome is that Katzenberg gets 35 while Eisner gets 100. 
If he leaves, Eisner must decide whether Disney will go ahead with A Bug’s Life.

At the same time, Katzenberg is considering whether his new studio should make Antz. 
The key tension is that Katzenberg has to make that production decision before he can tell whether Eisner chose to produce A Bug’s Life. 
So when Katzenberg chooses whether to make Antz, he knows he has left Disney, but he does not yet know what Eisner has done.

If Eisner does not produce A Bug’s Life, then Katzenberg’s choice about Antz determines the outcome directly: 
producing Antz gives Katzenberg 80 and Eisner 0, while not producing it leaves both at 0. 
If Eisner does produce A Bug’s Life and Katzenberg decides not to produce Antz, then Katzenberg gets 0 and Eisner gets 140. 

If both films are produced, Katzenberg faces one final timing decision: 
he can rush Antz into theaters early, which gives him 40 and Eisner 110, or he can avoid the early release and end with 13 while Eisner gets 120.
