A student is deciding how to prepare for an important quiz.
At the start, the student chooses between review summary notes (b) and follow a longer study plan (a).
If the student reviews the summary notes, the day ends with a solid award 1.
If the student follows the longer study plan, there is one more choice later between stay focused (c) and get distracted (d).
Staying focused leads to award 1.
Getting distracted leads to award 0.
The student knows the longer plan could work well, but also knows there is a chance of slipping later.
