V5 (fashion brand expansion)

A fashion brand first chooses whether to enter a new segment or stay out.
If it stays out, the interaction ends immediately and each side gets a final reward of 1.

If it enters, the competing brand then chooses whether to enter too or stay out.
If the competing brand stays out, the final reward is -1 for the first brand and 0 for the competitor.

If the competing brand also enters, the first brand then chooses between a more aggressive move and a more balanced move.
The aggressive move gives a final reward of 4 to the first brand and -1 to the competitor.
The balanced move gives a final reward of 3 to the first brand and 2 to the competitor.
