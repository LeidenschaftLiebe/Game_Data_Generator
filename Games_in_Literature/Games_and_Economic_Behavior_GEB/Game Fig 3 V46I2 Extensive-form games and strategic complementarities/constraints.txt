Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:

Player 1 moves first at the root and chooses between No and Yes.
Player 2 moves after No and after Yes, choosing between O and B.
After No, player 1 moves again after player 2’s choice, and the two resulting player 1 nodes are in the same information set.
After Yes, player 1 moves again after player 2’s choice, and the two resulting player 1 nodes are in the same information set.
The above two information sets are different.
At terminal no_branch_match_O, the payoffs are (2, 1).
