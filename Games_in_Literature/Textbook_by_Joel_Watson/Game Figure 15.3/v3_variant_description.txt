V3 (museum exhibition alliance)

A museum director moves first and chooses between the Urban exhibition plan and the Discovery exhibition plan.

If the director chooses the Urban plan, a curator then chooses between Gallery A and Gallery B.
If the curator chooses Gallery A, the museum director then chooses between an Evening opening and a Family opening.
The Evening opening gives a final reward of 3 for the director, 3 for the curator, and 6 for the donor.
The Family opening gives a final reward of 1 for the director, 5 for the curator, and 7 for the donor.

If the curator chooses Gallery B under the Urban plan, the donor then chooses between a Grant upgrade and a Heritage package.
The Grant upgrade gives a final reward of 2 for the director, 0 for the curator, and 3 for the donor.
The Heritage package gives a final reward of 7 for the director, 7 for the curator, and 2 for the donor.

If the director chooses the Discovery plan, the curator then chooses between Gallery C and Gallery D.
If the curator chooses Gallery D, the final reward is 6 for the director, 2 for the curator, and 4 for the donor.
If the curator chooses Gallery C, the donor again chooses between the Grant upgrade and the Heritage package.
At that point, the donor cannot tell whether this choice came after Gallery B or Gallery C.
If the donor chooses the Grant upgrade after Gallery C, the final reward is 0 for the director, 6 for the curator, and 1 for the donor.
If the donor chooses the Heritage package after Gallery C, the final reward is 8 for the director, 6 for the curator, and 0 for the donor.
