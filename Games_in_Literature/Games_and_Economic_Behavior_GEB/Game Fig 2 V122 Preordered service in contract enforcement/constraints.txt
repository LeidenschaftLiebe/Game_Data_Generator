Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints

The first mover chooses between staying out and entering.
Staying out ends the interaction immediately with 1, 1.
If entry occurs, a random event happens next: exogenous breach with probability 1/4, and no exogenous breach with probability 3/4.
If exogenous breach occurs, or if the second mover chooses not to honor, the judiciary acts.
The judiciary enforces with probability 1/2 and does not enforce with probability 1/2.
