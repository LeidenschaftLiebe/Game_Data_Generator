Player 1 moves first and chooses between action A and action B.
After that, Player 2 chooses between action C and action D.
When Player 2 makes that choice, Player 2 cannot tell whether Player 1 chose A or B.

If Player 2 chooses C after action A, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses D after action A, the final reward is also 1 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses C after action B, the final return is 3 for Player 1 and 1 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses D after action B, the final result is 2 for Player 1 and 4 for Player 2.
