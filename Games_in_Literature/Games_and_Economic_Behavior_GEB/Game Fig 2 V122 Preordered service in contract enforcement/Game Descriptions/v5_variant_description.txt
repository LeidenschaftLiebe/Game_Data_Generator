V5 [Polar supply flight]

A relief funder first chooses between HoldFunds and FundFlight.
If the funder chooses HoldFunds, this gives 1 to the relief funder, and 1 to the flight contractor.

If the funder chooses FundFlight, a random shock may occur.
With probability 1/4, there is WeatherBreakdown.
With probability 3/4, there is ClearRun.

If there is ClearRun, the flight contractor chooses between DeliverSupplies and KeepPayment.
If the contractor chooses DeliverSupplies, this gives 2 to the relief funder, and 2 to the flight contractor.

If the contractor chooses KeepPayment, the aviation tribunal reviews the case.
It CompelsPayment with probability 1/2, or DoesNotCompelPayment with probability 1/2.
CompelsPayment gives 2 to the relief funder, and 1.8 to the flight contractor.
DoesNotCompelPayment gives 0 to the relief funder, and 4 to the flight contractor.

If there is WeatherBreakdown, the tribunal review begins immediately and leads to those same two possible results.
