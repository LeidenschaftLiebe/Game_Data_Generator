V1 (music festival planning)

A festival director moves first and chooses between the Uptown festival plan and the Downtown festival plan.

If the director chooses the Uptown plan, a booking manager then chooses between signing Headliner A and signing Headliner B.
If the booking manager signs Headliner A, the festival director then chooses between an Early slot and a Final slot.
The Early slot leads to a final reward of 3 for the director, 3 for the booking manager, and 6 for the sponsor.
The Final slot leads to a final reward of 1 for the director, 5 for the booking manager, and 7 for the sponsor.

If the booking manager signs Headliner B under the Uptown plan, the sponsor then chooses between a Gold package and a Hospitality package.
The Gold package leads to a final reward of 2 for the director, 0 for the booking manager, and 3 for the sponsor.
The Hospitality package leads to a final reward of 7 for the director, 7 for the booking manager, and 2 for the sponsor.

If the director chooses the Downtown plan, the booking manager then chooses between signing Artist C and signing Artist D.
If the booking manager signs Artist D, the final reward is 6 for the director, 2 for the booking manager, and 4 for the sponsor.
If the booking manager signs Artist C, the sponsor again chooses between the Gold package and the Hospitality package.
At that point, the sponsor cannot tell whether this choice came after Headliner B or Artist C.
If the sponsor picks the Gold package after Artist C, the final reward is 0 for the director, 6 for the booking manager, and 1 for the sponsor.
If the sponsor picks the Hospitality package after Artist C, the final reward is 8 for the director, 6 for the booking manager, and 0 for the sponsor.
