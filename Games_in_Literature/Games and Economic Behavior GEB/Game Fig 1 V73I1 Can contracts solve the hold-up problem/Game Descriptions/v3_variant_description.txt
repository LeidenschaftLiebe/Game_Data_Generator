V3 — Archive License

There are two sides in a document project, an Archive Lab and a Publisher.
First, both sides decide whether to accept a publishing option.

If either side says Do Not Sign (no), the process ends immediately with reward 0 to each side.
If both sides say Sign Option (yes), the Archive Lab chooses the restoration level.

The Archive Lab then chooses between Light Restoration (e = 0) and Deep Restoration (e = 8).
After seeing the restoration level, the Publisher chooses between Activate License (exercise) and Do Not Activate (not exercise).

If the path is Sign Option (yes), Sign Option (yes), Light Restoration (e = 0), Activate License (exercise), 
the outcome gives reward 15 to the Archive Lab and reward -5 to the Publisher.
If the path is Sign Option (yes), Sign Option (yes), Light Restoration (e = 0), Do Not Activate (not exercise), 
the outcome gives reward 0 to the Archive Lab and reward 0 to the Publisher.

If the path is Sign Option (yes), Sign Option (yes), Deep Restoration (e = 8), Activate License (exercise), 
the outcome gives reward 7 to the Archive Lab and reward 7 to the Publisher.
If the path is Sign Option (yes), Sign Option (yes), Deep Restoration (e = 8), Do Not Activate (not exercise), 
the outcome gives reward -8 to the Archive Lab and reward 0 to the Publisher.
