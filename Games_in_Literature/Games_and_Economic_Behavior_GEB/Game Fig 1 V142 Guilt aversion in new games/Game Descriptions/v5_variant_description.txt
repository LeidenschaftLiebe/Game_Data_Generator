V5 [Community project]

A local organizer, a treasurer, and a passive resident are involved in a project decision.
The organizer first chooses between KeepResources (Out) and EntrustResources (In).

If the organizer chooses KeepResources (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the organizer, 100 for the treasurer, and 30 for the resident.

If the organizer chooses EntrustResources (In), 25 units from the organizer’s own resources are handed to the treasurer.
The treasurer then chooses between ReturnSmallPart (Left) and ReturnFullPart (Right).

If the treasurer chooses ReturnSmallPart (Left), the treasurer keeps 20 and sends back 5 to the organizer.
That returned amount is doubled, so the organizer receives 10.
The result is 155 for the organizer, 120 for the treasurer, and 30 for the resident.

If the treasurer chooses ReturnFullPart (Right), the treasurer sends back the full 25 to the organizer.
That amount is doubled, so the organizer receives 50.
The result is 195 for the organizer, 100 for the treasurer, and 30 for the resident.
