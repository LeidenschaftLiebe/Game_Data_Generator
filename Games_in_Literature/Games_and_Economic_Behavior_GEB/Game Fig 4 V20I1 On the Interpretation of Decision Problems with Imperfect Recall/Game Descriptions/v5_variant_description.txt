V5 — Museum Search with Repeated Hallways

A visitor first reaches a hallway choice and chooses GoLeft [L] or GoRight [R].

If the visitor chooses GoRight [R], the search ends immediately with reward 0.

If the visitor chooses GoLeft [L], the visitor reaches another hallway choice that feels identical to the first one.
There, the visitor again chooses GoLeft [L] or GoRight [R].

After that, the visitor reaches one of two final halls, but cannot tell which final hall it is.

In the left final hall, choosing LeftDoor [L] gives reward 1, while RightDoor [R] gives reward 0.

In the right final hall, choosing LeftDoor [L] gives reward 0, while RightDoor [R] gives reward 3.
