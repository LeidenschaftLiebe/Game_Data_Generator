V5 (fashion brand expansion)

A fashion brand first decides between strategy U and strategy D.
If it chooses strategy U, the competing brand then chooses between response A and response B.
If the competing brand chooses response A, the final reward is 3 for the first brand and 3 for the competitor.
If the competing brand chooses response B, the final reward is 5 for the first brand and 4 for the competitor.

If the first brand chooses strategy D, the competitor then chooses between response C and response D.
If the competitor chooses response D, the final reward is 2 for the first brand and 2 for the competitor.
If the competitor chooses response C, the first brand then chooses between follow-up option E and follow-up option F.
Follow-up option E leads to 6 for the first brand and 2 for the competitor.
Follow-up option F leads to 2 for the first brand and 6 for the competitor.
