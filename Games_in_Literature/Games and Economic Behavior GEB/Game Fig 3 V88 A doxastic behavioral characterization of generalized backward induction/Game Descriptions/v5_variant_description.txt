V5 — Transfer Planning

A club is deciding whether to end a player search or carry it forward.
The club moves first and chooses between Continue Search (a₁) and End Search (b₁).

If the club chooses End Search (b₁), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the club,
reward 2 to the sporting director, and reward 0 to the scout.

If the club chooses Continue Search (a₁), the sporting director moves next and chooses between
Domestic Route (a₂) and International Route (b₂).

If the sporting director chooses Domestic Route (a₂), the scout chooses between
Shortlist One (a₃) and Push Top Target (b₃).
If Shortlist One (a₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the club,
reward 1 to the sporting director, and reward 0 to the scout.
If Push Top Target (b₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 3 to the club,
reward 3 to the sporting director, and reward 1 to the scout.

If the sporting director chooses International Route (b₂), the scout chooses between
Balanced Search (c₃) and All-In Search (d₃).
If Balanced Search (c₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the club,
reward 2 to the sporting director, and reward 1 to the scout.
If All-In Search (d₃) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 4 to the club,
reward 0 to the sporting director, and reward 0 to the scout.
