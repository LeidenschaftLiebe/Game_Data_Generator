V3 [Hidden archive recovery]

A recovery leader moves first and chooses between LeaveArchive and BreakIn.
An inside contact sees that choice and then chooses between LeaveArchive and BreakIn.

If both leave the archive alone, this gives 1 to the recovery leader, and 2 to the inside contact.

If the recovery leader leaves it alone but the inside contact breaks in alone, this gives 3 to the recovery leader, and 0 to the inside contact.

If the recovery leader breaks in but the inside contact does not help, this gives 0 to the recovery leader, and 3 to the inside contact.

If both break in, this gives 3 to the recovery leader, and 2 to the inside contact.
