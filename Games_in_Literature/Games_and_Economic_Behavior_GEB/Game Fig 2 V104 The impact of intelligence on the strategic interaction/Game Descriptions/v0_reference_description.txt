V0 — Inspections

Set beta = 0.4, w1 = 0.6, r1 = 0.2, cL = 0.1, cH = 0.5, w2 = 0.8, r2 = 0.3
A hostile state may or may not find it costly to open its facilities to outside inspection.

At the start, the state is one of two types.
With probability 0.6 it is the low-cost type L.
With probability 0.4 it is the high-cost type H.

After learning its type, Player 1 chooses among Open (NBO), No Bomb (NB), and Build (B).

If the low-cost type chooses Open (NBO), the interaction ends with reward 0.5 to Player 1 and 1.0 to Player 2.

If the high-cost type chooses Open (NBO), the interaction ends with reward 0.1 to Player 1 and 1.0 to Player 2.

If Player 1 instead chooses No Bomb (NB) or Build (B), Player 2 moves next.

At that point, Player 2 only sees that the facilities were not opened.
Player 2 does not know whether Player 1 is type L or H, and does not know whether Player 1 chose No Bomb (NB) or Build (B).

Player 2 then chooses between No Attack (NA) and Attack (A).

If the path is No Bomb (NB), No Attack (NA), the outcome is reward 0.6 to Player 1 and 1.0 to Player 2.

If the path is No Bomb (NB), Attack (A), the outcome is reward 0.2 to Player 1 and 0.3 to Player 2.

If the path is Build (B), No Attack (NA), the outcome is reward 1.0 to Player 1 and 0.0 to Player 2.

If the path is Build (B), Attack (A), the outcome is reward 0.0 to Player 1 and 0.8 to Player 2.
