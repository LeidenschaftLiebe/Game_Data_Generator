V1 (smartphone brands)

A smartphone brand first decides whether to fight aggressively for a market, compete more softly, or withdraw from that market altogether.
If it withdraws, the game ends immediately and the withdrawing brand gets 0 while the rival gets 4.
If it stays in, the rival brand then chooses whether to take an aggressive stance or a passive stance.
The rival knows that the first brand stayed in, but does not know whether it chose the aggressive or passive approach.
If both brands fight aggressively, each gets 3.
If Brand 1 is aggressive and Brand 2 is passive, Brand 1 gets 4 and Brand 2 gets 2.
If Brand 1 is passive and Brand 2 is aggressive, Brand 1 gets 2 and Brand 2 gets 4.
If both brands stay passive, each gets 2.
