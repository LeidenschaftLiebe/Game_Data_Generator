V1 [Ancient tunnel expedition]

A hidden assessment first determines whether the expedition leader is SteadyLeader or NervousLeader.
SteadyLeader happens with probability 9/10, and NervousLeader happens with probability 1/10.

The expedition leader then chooses between CampMeal and TrailMeal.

After seeing that meal choice, the guard captain chooses between Challenge and StandDown.

If the leader is SteadyLeader and chooses CampMeal, then Challenge gives 0 to the leader, and 0 to the captain.
If the leader is SteadyLeader and chooses CampMeal, then StandDown gives 2 to the leader, and 1 to the captain.

If the leader is SteadyLeader and chooses TrailMeal, then Challenge gives 1 to the leader, and 0 to the captain.
If the leader is SteadyLeader and chooses TrailMeal, then StandDown gives 3 to the leader, and 1 to the captain.

If the leader is NervousLeader and chooses CampMeal, then Challenge gives 1 to the leader, and 1 to the captain.
If the leader is NervousLeader and chooses CampMeal, then StandDown gives 3 to the leader, and 0 to the captain.

If the leader is NervousLeader and chooses TrailMeal, then Challenge gives 0 to the leader, and 1 to the captain.
If the leader is NervousLeader and chooses TrailMeal, then StandDown gives 2 to the leader, and 0 to the captain.
