V3 (museum exhibit launch)

At the outset, a museum may be in either a strong-interest period or a weak-interest period, with probability 0.5 for each case.
The museum director learns which period applies.
The publicity partner does not observe whether interest is strong or weak.

If the museum is in the strong-interest period, the director chooses between the local launch plan and the media launch plan.
If the director chooses the local launch plan in the strong-interest period, the final reward is 2 for the director and 0 for the publicity partner.
If the director chooses the media launch plan in the strong-interest period, the publicity partner then chooses between an urgent campaign and a delayed campaign.
If the publicity partner chooses an urgent campaign there, the final reward is 3 for the director and 2 for the publicity partner.
If the publicity partner chooses a delayed campaign there, the final reward is 1 for the director and 0 for the publicity partner.

If the museum is in the weak-interest period, the director chooses between the local launch plan and the media launch plan.
If the director chooses the local launch plan in the weak-interest period, the final reward is 2 for the director and 0 for the publicity partner.
If the director chooses the media launch plan in the weak-interest period, the publicity partner then chooses between an urgent campaign and a delayed campaign.
The publicity partner cannot tell whether this decision follows the media launch plan in the strong-interest period or in the weak-interest period.
If the publicity partner chooses an urgent campaign there, the final reward is 1 for the director and 0 for the publicity partner.
If the publicity partner chooses a delayed campaign there, the final reward is 1 for the director and 1 for the publicity partner.
