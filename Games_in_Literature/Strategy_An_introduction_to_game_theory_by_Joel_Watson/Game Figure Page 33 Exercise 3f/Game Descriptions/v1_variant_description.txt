V1 (market entry)

A firm first chooses between expansion path U and expansion path D.
The rival firm then chooses between response A and response B without knowing which path the first firm selected.

If the rival chooses response A after expansion path U, the first firm then chooses between follow-up move X and follow-up move Y.
At that point, the first firm cannot tell whether the rival had chosen response A or response B on the upper branch.
Follow-up move X after U and A gives a final reward of 3 to the first firm and 8 to the rival.
Follow-up move Y after U and A gives a final reward of 8 to the first firm and 1 to the rival.

If the rival chooses response B after expansion path U, the first firm again chooses between follow-up move X and follow-up move Y 
at the same hidden-information situation as above.
Follow-up move X after U and B gives a final reward of 1 to the first firm and 2 to the rival.
Follow-up move Y after U and B gives a final reward of 2 to the first firm and 1 to the rival.

If the rival chooses response A after expansion path D, the game ends with 6 for each side.
If the rival chooses response B after expansion path D, the first firm then chooses between closing move P and closing move Q.
Closing move P gives a final reward of 5 to each side.
Closing move Q gives a final reward of 0 to each side.
