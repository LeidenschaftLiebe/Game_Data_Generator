V3 — Harbor Inspection

A port owner may face one of two inspection cases.
Nature first determines whether the case is Routine Check with probability 0.1 or Major Violation with probability 0.9.

If it is a Routine Check, the port owner chooses between Stop Here (Out1) and Disclose It (In1).
If the owner chooses Stop Here (Out1), the matter ends with reward 0 to the owner and reward 0 to the inspector.

If it is a Major Violation, the port owner chooses between Stop Here (Out2) and Disclose It (In2).
If the owner chooses Stop Here (Out2), the matter ends with reward 0 to the owner and reward 0 to the inspector.

If the owner chooses Disclose It (In1/In2), the inspector chooses between
Escalate (U), Record Only (M), and Drop It (D).
When the inspector moves, the inspector knows disclosure happened, but does not know which case occurred.

If the case was Routine Check and the inspector chooses Escalate (U),
the owner gets reward -1 and the inspector gets reward 3.
If the case was Routine Check and the inspector chooses Record Only (M),
the owner gets reward -1 and the inspector gets reward 2.
If the case was Routine Check and the inspector chooses Drop It (D),
the owner gets reward 2 and the inspector gets reward 0.

If the case was Major Violation and the inspector chooses Escalate (U),
the owner gets reward -3 and the inspector gets reward 0.
If the case was Major Violation and the inspector chooses Record Only (M),
the owner gets reward -1 and the inspector gets reward 2.
If the case was Major Violation and the inspector chooses Drop It (D),
the owner gets reward 3 and the inspector gets reward 3.
