V3 — Film Release Choice

A producer is deciding how to release a film.
The producer moves first and chooses between Festival Debut (a), Standard Release (b),
and Quiet Release (c).

After that, the distributor moves.
The distributor cannot tell which of those three release plans was chosen.
The distributor then chooses between Promote Widely (d) and Keep It Limited (e).

If the path is Festival Debut (a), Promote Widely (d),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the producer and reward 1 to the distributor.
If the path is Festival Debut (a), Keep It Limited (e),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer and reward 0 to the distributor.

If the path is Standard Release (b), Promote Widely (d),
the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer and reward 1 to the distributor.
If the path is Standard Release (b), Keep It Limited (e),
the outcome gives reward 2 to the producer and reward 0 to the distributor.

If the path is Quiet Release (c), Promote Widely (d),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the producer and reward 0 to the distributor.
If the path is Quiet Release (c), Keep It Limited (e),
the outcome gives reward 0 to the producer and reward 1 to the distributor.
