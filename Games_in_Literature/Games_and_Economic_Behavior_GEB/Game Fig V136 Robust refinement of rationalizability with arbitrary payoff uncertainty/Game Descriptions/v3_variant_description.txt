V3 [Diplomatic signal]

A government moves first and chooses between ShowResolve (U) and StepBack (D).

Then a rival responds, but the rival cannot tell whether the government showed resolve or stepped back.
The rival chooses between OfferDeal (L), Wait (R), and EscalatePressure (X).

If the government chose ShowResolve (U) and the rival chooses OfferDeal (L), the result is 1, 3.
If the government chose ShowResolve (U) and the rival chooses Wait (R), the result is 0, 2.
If the government chose ShowResolve (U) and the rival chooses EscalatePressure (X), the result is 1, 1.

If the government chose StepBack (D) and the rival chooses OfferDeal (L), the result is 0, 2.
If the government chose StepBack (D) and the rival chooses Wait (R), the result is 1, 3.

If the government chose StepBack (D) and the rival chooses EscalatePressure (X), the government moves again.
The government then chooses between PushBack (u) and Yield (d).

StepBack (D), EscalatePressure (X), PushBack (u) leads to 2, 1.
StepBack (D), EscalatePressure (X), Yield (d) leads to 0, 1.
