V0 (closest to the book)

Player 1 moves first and chooses between action U and action D.

If Player 1 chooses action U, then Player 2 chooses between action A and action B.
If Player 2 chooses action A, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and 4 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses action B, the final reward is 5 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses action D, then Player 2 chooses between action C and action D.
If Player 2 chooses action D there, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
If Player 2 chooses action C, then Player 1 moves again and chooses between action E and action F.
If Player 1 chooses action E, the final reward is 3 for Player 1 and 3 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chooses action F, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
