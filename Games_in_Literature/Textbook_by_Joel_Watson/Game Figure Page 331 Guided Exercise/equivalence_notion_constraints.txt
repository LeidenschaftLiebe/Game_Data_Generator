Strategic Equivalence Notion: Better-response equivalence

Constraints:
Check that the incumbent is high-cost with probability 0.5 and low-cost with probability 0.5.
Check that the entrant’s two decision nodes after price option 1 are in the same information set.
Check that the entrant’s two decision nodes after price option 2 are in the same information set.

Check that if the low-cost incumbent chooses price option 2 and the entrant stays out, the final reward is 4 for the incumbent and 0 for the entrant.
