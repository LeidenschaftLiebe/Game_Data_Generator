V2 — Flood Warning

There are two players, a River Station and a Control Center.
At the start, chance selects one of two hidden conditions: North Flow (A) or South Flow (B).

The River Station learns which condition was selected.
The River Station then sends either North Signal (A) or South Signal (B).

After seeing the signal, the Control Center chooses between Open Gates (U) and
Hold Gates (D).

If the true condition is North Flow (A) and the Control Center chooses
Open Gates (U), the outcome gives reward 2 to the River Station and reward 1 to the Control Center.
If the true condition is North Flow (A) and the Control Center chooses
Hold Gates (D), the outcome gives reward 1 to the River Station and reward 2 to the Control Center.

If the true condition is South Flow (B) and the Control Center chooses
Open Gates (U), the outcome gives reward 1 to the River Station and reward 2 to the Control Center.
If the true condition is South Flow (B) and the Control Center chooses
Hold Gates (D), the outcome gives reward 2 to the River Station and reward 1 to the Control Center.
