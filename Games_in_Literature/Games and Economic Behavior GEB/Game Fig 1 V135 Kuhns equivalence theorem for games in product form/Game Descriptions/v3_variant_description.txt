V3 [Campaign strategy]

The political climate first turns out to be either FriendlyClimate (omega+) or HostileClimate (omega-).

The campaign manager observes that climate and chooses between RegionalFocus (L) and NationalFocus (R).

The candidate then observes both the climate and the manager’s choice,
and chooses between ToughSpeech (T) and BridgeSpeech (B).

If the climate is FriendlyClimate (omega+), then:
RegionalFocus (L), ToughSpeech (T) gives 3, 2.
RegionalFocus (L), BridgeSpeech (B) gives 1, 4.
NationalFocus (R), ToughSpeech (T) gives 4, 1.
NationalFocus (R), BridgeSpeech (B) gives 2, 3.

If the climate is HostileClimate (omega-), then:
RegionalFocus (L), ToughSpeech (T) gives 2, 3.
RegionalFocus (L), BridgeSpeech (B) gives 4, 1.
NationalFocus (R), ToughSpeech (T) gives 1, 4.
NationalFocus (R), BridgeSpeech (B) gives 3, 2.
