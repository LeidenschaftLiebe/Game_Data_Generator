V1 [Arctic supply mission]

A mission commander first chooses between NorthernRoute (A), DirectRoute (B), and AbortMission (E).

If the commander chooses AbortMission (E), the trip ends there.
This gives 3 to the mission commander, and 0 to the ice pilot.

If the commander chooses DirectRoute (B), the ice pilot chooses between SafeSpeed (a) and FastSpeed (b).
SafeSpeed (a) gives 0 to the mission commander, and 0 to the ice pilot.
FastSpeed (b) gives 1 to the mission commander, and 1 to the ice pilot.

If the commander chooses NorthernRoute (A), the commander then chooses between WestPass (C) and EastPass (D).

After WestPass (C), the ice pilot again chooses between SafeSpeed (a) and FastSpeed (b).
SafeSpeed (a) gives 4 to the mission commander, and 0 to the ice pilot.
FastSpeed (b) gives 2 to the mission commander, and 0 to the ice pilot.

After EastPass (D), the ice pilot again chooses between SafeSpeed (a) and FastSpeed (b).
SafeSpeed (a) gives 1 to the mission commander, and 1 to the ice pilot.
FastSpeed (b) gives 0 to the mission commander, and 0 to the ice pilot.

When choosing between SafeSpeed (a) and FastSpeed (b), the ice pilot cannot tell which of those three situations led there.
