V0 — Perfect-Information Example

There are three players.
Player 1 moves first and chooses between a₁ and b₁.

If Player 1 chooses b₁, the game ends immediately with reward 2 to Player 1,
reward 2 to Player 2, and reward 0 to Player 3.

If Player 1 chooses a₁, Player 2 moves next and chooses between a₂ and b₂.

If Player 2 chooses a₂, Player 3 moves next and chooses between a₃ and b₃.
If Player 3 chooses a₃, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1, reward 1 to Player 2,
and reward 0 to Player 3.
If Player 3 chooses b₃, the outcome gives reward 3 to Player 1, reward 3 to Player 2,
and reward 1 to Player 3.

If Player 2 chooses b₂, Player 3 moves next and chooses between c₃ and d₃.
If Player 3 chooses c₃, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1, reward 2 to Player 2,
and reward 1 to Player 3.
If Player 3 chooses d₃, the outcome gives reward 4 to Player 1, reward 0 to Player 2,
and reward 0 to Player 3.
