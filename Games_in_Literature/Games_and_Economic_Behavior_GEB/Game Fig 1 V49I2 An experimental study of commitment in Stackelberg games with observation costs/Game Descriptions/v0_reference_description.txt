V0 — Costly Leader Game

Two firms interact, a Leader and a Follower.

The Leader moves first and chooses S or C.

After that, the Follower decides whether to Observe the Leader’s choice or Not Observe it.
Observing costs the Follower 30 points.

If the Follower chooses Observe, the Follower sees whether the Leader chose S or C before making the final choice.
If the Follower chooses Not Observe, the Follower receives no information about the Leader’s earlier move.

The Follower then chooses s or c.

If the Leader chose S, the Follower observed, and then chose s, the Leader gets 500 and the Follower gets 170.
If the Leader chose S, the Follower observed, and then chose c, the Leader gets 300 and the Follower gets 70.

If the Leader chose S, the Follower did not observe, and then chose s, the Leader gets 500 and the Follower gets 200.
If the Leader chose S, the Follower did not observe, and then chose c, the Leader gets 300 and the Follower gets 100.

If the Leader chose C, the Follower did not observe, and then chose s, the Leader gets 600 and the Follower gets 300.
If the Leader chose C, the Follower did not observe, and then chose c, the Leader gets 400 and the Follower gets 400.

If the Leader chose C, the Follower observed, and then chose s, the Leader gets 600 and the Follower gets 270.
If the Leader chose C, the Follower observed, and then chose c, the Leader gets 400 and the Follower gets 370.
