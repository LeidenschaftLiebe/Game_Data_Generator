V5 — Streaming Release Strategy

A major streaming platform chooses its release strategy first, and a smaller platform reacts after that.

The major platform chooses SplashRelease [S] or ConventionalRelease [C].

The smaller platform then decides whether to TrackLaunch [Observe] or IgnoreLaunch [Not Observe].
Tracking the launch costs 30 points.

If it tracks the launch, it sees the major platform’s move before choosing its own response.
If it ignores the launch, it chooses without that information.

The smaller platform then chooses PushSignatureShow [s] or HoldLineup [c].

If the major platform chose SplashRelease [S], the smaller platform tracked the move, and then chose PushSignatureShow [s], 
the major platform gets 500 and the smaller platform gets 170.
If the major platform chose SplashRelease [S], the smaller platform tracked the move, and then chose HoldLineup [c], 
the major platform gets 300 and the smaller platform gets 70.

If the major platform chose SplashRelease [S], the smaller platform did not track the move, and then chose PushSignatureShow [s], 
the major platform gets 500 and the smaller platform gets 200.
If the major platform chose SplashRelease [S], the smaller platform did not track the move, and then chose HoldLineup [c], 
the major platform gets 300 and the smaller platform gets 100.

If the major platform chose ConventionalRelease [C], the smaller platform did not track the move, and then chose PushSignatureShow [s], 
the major platform gets 600 and the smaller platform gets 300.
If the major platform chose ConventionalRelease [C], the smaller platform did not track the move, and then chose HoldLineup [c], 
the major platform gets 400 and the smaller platform gets 400.

If the major platform chose ConventionalRelease [C], the smaller platform tracked the move, and then chose PushSignatureShow [s], 
the major platform gets 600 and the smaller platform gets 270.
If the major platform chose ConventionalRelease [C], the smaller platform tracked the move, and then chose HoldLineup [c], 
the major platform gets 400 and the smaller platform gets 370.
