V5 (space habitat supply planning)

At the outset, a mission planner may face either a high-resource window or a low-resource window, with probability 0.5 for each case.
The mission planner learns which window applies.
The logistics team does not observe whether resources are high or low.

If it is a high-resource window, the mission planner chooses between the standard supply plan and the expanded supply plan.
If the mission planner chooses the standard supply plan in the high-resource window, the final reward is 4 for the mission planner and 4 for the logistics team.
If the mission planner chooses the expanded supply plan in the high-resource window, the logistics team then chooses between option X and option Y.
If the logistics team chooses option X there, the final reward is 6 for the mission planner and 4 for the logistics team.
If the logistics team chooses option Y there, the final reward is 0 for the mission planner and 0 for the logistics team.

If it is a low-resource window, the mission planner chooses between the standard supply plan and the expanded supply plan.
If the mission planner chooses the standard supply plan in the low-resource window, the final reward is 4 for the mission planner and 2 for the logistics team.
If the mission planner chooses the expanded supply plan in the low-resource window, the logistics team then chooses between option X and option Y.
The logistics team cannot tell whether this decision follows the expanded supply plan in the high-resource window or in the low-resource window.
If the logistics team chooses option X there, the final reward is 6 for the mission planner and 0 for the logistics team.
If the logistics team chooses option Y there, the final reward is 0 for the mission planner and 6 for the logistics team.
