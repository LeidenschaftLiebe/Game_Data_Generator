Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:

Player 1 moves first at the root and chooses between C and D.
Player 2 moves only after C, choosing between c and d.
Player 3 moves after D and after C then d, and these two player 3 nodes are in the same information set.
At terminal C_then_c, the payoffs are (1, 1, 1).
