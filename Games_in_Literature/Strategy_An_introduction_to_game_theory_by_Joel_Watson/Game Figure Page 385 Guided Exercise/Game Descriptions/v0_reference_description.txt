At the outset, the shoes may either be on sale or not on sale, with probability 0.5 for each case.
Amy learns whether the shoes are on sale.
Brenda does not observe this directly.

If the shoes are on sale, Amy chooses whether to take the newspaper to Brenda or not.
If Amy takes the newspaper when the shoes are on sale, Brenda then chooses whether to drive Amy to the mall or not.
If Brenda drives Amy after seeing the newspaper in the sale case, the final reward is 1 for Amy and 1 for Brenda.
If Brenda refuses after seeing the newspaper in the sale case, the final reward is -2 for Amy and 0 for Brenda.

If Amy does not take the newspaper when the shoes are on sale, Brenda still chooses whether to drive Amy to the mall or not.
If Brenda drives Amy after Amy does not take the newspaper in the sale case, the final reward is 3 for Amy and 1 for Brenda.
If Brenda refuses after Amy does not take the newspaper in the sale case, the final reward is 0 for Amy and 0 for Brenda.

If the shoes are not on sale, Amy again chooses whether to take the newspaper to Brenda or not.
If Amy takes the newspaper when the shoes are not on sale, Brenda then chooses whether to drive Amy to the mall or not.
If Brenda drives Amy after seeing the newspaper in the no-sale case, the final reward is -1 for Amy and -1 for Brenda.
If Brenda refuses after seeing the newspaper in the no-sale case, the final reward is -2 for Amy and 0 for Brenda.

If Amy does not take the newspaper when the shoes are not on sale, Brenda still chooses whether to drive Amy to the mall or not.
Brenda cannot tell from Amy’s refusal to show the paper whether the shoes are on sale or not.
If Brenda drives Amy after Amy does not take the newspaper in the no-sale case, the final reward is 1 for Amy and -1 for Brenda.
If Brenda refuses after Amy does not take the newspaper in the no-sale case, the final reward is 0 for Amy and 0 for Brenda.
