V5 — Expedition Slot

A mission leader is deciding who gets a final expedition seat.
The leader first chooses between Choose Specialist 2 (L), Coin Toss (M), and Choose Specialist 3 (R).

If the leader chooses Choose Specialist 2 (L), Specialist 2 then chooses between Remain Cooperative (l) and Push Back (r).
If the path is Choose Specialist 2 (L), Remain Cooperative (l), 
the outcome gives reward 3 to the leader, reward 0 to Specialist 2, and reward 1 to Specialist 3.
If the path is Choose Specialist 2 (L), Push Back (r), 
the outcome gives reward 1 to the leader, reward -1 to Specialist 2, and reward 1 to Specialist 3.

If the leader chooses Choose Specialist 3 (R), Specialist 3 then chooses between Remain Cooperative (l) and Push Back (r).
If the path is Choose Specialist 3 (R), Remain Cooperative (l), 
the outcome gives reward 3 to the leader, reward 1 to Specialist 2, and reward 0 to Specialist 3.
If the path is Choose Specialist 3 (R), Push Back (r), 
the outcome gives reward 1 to the leader, reward 1 to Specialist 2, and reward -1 to Specialist 3.

If the leader chooses Coin Toss (M), chance selects the left or right side with probability 1/2 each, 
and the corresponding specialist then responds as above.
