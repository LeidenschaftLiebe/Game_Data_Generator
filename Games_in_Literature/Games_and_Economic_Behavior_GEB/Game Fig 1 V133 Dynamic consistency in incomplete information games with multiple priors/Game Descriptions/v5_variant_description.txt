V5 [Film greenlight process]

Production conditions first turn out to be LimitedAudience (L), RealHit (R), or OutsideDisruption (O).

The producer can tell whether the situation is OutsideDisruption (O) or not.
If it is not OutsideDisruption (O), the producer chooses between BigBudget (M) and NarrowRelease (N).

If conditions are LimitedAudience (L) and the producer chooses BigBudget (M), the result is 0, 0.
If conditions are LimitedAudience (L) and the producer chooses NarrowRelease (N), the result is 101, 0.

If conditions are RealHit (R) and the producer chooses BigBudget (M), the result is 101, 0.
If conditions are RealHit (R) and the producer chooses NarrowRelease (N), then the studio head moves.

If conditions are OutsideDisruption (O), the studio head also moves.
The studio head chooses between StreamingPush (S) and TheatricalPush (T) without knowing whether the hidden case 
is RealHit (R) after NarrowRelease (N) or directly OutsideDisruption (O).

If the hidden case is RealHit (R), both StreamingPush (S) and TheatricalPush (T) give 100, 0.
If the hidden case is OutsideDisruption (O), both StreamingPush (S) and TheatricalPush (T) give -1, 0.
