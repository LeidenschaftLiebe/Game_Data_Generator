V5 (cybersecurity platform)

Player 1 controls a security platform that becomes useful only if both players invest in making it operational.
At the beginning, Player 1 chooses whether to invest in the platform or not.
If Player 1 does not invest, the process ends immediately and the final reward is 0 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.

If Player 1 invests, then Player 2 chooses whether to invest as well.
If Player 2 does not invest, the process ends and the final reward is -2 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.

If Player 2 also invests, then Player 1 decides how to use the working platform.
Player 1 can keep it for private use or share access with Player 2.
If Player 1 keeps it for private use, the final reward is 6 for Player 1 and -2 for Player 2.
If Player 1 shares access, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 2 for Player 2.
