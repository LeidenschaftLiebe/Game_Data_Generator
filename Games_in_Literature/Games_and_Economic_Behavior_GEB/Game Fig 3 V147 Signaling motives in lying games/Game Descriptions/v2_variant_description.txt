V2 [Expense reimbursement]

An employee is deciding whether to use a reimbursement channel that may later allow flexible reporting.
The employee first chooses between StandardRoute (i = 0) and FlexibleRoute (i = 1).

After StandardRoute (i = 0), chance leads to the reporting stage with probability 1/10 and to the automatic route with probability 9/10.
After FlexibleRoute (i = 1), chance leads to the reporting stage with probability 3/5 and to a different automatic route with probability 2/5.

Chance then determines the true expense level: SmallExpense (j = 1) or LargeExpense (j = 2), each with probability 1/2.
If the employee ends up on an automatic route, the result equals the true level: 0 after SmallExpense (j = 1) and 2 after LargeExpense (j = 2).

If the employee reaches the reporting stage, they choose between ClaimSmall (a = 1) and ClaimLarge (a = 2).
After SmallExpense (j = 1), ClaimSmall (a = 1) gives 0 and ClaimLarge (a = 2) gives 2.
After LargeExpense (j = 2), ClaimSmall (a = 1) gives 0 and ClaimLarge (a = 2) gives 2.
