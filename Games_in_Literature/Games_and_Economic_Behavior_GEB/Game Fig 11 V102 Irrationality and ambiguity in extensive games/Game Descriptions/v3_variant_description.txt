V3 — Film Campaign

A producer is deciding how to handle a film campaign.
The producer moves first and chooses between Drop Project (O), Festival Route (L), and Wide Release (R).
If the producer chooses Drop Project (O), the game ends immediately with reward 2 to the producer and reward 1 to the distributor.

If the producer chooses Festival Route (L) or Wide Release (R), the distributor moves next.
The distributor cannot tell which of those two release plans was chosen.
The distributor then chooses between Light Support (A) and Heavy Support (B).

If the path is Festival Route (L), Light Support (A), the producer moves again.
The producer then chooses between Keep Pushing (C) and Withdraw Entry (D).
If Keep Pushing (C) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 2 to the producer and reward 3 to the distributor.
If Withdraw Entry (D) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 0 to the producer and reward 2 to the distributor.

If the path is Festival Route (L), Heavy Support (B), the producer moves again.
The producer then chooses between Continue Campaign (E) and End Campaign (F).
If Continue Campaign (E) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer and reward 4 to the distributor.
If End Campaign (F) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 0 to both players.

If the path is Wide Release (R), Light Support (A), the producer moves again.
The producer then chooses between Expand Release (G) and Cut Scope (H).
If Expand Release (G) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 3 to the producer and reward 5 to the distributor.
If Cut Scope (H) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 0 to the producer and reward 1 to the distributor.

If the path is Wide Release (R), Heavy Support (B), the producer moves again.
The producer then chooses between Keep Promotion (J) and Cancel Promotion (K).
If Keep Promotion (J) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 1 to the producer and reward 4 to the distributor.
If Cancel Promotion (K) is chosen, the outcome gives reward 0 to both players.
