V1 — Clear Route or Foggy Route

At START, the courier chooses between ClearRoute and FoggyRoute.

Under ClearRoute, the courier reaches CheckpointX [X] first.
There, the courier takes the side road with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or stays on the main road with probability 4/7.

If the courier stays on the main road, the courier reaches CheckpointY [Y].
There, the courier takes the side road with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or stays on the main road with probability 4/7 and gets 1.

Under FoggyRoute, the courier also reaches CheckpointX [X] first.
There, the courier takes the side road with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or stays on the main road with probability 1/2.

If the courier stays on the main road, the courier reaches CheckpointY [Y].
There, the courier takes the side road with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or stays on the main road with probability 1/2 and gets 1.

Under FoggyRoute, the courier cannot tell whether the current checkpoint is CheckpointX [X] or CheckpointY [Y].
