V3 (museum security incident)

At the outset, a museum guard may be either fully ready or exhausted, with probability 0.5 for each case.
The guard knows which condition applies.
The intruder does not observe the guard’s condition.

If the guard is fully ready, the guard chooses whether to stay behind the desk or step out into the gallery.
If the guard stays behind the desk, the intruder then chooses whether to back off or press forward.
If the intruder backs off after the guard stays behind the desk, the final reward is 1 for the guard and 0 for the intruder.
If the intruder presses forward after the guard stays behind the desk, the final reward is 0 for the guard and -2 for the intruder.

If the guard steps out while fully ready, the intruder again chooses whether to back off or press forward.
The intruder observes that the guard stepped out, but still does not know whether the guard is fully ready or exhausted.
If the intruder backs off after the guard steps out while fully ready, the final reward is 1 for the guard and 0 for the intruder.
If the intruder presses forward after the guard steps out while fully ready, the final reward is 0 for the guard and -2 for the intruder.

If the guard is exhausted, the guard chooses whether to stay behind the desk or step out.
If the guard stays behind the desk while exhausted, the intruder then chooses whether to back off or press forward.
If the intruder backs off there, the final reward is 1 for the guard and 0 for the intruder.
If the intruder presses forward there, the final reward is -1 for the guard and 1 for the intruder.

If the guard steps out while exhausted, the intruder again chooses whether to back off or press forward.
The intruder observes the step out, but does not know the guard’s true condition.
If the intruder backs off there, the final reward is 0.5 for the guard and 0 for the intruder.
If the intruder presses forward there, the final reward is -1.5 for the guard and 1 for the intruder.
