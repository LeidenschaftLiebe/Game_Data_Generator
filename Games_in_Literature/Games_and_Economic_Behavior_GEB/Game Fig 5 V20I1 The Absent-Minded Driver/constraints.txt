Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:

The player moves first at the root and chooses between ClearHeaded and AbsentMinded.
After ClearHeaded, there are two successive chance nodes. At each one, EXIT occurs with probability 3/7 and CONT occurs with probability 4/7.
After AbsentMinded, there are two successive chance nodes. At each one, EXIT occurs with probability 1/2 and CONT occurs with probability 1/2.
Under ClearHeaded and AbsentMinded: All other outcomes are 0; Only continuing past the second node leads to payoff 1.
