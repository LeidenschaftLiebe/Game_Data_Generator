Strategic Equivalence Notion: Better-response equivalence

Constraints:
Check that the firm is high quality with probability 0.5 and low quality with probability 0.5.
Check that the worker’s two decision nodes after an offer are in the same information set.
Check that if no offer is made, both sides get 0.
Check that if a high-quality firm makes an offer and the worker accepts, the final reward is 2 for the firm and 2 for the worker.
