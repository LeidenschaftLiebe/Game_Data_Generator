V5 [Ancient tunnel expedition]

A hidden assessment first determines whether the expedition crew is StableCrew or FragileCrew.
StableCrew happens with probability 9/10, and FragileCrew happens with probability 1/10.

The expedition chief then chooses between Withdraw and GoInside.

If the expedition chief chooses Withdraw, the day ends there.
This gives 0 to the chief, and 0 to the navigator.

If the expedition chief chooses GoInside, the navigator chooses between UpPassage, DownPassage, and CrossPassage.
The navigator cannot tell whether the crew is StableCrew or FragileCrew.

If the crew is StableCrew and the navigator chooses UpPassage, this gives 2 to the chief, and 1 to the navigator.
If the crew is StableCrew and the navigator chooses DownPassage, this gives -1 to the chief, and 0 to the navigator.
If the crew is StableCrew and the navigator chooses CrossPassage, this gives 1 to the chief, and -1 to the navigator.

If the crew is FragileCrew and the navigator chooses UpPassage, this gives 1 to the chief, and 0 to the navigator.
If the crew is FragileCrew and the navigator chooses DownPassage, this gives -1 to the chief, and 1 to the navigator.
If the crew is FragileCrew and the navigator chooses CrossPassage, this gives 3 to the chief, and -1 to the navigator.
