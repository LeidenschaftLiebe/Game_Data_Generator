V1 [Startup funding]

At the start, a hidden draw determines the planning type of the founder and the investor.
The founder’s type is 01, 11, or 21, each with probability 1/3.
The investor’s type is 02 with probability 1/10 and 12 with probability 9/10.

So the 6 hidden type combinations have probabilities:
(01,02) = 1/30, (01,12) = 3/10, (11,02) = 1/30, (11,12) = 3/10, (21,02) = 1/30, and (21,12) = 3/10.

The founder does not know the investor’s type.
The investor does not know the founder’s type.

At the first move, the founder knows only the founder’s own type.
The founder first chooses between SeekSeedRound (Invest1) and StopNow (Out1).

If the founder chooses StopNow (Out1), the result is 5, 0.

If the founder chooses SeekSeedRound (Invest1), the investor moves and chooses between JoinRound (Invest2) and PassNow (Out2).

At that point, the investor observes that the founder chose SeekSeedRound (Invest1),
and the investor knows the investor’s own type,
but still does not know whether the founder is type 01, 11, or 21.

If the investor chooses PassNow (Out2), the result is 0, 6.

If the investor chooses JoinRound (Invest2), the founder moves again and chooses between ScaleFully (Invest3) and CashOutLate (Out3).

At that point, the founder observes the earlier realized actions, knows the founder’s own type, 
but still does not know whether the investor is type 02 or 12.

ScaleFully (Invest3) gives 8, 8, and CashOutLate (Out3) gives 7, 0.
