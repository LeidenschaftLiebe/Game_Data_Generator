V1 — Recruiter, employer, and candidate

There are three people in this hiring process: a recruiter, an employer, and a candidate.

The recruiter moves first and chooses whether to Pass (Out₁) or Send Forward (In₁).

If the recruiter chooses Pass (Out₁), the process ends at once.
The recruiter gets reward 2, while the employer and candidate each get 0.

If the recruiter chooses Send Forward (In₁), the employer moves next.

The employer then chooses between Strong Offer (U₂) and Weak Offer (D₂).

After that, the candidate makes the last decision, but the candidate does not know which offer level the employer chose.

The candidate chooses between Accept (U₃) and Reject (D₃).

If the path is Send Forward (In₁), Strong Offer (U₂), Accept (U₃), all three end with reward 3.

If the path is Send Forward (In₁), Strong Offer (U₂), Reject (D₃), all three end with reward 0.

If the path is Send Forward (In₁), Weak Offer (D₂), Accept (U₃), all three end with reward 0.

If the path is Send Forward (In₁), Weak Offer (D₂), Reject (D₃), all three end with reward 1.
