V0 — More Challenging Absent-Minded Driver Example

A driver starts at START and keeps moving forward along the road.

Along the way, the driver may arrive at X, Y, or Z.

At each of these points, the driver chooses EXIT or CONT.

The driver cannot tell whether the current point is X, Y, or Z.
The same reasoning therefore applies at all three points.

If the driver exits at X, the reward is 7.

If the driver exits at Y, the reward is 0.

If the driver exits at Z, the reward is 22.

If the driver continues past Z, the reward is 2.
