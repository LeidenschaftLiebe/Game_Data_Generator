Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints

Player 1 moves first. Player 2 then moves without observing whether Player 1 chose U or D.
Player 2’s two nodes are in the same infoset and have the same three actions: L, R, and X.
Seed payoff constraint: U_L gives 1, 3.
Only after D_X does Player 1 move again, choosing between u and d, with payoffs 2, 1 and 0, 1.
