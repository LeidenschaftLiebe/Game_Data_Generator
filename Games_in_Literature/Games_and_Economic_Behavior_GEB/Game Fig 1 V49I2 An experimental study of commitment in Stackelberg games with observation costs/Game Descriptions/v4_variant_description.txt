V4 — Retail Promotion Decision

A large retailer commits to a promotion plan first, and a rival retailer responds afterward.

The large retailer chooses BigPromotion [S] or BasePromotion [C].

The rival retailer then decides whether to CheckFlyers [Observe] or DoNotCheck [Not Observe].
Checking the flyers costs 30 points.

If the rival checks, it sees the earlier promotion plan before making its own decision.
If it does not check, it must choose without that information.

The rival retailer then chooses StrongMatch [s] or LimitedMatch [c].

If the large retailer chose BigPromotion [S], the rival checked, and then chose StrongMatch [s], 
the large retailer gets 500 and the rival gets 170.
If the large retailer chose BigPromotion [S], the rival checked, and then chose LimitedMatch [c], 
the large retailer gets 300 and the rival gets 70.

If the large retailer chose BigPromotion [S], the rival did not check, and then chose StrongMatch [s], 
the large retailer gets 500 and the rival gets 200.
If the large retailer chose BigPromotion [S], the rival did not check, and then chose LimitedMatch [c], 
the large retailer gets 300 and the rival gets 100.

If the large retailer chose BasePromotion [C], the rival did not check, and then chose StrongMatch [s], 
the large retailer gets 600 and the rival gets 300.
If the large retailer chose BasePromotion [C], the rival did not check, and then chose LimitedMatch [c], 
the large retailer gets 400 and the rival gets 400.

If the large retailer chose BasePromotion [C], the rival checked, and then chose StrongMatch [s], 
the large retailer gets 600 and the rival gets 270.
If the large retailer chose BasePromotion [C], the rival checked, and then chose LimitedMatch [c], 
the large retailer gets 400 and the rival gets 370.
