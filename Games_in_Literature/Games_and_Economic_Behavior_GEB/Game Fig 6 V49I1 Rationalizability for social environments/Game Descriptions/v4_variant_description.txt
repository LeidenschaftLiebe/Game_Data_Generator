V4 — Film Slate Selection

A studio can greenlight Film A [x1], Film B [x2], or keep the slate unchanged at No New Film [x0].

At the start, the studio head sets an unseen ranking for what happens if both projects are pushed at once.
The order is either AFirst [ (x1, x2) ] or BFirst [ (x2, x1) ].

Then Producer A chooses BackProject [1] or StepBack [0] for Film A [x1].

After that, Producer B chooses BackProject [1] or StepBack [0] for Film B [x2].

Producer A does not know which ranking was set.
Producer B does not know which ranking was set, and also does not know Producer A’s choice.

If neither producer backs a project, the result is No New Film [x0], and both producers get 0.

If only Producer A backs a project, the result is Film A [x1].
Producer A gets 2 and Producer B gets 1.

If only Producer B backs a project, the result is Film B [x2].
Producer A gets 1 and Producer B gets 2.

If both producers back their projects, the chosen ranking decides which film is greenlit.
