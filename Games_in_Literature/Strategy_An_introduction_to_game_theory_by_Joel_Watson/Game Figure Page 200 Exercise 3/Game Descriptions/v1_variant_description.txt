V1 (deep-sea exploration program)

A mission leader first chooses between exploration plan A and exploration plan B.
If the leader chooses exploration plan B, the mission ends immediately with a final reward of 5 for the leader and 4 for the operations chief.

If the leader chooses exploration plan A, the operations chief then chooses between route C and route D.
If the operations chief chooses route D, the leader then chooses between survey package I and survey package J.
Survey package I gives a final reward of 9 to the leader and 2 to the operations chief.
Survey package J gives a final reward of 4 to the leader and 5 to the operations chief.

If the operations chief chooses route C, the leader then chooses between sector plan G and sector plan H.
Sector plan H gives a final reward of 7 to the leader and 3 to the operations chief.
Sector plan G leads to one more choice by the operations chief between support option E and support option F.

If the operations chief chooses support option E, the leader then chooses between equipment set K and equipment set L.
Equipment set K gives a final reward of 2 to the leader and 4 to the operations chief.
Equipment set L gives a final reward of 3 to the leader and 6 to the operations chief.

If the operations chief chooses support option F, the leader then chooses between equipment set M and equipment set N.
Equipment set M gives a final reward of 6 to the leader and 7 to the operations chief.
Equipment set N gives a final reward of 8 to the leader and 1 to the operations chief.
