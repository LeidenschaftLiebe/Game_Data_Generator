V4 [Online marketplace guarantee]

A buyer, a platform seller, and a passive observer are involved in a guarantee decision.
The buyer first chooses between KeepPayment (Out) and ReleasePayment (In).

If the buyer chooses KeepPayment (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the buyer, 100 for the seller, and 30 for the observer.

If the buyer chooses ReleasePayment (In), 25 units from the buyer’s own funds are handed to the seller.
The seller then chooses between RefundSmallPart (Left) and RefundFullAmount (Right).

If the seller chooses RefundSmallPart (Left), the seller keeps 20 and returns 5 to the buyer.
That returned amount is doubled, so the buyer receives 10.
The result is 155 for the buyer, 120 for the seller, and 30 for the observer.

If the seller chooses RefundFullAmount (Right), the seller returns the full 25 to the buyer.
That amount is doubled, so the buyer receives 50.
The result is 195 for the buyer, 100 for the seller, and 30 for the observer.
