V2 — Wildlife Corridor

A conservation director is deciding how to manage a wildlife corridor.
The director moves first and chooses between Active Monitoring (a1) and Leave Undisturbed (b1).

If the director chooses Leave Undisturbed (b1), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the director,
reward 2 to the field coordinator, and reward 0 to the ecologist.

If the director chooses Active Monitoring (a1), the field coordinator moves next and chooses between
Deploy Camera Grid (a2) and Deploy Tracking Team (b2).

If the field coordinator chooses Deploy Camera Grid (a2), the ecologist then chooses between
Tag Now (a3) and Observe Longer (b3).
If the path is a1, a2, a3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the director, reward 1 to the coordinator,
and reward 0 to the ecologist.
If the path is a1, a2, b3, the outcome gives reward 3 to the director, reward 3 to the coordinator,
and reward 1 to the ecologist.

If the field coordinator chooses Deploy Tracking Team (b2), the ecologist then chooses between
Lowland Route (c3) and Highland Route (d3).
If the path is a1, b2, c3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the director, reward 2 to the coordinator,
and reward 1 to the ecologist.
If the path is a1, b2, d3, the outcome gives reward 4 to the director, reward 0 to the coordinator,
and reward 0 to the ecologist.
