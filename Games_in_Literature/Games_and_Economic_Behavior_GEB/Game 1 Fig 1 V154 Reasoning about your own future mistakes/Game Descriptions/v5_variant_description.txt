A job applicant is deciding how to respond to an employer.
At the start, the applicant chooses between send the polished message now (b) and revise it one more time first (a).
Sending it now leads directly to outcome 1.
Revising it first creates a later choice between send the correct version (c) and send the wrong attachment (d).
Sending the correct version leads to outcome 1.
Sending the wrong attachment leads to outcome 0.
The applicant understands that adding one more step also adds one more chance for an avoidable mistake.
