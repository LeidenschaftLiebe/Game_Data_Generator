V4 (museum fundraising drive)

At the outset, a museum director may face either a strong-donor season or a weak-donor season, with probability 0.5 for each case.
The director learns which season has arrived.
The fundraising partner does not observe whether donor conditions are strong or weak.

If it is a strong-donor season, the director chooses between the regular campaign and the premium campaign.
If the director chooses the regular campaign in the strong-donor season, the final reward is 4 for the director and 4 for the fundraising partner.
If the director chooses the premium campaign in the strong-donor season, the fundraising partner then chooses between plan X and plan Y.
If the fundraising partner chooses plan X there, the final reward is 6 for the director and 4 for the fundraising partner.
If the fundraising partner chooses plan Y there, the final reward is 0 for the director and 0 for the fundraising partner.

If it is a weak-donor season, the director chooses between the regular campaign and the premium campaign.
If the director chooses the regular campaign in the weak-donor season, the final reward is 4 for the director and 2 for the fundraising partner.
If the director chooses the premium campaign in the weak-donor season, the fundraising partner then chooses between plan X and plan Y.
The fundraising partner cannot tell whether this decision follows the premium campaign in the strong-donor season or in the weak-donor season.
If the fundraising partner chooses plan X there, the final reward is 6 for the director and 0 for the fundraising partner.
If the fundraising partner chooses plan Y there, the final reward is 0 for the director and 6 for the fundraising partner.
