V2 (festival souvenir bag)

A festival organizer is randomly assigned one of two moods, generous or prankish, with probability 0.5 for each.
The organizer knows which mood applies.

If the organizer is generous, he chooses whether to give no souvenir bag or to hand over a sealed souvenir bag to a visitor.
If he gives no bag, the final reward is 0 for the organizer and 0 for the visitor.
If he gives the bag, the visitor then chooses whether to accept it or turn it down.
If the visitor accepts a bag from a generous organizer, the final reward is 1 for the organizer and 1 for the visitor.
If the visitor turns down a bag from a generous organizer, the final reward is -1 for the organizer and 0 for the visitor.

If the organizer is prankish, he chooses whether to give no souvenir bag or to hand over a sealed souvenir bag.
If he gives no bag, the final reward is 0 for the organizer and 0 for the visitor.
If he gives the bag, the visitor again chooses whether to accept it or turn it down.
The visitor cannot tell whether the sealed bag comes from a generous organizer or a prankish organizer.
If the visitor accepts a bag from a prankish organizer, the final reward is 1 for the organizer and -1 for the visitor.
If the visitor turns down a bag from a prankish organizer, the final reward is -1 for the organizer and 0 for the visitor.
