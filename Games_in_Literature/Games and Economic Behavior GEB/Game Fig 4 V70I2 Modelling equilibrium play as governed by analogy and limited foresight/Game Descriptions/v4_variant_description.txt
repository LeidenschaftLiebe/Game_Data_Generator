V4 — Archive Transfer

An archive director is deciding how to move a collection.
The director moves first and chooses between Begin Transfer (L) and Pause Transfer (R).

If the director chooses Pause Transfer (R), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the director and reward 2 to the restoration lead.

If the director chooses Begin Transfer (L), the director moves again and chooses between Slow Packing (L) and Fast Packing (R).

If the director chooses Slow Packing (L), the restoration lead then chooses between Label First (l) and Skip Labels (r).
If the path is Begin Transfer (L), Slow Packing (L), Label First (l), the outcome gives reward 0 to the director and reward 1 to the restoration lead.
If the path is Begin Transfer (L), Slow Packing (L), Skip Labels (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the director chooses Fast Packing (R), the restoration lead then chooses between Label First (l) and Skip Labels (r).
If the path is Begin Transfer (L), Fast Packing (R), Label First (l), the outcome gives reward 1 to the director and reward 0 to the restoration lead.
If the path is Begin Transfer (L), Fast Packing (R), Skip Labels (r), the outcome gives reward 4 to the director and reward 1 to the restoration lead.
