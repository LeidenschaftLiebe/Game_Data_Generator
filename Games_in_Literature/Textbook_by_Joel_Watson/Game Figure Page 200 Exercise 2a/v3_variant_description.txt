V3 (theme park attraction design)

A park director first chooses between the waterfront expansion and the central-park expansion.

If the director chooses the waterfront expansion, the operations chief then chooses between design branch Alpha and design branch Beta.
If the operations chief chooses design branch Alpha, the director then chooses between ride concept X and ride concept Y.
When making that choice, the director cannot tell whether the operations chief previously selected branch Alpha or branch Beta.
Ride concept X after branch Alpha gives a final reward of 3 to the director and 0 to the operations chief.
Ride concept Y after branch Alpha gives a final reward of 8 to the director and 5 to the operations chief.

If the operations chief chooses design branch Beta, the director again chooses between ride concept X and ride concept Y at that same hidden-information point.
Ride concept X after branch Beta gives a final reward of 4 to the director and 6 to the operations chief.
Ride concept Y after branch Beta gives a final reward of 2 to the director and 1 to the operations chief.

If the director chooses the central-park expansion, the operations chief then chooses between construction option C and construction option D.
Construction option C gives a final reward of 6 to the director and 4 to the operations chief.
Construction option D gives a final reward of 3 to the director and 2 to the operations chief.
