V3 [Mars habitat repair]

A mission commander and an engineer are deciding how to repair a damaged habitat section.

The mission commander first chooses between SealSection (X), TopAccess (T), and BottomAccess (B).

If the mission commander chooses SealSection (X), the job ends there.
This gives 4 to the mission commander, and 4 to the engineer.

If the mission commander chooses TopAccess (T), the engineer chooses between LeftDuct (L) and RightDuct (R).
If the mission commander chooses BottomAccess (B), the engineer again chooses between LeftDuct (L) and RightDuct (R).

When making that choice, the engineer cannot tell whether the earlier access plan was TopAccess (T) or BottomAccess (B).

After that, the mission commander chooses between UpperToolset (U) and DeepToolset (D).
When making that later choice, the mission commander cannot tell which earlier pair of choices led there.

After TopAccess (T) and LeftDuct (L), UpperToolset (U) gives 5 to the mission commander, and 0 to the engineer, 
while DeepToolset (D) gives 2 to the mission commander, and 1 to the engineer.

After TopAccess (T) and RightDuct (R), UpperToolset (U) gives 2 to the mission commander, and 0 to the engineer, 
while DeepToolset (D) gives 0 to the mission commander, and 1 to the engineer.

After BottomAccess (B) and LeftDuct (L), UpperToolset (U) gives 0 to the mission commander, and 1 to the engineer, 
while DeepToolset (D) gives 2 to the mission commander, and 0 to the engineer.

After BottomAccess (B) and RightDuct (R), UpperToolset (U) gives 2 to the mission commander, and 1 to the engineer, 
while DeepToolset (D) gives 5 to the mission commander, and 0 to the engineer.
