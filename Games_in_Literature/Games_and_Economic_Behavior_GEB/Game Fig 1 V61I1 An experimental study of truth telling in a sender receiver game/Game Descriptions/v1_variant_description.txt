V1 — Product Safety Report

There are two players, a Manufacturer and an Inspector.
At the start, chance selects one of two hidden conditions: Routine Case (A) or Critical Case (B).

The Manufacturer learns which condition was selected.
The Manufacturer then sends either Routine Report (A) or Critical Report (B).

After seeing the report, the Inspector chooses between Approve Release (U) and
Delay Release (D).

If the true condition is Routine Case (A) and the Inspector chooses
Approve Release (U), the outcome gives reward 2 to the Manufacturer and reward 1 to the Inspector.
If the true condition is Routine Case (A) and the Inspector chooses
Delay Release (D), the outcome gives reward 1 to the Manufacturer and reward 2 to the Inspector.

If the true condition is Critical Case (B) and the Inspector chooses
Approve Release (U), the outcome gives reward 1 to the Manufacturer and reward 2 to the Inspector.
If the true condition is Critical Case (B) and the Inspector chooses
Delay Release (D), the outcome gives reward 2 to the Manufacturer and reward 1 to the Inspector.
