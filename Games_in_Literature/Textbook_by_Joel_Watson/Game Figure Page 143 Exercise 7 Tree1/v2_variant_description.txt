V2 (cybersecurity contract)

A company first chooses between contract option A, contract option B, and contract option C.
If the company chooses contract option C, the negotiation ends immediately and each side gets a final return of 5.

If the company chooses contract option A, an outside contractor then chooses between action X and action Y.
If the contractor chooses action X after contract option A, each side gets a final return of 8.
If the contractor chooses action Y after contract option A, each side gets 0.

If the company chooses contract option B, the contractor again chooses between action X and action Y.
When making that choice, the contractor cannot tell whether the earlier decision was contract option A or contract option B.
If the contractor chooses action X after contract option B, each side gets a final return of 2.
If the contractor chooses action Y after contract option B, each side gets a final return of 6.
