V1 [Lottery entry]

A participant is deciding whether to enter a lottery version that can be manipulated after the number is seen.
The participant first chooses between StaySafe (i = 0) and SeekOpportunity (i = 1).

If the participant chooses StaySafe (i = 0), chance sends them to the manipulable stage with probability 1/10 
and to the fixed-outcome route with probability 9/10.
If the participant chooses SeekOpportunity (i = 1), chance sends them to the manipulable stage with probability 3/5 
and to another fixed-outcome route with probability 2/5.

Chance then draws a hidden number, either LowState (j = 1) or HighState (j = 2), each with probability 1/2.
If the participant ends up on a fixed-outcome route, the result simply follows the hidden number: 
0 after LowState (j = 1) and 2 after HighState (j = 2).

If the participant reaches the manipulable stage, they choose between ReportLow (a = 1) and ReportHigh (a = 2).
After LowState (j = 1), ReportLow (a = 1) gives 0 and ReportHigh (a = 2) gives 2.
After HighState (j = 2), ReportLow (a = 1) gives 0 and ReportHigh (a = 2) gives 2.
