V2 — Film Production Deal

Producer 1 moves first and chooses StartProduction [A] or HoldProject [D].

If the producer chooses StartProduction [A], distributor 2 then chooses Greenlight [a] or RequestRewrite [d].
If the distributor chooses Greenlight [a], the process ends there and all three sides get 1.

If the producer chooses HoldProject [D], the matter goes to critic 3.
If the producer chooses StartProduction [A] and the distributor chooses RequestRewrite [d], the matter also goes to critic 3.

When critic 3 moves, the critic cannot tell whether the current situation came from HoldProject [D] or from StartProduction [A] followed by RequestRewrite [d].
The critic then chooses Praise [l] or Dismiss [r].

If critic 3 is acting after HoldProject [D] and chooses Praise [l], the rewards are 3, 2, and 2.
If critic 3 is acting after HoldProject [D] and chooses Dismiss [r], the rewards are 0, 0, and 0.

If critic 3 is acting after StartProduction [A] then RequestRewrite [d] and chooses Praise [l], the rewards are 4, 4, and 0.
If critic 3 is acting after StartProduction [A] then RequestRewrite [d] and chooses Dismiss [r], the rewards are 0, 0, and 1.
