V5 [Club member repayment]

A club president, a treasurer, and a member are involved in a resource decision.
The president first chooses between DoNothing (Out) and UseMemberFunds (In).

If the president chooses DoNothing (Out), the interaction ends immediately.
The result is 170 for the president, 100 for the treasurer, and 30 for the member.

If the president chooses UseMemberFunds (In), 25 units are taken from the member’s own funds and handed to the treasurer.
The treasurer then chooses between SmallRepayment (Left) and FullRepayment (Right).

If the treasurer chooses SmallRepayment (Left), the treasurer keeps 20 and sends 5 to the member.
That transferred amount is doubled, so the member receives 10.
The result is 170 for the president, 120 for the treasurer, and 15 for the member.

If the treasurer chooses FullRepayment (Right), the treasurer sends the full 25 to the member.
That amount is doubled, so the member receives 50.
The result is 170 for the president, 100 for the treasurer, and 55 for the member.
