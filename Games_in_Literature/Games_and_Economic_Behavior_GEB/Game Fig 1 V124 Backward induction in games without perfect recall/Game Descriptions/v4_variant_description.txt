V4 [Museum security]

A museum curator and a security lead are deciding how to handle a fragile artifact transfer.

A hidden circumstance first picks the left side or the right side, each with probability 1/2.

On the left side, the security lead moves first and chooses between StopTransfer (OUT) and ProceedTransfer (IN).

If the security lead chooses StopTransfer (OUT), this gives the curator 1, and the security lead -1.

If the security lead chooses ProceedTransfer (IN), then the curator chooses between SideHall (S) and DirectHall (D).
SideHall (S) gives the curator -2, and the security lead 2.
DirectHall (D) gives the curator 2, and the security lead -2.

On the right side, the curator moves first and chooses between CancelMove (Out) and StartMove (In).

If the curator chooses CancelMove (Out), this gives the curator -1, and the security lead 1.

If the curator chooses StartMove (In), the curator later chooses between SideHall (S) and DirectHall (D).
SideHall (S) gives the curator 2, and the security lead -2.
DirectHall (D) gives the curator -2, and the security lead 2.

At the upper choice, the curator cannot tell which earlier path led there.
