V0 — Perfect-Information Game

There are three players.
Player 1 moves first and chooses between a1 and b1.

If Player 1 chooses b1, the game ends immediately with reward 2 to Player 1, reward 2 to Player 2,
and reward 0 to Player 3.

If Player 1 chooses a1, Player 2 moves next and chooses between a2 and b2.

If Player 2 chooses a2, Player 3 then chooses between a3 and b3.
If the path is a1, a2, a3, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1, reward 1 to Player 2,
and reward 0 to Player 3.
If the path is a1, a2, b3, the outcome gives reward 3 to Player 1, reward 3 to Player 2,
and reward 1 to Player 3.

If Player 2 chooses b2, Player 3 then chooses between c3 and d3.
If the path is a1, b2, c3, the outcome gives reward 1 to Player 1, reward 2 to Player 2,
and reward 1 to Player 3.
If the path is a1, b2, d3, the outcome gives reward 4 to Player 1, reward 0 to Player 2,
and reward 0 to Player 3.
