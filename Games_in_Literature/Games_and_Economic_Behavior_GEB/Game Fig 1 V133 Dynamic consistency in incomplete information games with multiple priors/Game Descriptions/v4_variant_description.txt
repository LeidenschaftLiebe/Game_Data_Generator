V4 [Research project screening]

The research environment first turns out to be LowNovelty (L), RealBreakthrough (R), or OutsideNoise (O).

The principal investigator can tell whether the situation is OutsideNoise (O) or not.
If it is not OutsideNoise (O), the investigator chooses between MainTrack (M) and NewTrack (N).

If the environment is LowNovelty (L) and the investigator chooses MainTrack (M), the result is 0, 0.
If the environment is LowNovelty (L) and the investigator chooses NewTrack (N), the result is 101, 0.

If the environment is RealBreakthrough (R) and the investigator chooses MainTrack (M), the result is 101, 0.
If the environment is RealBreakthrough (R) and the investigator chooses NewTrack (N), then the committee chair moves.

If the environment is OutsideNoise (O), the committee chair also moves.
The committee chair chooses between SendForward (S) and TableIt (T) without knowing whether the hidden case 
is RealBreakthrough (R) after NewTrack (N) or directly OutsideNoise (O).

If the hidden case is RealBreakthrough (R), both SendForward (S) and TableIt (T) give 100, 0.
If the hidden case is OutsideNoise (O), both SendForward (S) and TableIt (T) give -1, 0.
