V1 [Online marketplace]

A seller and a buyer are considering a trade on an online marketplace.
The seller moves first and sends one of three messages: NoPromise (m(E)), WillRefund (m(S)), or WillKeepFullPayment (m(K)).

After seeing the message, the buyer chooses between Pay (I) and WalkAway (N).
If the buyer chooses WalkAway (N), the interaction ends immediately and the result is 2 for the buyer and 0 for the seller.

If the buyer chooses Pay (I), the seller then chooses between ShareFairly (S) and KeepAll (K).
After NoPromise (m(E)), ShareFairly (S) gives 4 to the buyer and 4 to the seller, 
while KeepAll (K) gives 0 to the buyer and 8 to the seller.

After WillRefund (m(S)), ShareFairly (S) gives 4 to the buyer and 4 to the seller.
If the seller instead chooses KeepAll (K), the seller is penalized for breaking the earlier message.
With f = 2, that result is 0 for the buyer and 6 for the seller.

After WillKeepFullPayment (m(K)), KeepAll (K) gives 0 to the buyer and 8 to the seller.
If the seller instead chooses ShareFairly (S), the seller is penalized because the later action does not match the message.
With f = 2, that result is 4 for the buyer and 2 for the seller.
