V2 (space bay guessing game)

In a training exercise on a space station, Lee hides inside Bay A or Bay B before Nova enters.
Marshal sees where Lee goes.
Nova then walks in, and Marshal announces either “Signal Red” or “Signal Green.”
After hearing that announcement, Nova chooses one of the two bays.
If Nova chooses the correct bay, Nova gets 100 and Lee gets 0.
If Nova chooses the wrong bay, Lee gets 100 and Nova gets 0.
Marshal gets 10 if Nova chooses Bay A and 0 if Nova chooses Bay B.

If Lee hides in Bay A and Marshal says Signal Red, Nova’s choice determines the result.
Choosing Bay A gives final rewards of 0 for Lee, 10 for Marshal, and 100 for Nova.
Choosing Bay B gives final rewards of 100 for Lee, 0 for Marshal, and 0 for Nova.

If Lee hides in Bay A and Marshal says Signal Green, Nova again chooses between Bay A and Bay B.
Choosing Bay A gives final rewards of 0 for Lee, 10 for Marshal, and 100 for Nova.
Choosing Bay B gives final rewards of 100 for Lee, 0 for Marshal, and 0 for Nova.

If Lee hides in Bay B and Marshal says Signal Red, Nova chooses between the two bays.
Choosing Bay A gives final rewards of 100 for Lee, 10 for Marshal, and 0 for Nova.
Choosing Bay B gives final rewards of 0 for Lee, 0 for Marshal, and 100 for Nova.

If Lee hides in Bay B and Marshal says Signal Green, Nova again chooses between the two bays.
Choosing Bay A gives final rewards of 100 for Lee, 10 for Marshal, and 0 for Nova.
Choosing Bay B gives final rewards of 0 for Lee, 0 for Marshal, and 100 for Nova.
