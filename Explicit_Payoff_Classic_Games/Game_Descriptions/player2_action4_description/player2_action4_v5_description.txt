Game Description 1: Student Council Election

In the heart of student council election, critical choices carve the path to triumph.

**Player1** evaluates the following strategies:
- free vending
- campus concerts
- mental health week
- green fund

Meanwhile, **Player2** plots their countermoves:
- poster blitz
- social media
- Q&A forum
- alliance with club

When both players commit to their choices, these outcomes unfold:

- If **Player1** selects **free vending**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **poster blitz**, the resulting payoff is (4, -4).
  - And **Player2** counters with **social media**, the resulting payoff is (65, -65).
  - And **Player2** counters with **Q&A forum**, the resulting payoff is (-11, 11).
  - And **Player2** counters with **alliance with club**, the resulting payoff is (-14, 14).

- If **Player1** selects **campus concerts**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **poster blitz**, the resulting payoff is (3, -3).
  - And **Player2** counters with **social media**, the resulting payoff is (20, -20).
  - And **Player2** counters with **Q&A forum**, the resulting payoff is (-24, 24).
  - And **Player2** counters with **alliance with club**, the resulting payoff is (-62, 62).

- If **Player1** selects **mental health week**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **poster blitz**, the resulting payoff is (-35, 35).
  - And **Player2** counters with **social media**, the resulting payoff is (51, -51).
  - And **Player2** counters with **Q&A forum**, the resulting payoff is (-25, 25).
  - And **Player2** counters with **alliance with club**, the resulting payoff is (36, -36).

- If **Player1** selects **green fund**:
  - And **Player2** counters with **poster blitz**, the resulting payoff is (79, -79).
  - And **Player2** counters with **social media**, the resulting payoff is (56, -56).
  - And **Player2** counters with **Q&A forum**, the resulting payoff is (-85, 85).
  - And **Player2** counters with **alliance with club**, the resulting payoff is (-29, 29).

In this dynamic contest, foresight and adaptation decide the victor.
