Game Description 1: A Viral Meme Contest

Under the bright lights of a viral meme contest, every decision shapes the future.

**Player1** stands ready to unleash either:
- A masterful **cat template**, or
- A daring **niche inside joke**.

Meanwhile, **Player2** prepares their countermove:
- A nimble **perfect caption**, or
- A cunning **absurd remix**.

Every pairing of choices leads to different consequences:

- If Player1 chooses **cat template**:
  - And Player2 responds with **perfect caption**, the resulting payoff is (15, -15).
  - And Player2 responds with **absurd remix**, the resulting payoff is (30, -30).

- If Player1 chooses **niche inside joke**:
  - And Player2 responds with **perfect caption**, the resulting payoff is (-15, 15).
  - And Player2 responds with **absurd remix**, the resulting payoff is (0, 0).

The sharpest strategy and the boldest timing will claim victory.
