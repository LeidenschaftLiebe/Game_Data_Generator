1. The Fishermen’s Choice

Two fishermen set out to sea. They can either team up to catch a whale or fish alone for tuna.

If both go for the whale, each receives a high catch.

If one goes for the whale while the other fishes tuna, the tuna fisher earns a small haul, while the whale hunter comes back with nothing.

If both fish for tuna, each secures a moderate catch.
