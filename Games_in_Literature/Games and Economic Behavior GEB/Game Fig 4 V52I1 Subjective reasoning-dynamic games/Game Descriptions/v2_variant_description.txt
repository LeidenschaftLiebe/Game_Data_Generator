V2 — Museum Security

A museum director may be facing one of two security situations.
Nature first determines whether it is a Small Risk with probability 0.1 or a Serious Risk with probability 0.9.

If it is a Small Risk, the director chooses between Keep Closed (Out1) and Escalate (In1).
If the director chooses Keep Closed (Out1), the matter ends with reward 0 to the director
and reward 0 to the security chief.

If it is a Serious Risk, the director chooses between Keep Closed (Out2) and Escalate (In2).
If the director chooses Keep Closed (Out2), the matter ends with reward 0 to the director
and reward 0 to the security chief.

If the director chooses Escalate (In1/In2), the security chief then chooses between
Lockdown (U), Extra Patrol (M), and Stand Down (D).
When the security chief moves, the chief knows the director escalated, but does not know which risk level was present.

If the risk was Small Risk and the chief chooses Lockdown (U),
the director gets reward -1 and the chief gets reward 3.
If the risk was Small Risk and the chief chooses Extra Patrol (M),
the director gets reward -1 and the chief gets reward 2.
If the risk was Small Risk and the chief chooses Stand Down (D),
the director gets reward 2 and the chief gets reward 0.

If the risk was Serious Risk and the chief chooses Lockdown (U),
the director gets reward -3 and the chief gets reward 0.
If the risk was Serious Risk and the chief chooses Extra Patrol (M),
the director gets reward -1 and the chief gets reward 2.
If the risk was Serious Risk and the chief chooses Stand Down (D),
the director gets reward 3 and the chief gets reward 3.
