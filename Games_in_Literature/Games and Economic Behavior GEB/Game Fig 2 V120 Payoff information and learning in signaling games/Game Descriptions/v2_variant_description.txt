V2 [Harbor inspection]

A hidden report first determines whether the ship captain is ReliableCaptain or ShakyCaptain.
ReliableCaptain happens with probability 9/10, and ShakyCaptain happens with probability 1/10.

The captain then chooses between BlueFlag and RedFlag.

After seeing the flag, the harbor inspector chooses between Board and WaveThrough.

If the captain is ReliableCaptain and chooses BlueFlag, then Board gives 0 to the captain, and 0 to the inspector.
If the captain is ReliableCaptain and chooses BlueFlag, then WaveThrough gives 2 to the captain, and 1 to the inspector.

If the captain is ReliableCaptain and chooses RedFlag, then Board gives 1 to the captain, and 0 to the inspector.
If the captain is ReliableCaptain and chooses RedFlag, then WaveThrough gives 3 to the captain, and 1 to the inspector.

If the captain is ShakyCaptain and chooses BlueFlag, then Board gives 1 to the captain, and 1 to the inspector.
If the captain is ShakyCaptain and chooses BlueFlag, then WaveThrough gives 3 to the captain, and 0 to the inspector.

If the captain is ShakyCaptain and chooses RedFlag, then Board gives 0 to the captain, and 1 to the inspector.
If the captain is ShakyCaptain and chooses RedFlag, then WaveThrough gives 2 to the captain, and 0 to the inspector.
