V3 (film distribution)

A studio first chooses between distribution plan A, distribution plan B, and distribution plan C.
If the studio chooses distribution plan C, the matter ends immediately and each side gets a final return of 1.

If the studio chooses distribution plan A, a distribution partner then chooses between option X and option Y.
If the partner chooses option X after distribution plan A, the studio gets 3 and the partner gets 0.
If the partner chooses option Y after distribution plan A, the studio gets 0 and the partner gets 3.

If the studio chooses distribution plan B, the partner again chooses between option X and option Y.
At that point, the partner cannot tell whether the studio had chosen distribution plan A or distribution plan B.
If the partner chooses option X after distribution plan B, the studio gets 0 and the partner gets 3.
If the partner chooses option Y after distribution plan B, the studio gets 3 and the partner gets 0.
