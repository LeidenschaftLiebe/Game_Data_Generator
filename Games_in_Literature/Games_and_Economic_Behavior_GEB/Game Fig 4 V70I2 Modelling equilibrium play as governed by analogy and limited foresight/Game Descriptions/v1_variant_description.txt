V1 — Museum Wing

A museum director is deciding how to handle a new wing.
The director moves first and chooses between Open Wing (L) and Keep Current Layout (R).

If the director chooses Keep Current Layout (R), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the director and reward 2 to the operations manager.

If the director chooses Open Wing (L), the director moves again and chooses between Quiet Opening (L) and Public Launch (R).

If the director chooses Quiet Opening (L), the operations manager then chooses between Limited Staffing (l) and Full Reset (r).
If the path is Open Wing (L), Quiet Opening (L), Limited Staffing (l), the outcome gives reward 0 to the director and reward 1 to the manager.
If the path is Open Wing (L), Quiet Opening (L), Full Reset (r), the outcome gives reward 0 to each player.

If the director chooses Public Launch (R), the operations manager then chooses between Limited Staffing (l) and Full Reset (r).
If the path is Open Wing (L), Public Launch (R), Limited Staffing (l), the outcome gives reward 1 to the director and reward 0 to the manager.
If the path is Open Wing (L), Public Launch (R), Full Reset (r), the outcome gives reward 4 to the director and reward 1 to the manager.
