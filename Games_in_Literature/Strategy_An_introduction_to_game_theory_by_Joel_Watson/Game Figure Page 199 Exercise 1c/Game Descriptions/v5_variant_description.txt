V5 (theme park expansion)

A park director first decides whether to keep expansion plans on hold or move forward with a full development push.
If the director keeps expansion on hold, the project ends immediately and the final reward is 2 for the director, 2 for the finance chief, and 2 for the attractions designer.

If the director moves forward with the full push, the finance chief then chooses among package A, package B, and package C.
If the finance chief chooses package C, the project ends immediately with a final reward of 4 for the director, 3 for the finance chief, and 1 for the attractions designer.

If the finance chief chooses package A, the attractions designer then chooses between concept X and concept Y.
Concept X after package A gives a final reward of 3 for the director, 2 for the finance chief, and 1 for the attractions designer.
Concept Y after package A gives a final reward of 5 for the director, 0 for the finance chief, and 0 for the attractions designer.

If the finance chief chooses package B, the attractions designer again chooses between concept X and concept Y.
At that point, the attractions designer cannot tell whether the project reached this stage after package A or package B.
Concept X after package B gives a final reward of 1 for the director, 2 for the finance chief, and 6 for the attractions designer.
Concept Y after package B gives a final reward of 7 for the director, 5 for the finance chief, and 5 for the attractions designer.
