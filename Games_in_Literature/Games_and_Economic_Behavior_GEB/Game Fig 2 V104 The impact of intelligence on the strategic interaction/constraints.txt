Strategic Definition:
Better-Response Equivalence

Constraints:
1) Check the move order: first a chance node selects Player 1's type; then Player 1 moves; if Player 1 does not choose the opening action, Player 2 moves.
2) Check that Player 2's four decision nodes are in the same information set.
3) Check the chance probabilities at the root: 3/5 for the low-cost type and 2/5 for the high-cost type.
4) Check the anchor payoff: the outcome after B -> A is (0.0, 0.8).
