Player 1 moves first and chooses between action U and action D.
If Player 1 chooses action D, the game ends immediately with a final reward of 2 for Player 1 and 6 for Player 2.

If Player 1 chooses action U, then Player 1 moves again and chooses between action A and action B.
After that, Player 2 chooses between action X and action Y.
When Player 2 makes that choice, Player 2 cannot tell whether Player 1 previously chose action A or action B.

If Player 1 chose action A and Player 2 chooses action X, the final reward is 3 for Player 1 and 4 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chose action A and Player 2 chooses action Y, the final reward is 1 for Player 1 and 4 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chose action B and Player 2 chooses action X, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 1 for Player 2.
If Player 1 chose action B and Player 2 chooses action Y, the final reward is 2 for Player 1 and 0 for Player 2.
