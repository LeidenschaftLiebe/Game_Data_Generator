V4 [Political alliance]

A party leader is deciding how much influence to hand to a possible coalition partner.

The party leader moves first and chooses one of four levels:
StayIndependent (N), LimitedDeal (L), BroadDeal (M), or FullAlliance (H).

If the party leader chooses StayIndependent (N), this gives the party leader 24, and the partner 15.

If the party leader chooses LimitedDeal (L), the partner chooses between HonorDeal (R) and ExploitDeal (D).
If the partner chooses HonorDeal (R), this gives the party leader 32, and the partner 22.
If the partner chooses ExploitDeal (D), this gives the party leader 20, and the partner 42.

If the party leader chooses BroadDeal (M), the partner again chooses between HonorDeal (R) and ExploitDeal (D).
If the partner chooses HonorDeal (R), this gives the party leader 44, and the partner 36.
If the partner chooses ExploitDeal (D), this gives the party leader 12, and the partner 72.

If the party leader chooses FullAlliance (H), the partner again chooses between HonorDeal (R) and ExploitDeal (D).
If the partner chooses HonorDeal (R), this gives the party leader 56, and the partner 42.
If the partner chooses ExploitDeal (D), this gives the party leader 0, and the partner 80.
