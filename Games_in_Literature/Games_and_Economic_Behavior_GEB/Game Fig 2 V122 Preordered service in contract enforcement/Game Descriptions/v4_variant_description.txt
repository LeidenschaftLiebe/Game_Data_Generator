V4 [Historic artifact restoration]

A museum patron first chooses between DoNotFund and FundRestoration.
If the patron chooses DoNotFund, this gives 1 to the museum patron, and 1 to the restorer.

If the patron chooses FundRestoration, a random complication may occur.
With probability 1/4, there is UnexpectedDamage.
With probability 3/4, there is NoDamage.

If there is NoDamage, the restorer chooses between CompleteHonestly and PocketTheBudget.
If the restorer chooses CompleteHonestly, this gives 2 to the museum patron, and 2 to the restorer.

If the restorer chooses PocketTheBudget, the legal review begins.
The court EnforcesContract with probability 1/2, or DoesNotEnforceContract with probability 1/2.
EnforcesContract gives 2 to the museum patron, and 1.8 to the restorer.
DoesNotEnforceContract gives 0 to the museum patron, and 4 to the restorer.

If there is UnexpectedDamage, the legal review begins immediately and leads to those same two possible results.
