V4 — Guided Robot or Memory-Loss Robot

At START, the robot chooses between GuidedMode and MemoryLossMode.

Under GuidedMode, the robot reaches NodeX [X] first.
At NodeX [X], the robot shuts down with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or continues with probability 4/7.

If the robot continues, it reaches NodeY [Y].
At NodeY [Y], the robot shuts down with probability 3/7 and gets 0, or continues with probability 4/7 and gets 1.

Under MemoryLossMode, the robot reaches NodeX [X] first.
At NodeX [X], the robot shuts down with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or continues with probability 1/2.

If the robot continues, it reaches NodeY [Y].
At NodeY [Y], the robot shuts down with probability 1/2 and gets 0, or continues with probability 1/2 and gets 1.

Under MemoryLossMode, the robot cannot tell whether the current node is NodeX [X] or NodeY [Y].
