V3 [Volcano evacuation]

A mayor first chooses between MountainPlan (A), ValleyPlan (B), and StopEvacuation (E).

If the mayor chooses StopEvacuation (E), the process ends there.
This gives 3 to the mayor, and 0 to the field coordinator.

If the mayor chooses ValleyPlan (B), the field coordinator chooses between RouteAlpha (a) and RouteBeta (b).
RouteAlpha (a) gives 0 to the mayor, and 0 to the field coordinator.
RouteBeta (b) gives 1 to the mayor, and 1 to the field coordinator.

If the mayor chooses MountainPlan (A), the mayor then chooses between NorthSlope (C) and SouthSlope (D).

After NorthSlope (C), the field coordinator chooses between RouteAlpha (a) and RouteBeta (b).
RouteAlpha (a) gives 4 to the mayor, and 0 to the field coordinator.
RouteBeta (b) gives 2 to the mayor, and 0 to the field coordinator.

After SouthSlope (D), the field coordinator chooses between RouteAlpha (a) and RouteBeta (b).
RouteAlpha (a) gives 1 to the mayor, and 1 to the field coordinator.
RouteBeta (b) gives 0 to the mayor, and 0 to the field coordinator.

When choosing between RouteAlpha (a) and RouteBeta (b), the field coordinator cannot tell which earlier situation led there.
