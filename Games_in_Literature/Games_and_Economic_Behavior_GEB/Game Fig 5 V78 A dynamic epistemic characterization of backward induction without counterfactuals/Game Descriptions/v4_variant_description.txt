V4 — Vaccine Rollout

A health director is deciding how to organize a vaccination campaign.
The director moves first and chooses between Targeted Rollout (a1) and Pause Expansion (b1).

If the director chooses Pause Expansion (b1), the process ends immediately with reward 2 to the director,
reward 2 to the clinic manager, and reward 0 to the medical analyst.

If the director chooses Targeted Rollout (a1), the clinic manager moves next and chooses between
Neighborhood Clinics (a2) and Mobile Units (b2).

If the clinic manager chooses Neighborhood Clinics (a2), the medical analyst then chooses between
Start This Week (a3) and Delay Start (b3).
If the path is a1, a2, a3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the director, reward 1 to the clinic manager,
and reward 0 to the analyst.
If the path is a1, a2, b3, the outcome gives reward 3 to the director, reward 3 to the clinic manager,
and reward 1 to the analyst.

If the clinic manager chooses Mobile Units (b2), the medical analyst then chooses between
Partial Coverage (c3) and Full Coverage (d3).
If the path is a1, b2, c3, the outcome gives reward 1 to the director, reward 2 to the clinic manager,
and reward 1 to the analyst.
If the path is a1, b2, d3, the outcome gives reward 4 to the director, reward 0 to the clinic manager,
and reward 0 to the analyst.
